Poppy Delevingne Collaborates with Solid &amp; Striped

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Poppy Delevingne is back with her second collaboration for swimwear brand and Instagram sensation Solid & Striped. While her first saw playful splashes of pink and flamingo prints, her latest design stint is more subdued—thanks to a tranquil vacation in the Ionian Sea. "Last summer I spent some time in Corfu, Greece, and I was really inspired by the colors there, and how simple, peaceful and refined it was. It really hasn't been touched since the 1970s." A blue and white maillot with cut out detail at the stomach is already a sell-out but there are more gingham one pieces and blush and cream bikinis where that came from. Just back from Coachella, Delevingne took some time to undertake BAZAAR.com's Style Q&A—see what she's drinking and who inspires her. 

Describe your style in 3 words:

I couldn't possibly choose 3 words. I dress to suit my mood, so every day is a surprise... 

Poolside cocktail?

Tequila on the rocks, with a dash of jalapeño.

Favorite spring break destination?

Going loco down in Acapulco.

Favorite hotel in the world?

The Beverly Hills Hotel in LA. The pink palace. 

5 must have warm weather beauty products?

Sisley SPF 30 for the face and body, Charlotte Tilbury bronze & glow, a Mac (Ruby Woo) lipstick, tuberose angelica scent by Jo Malone London travel size, Chanel inimitable mascara

First childhood fashion memory?

Wearing my first pair of stone washed dungarees and thinking I never wanted to take them off. Ever. 

Courtesy Solid & Striped

Style icons? 

Marlene Dietrich and Jane Birkin.

You're just back from Coachella, who would be your ultimate band or performer to see live?

Prince.

If you weren't blonde what color would you try?

For this Coachella I tried out Salmon pink for the first time. It was Josh Wood's idea. He's the magic man when it comes to dying your hair. I'm tempted to keep it for longer.

One piece you're buying to update your spring look?

A silk embroidered bomber jacket. Louis Vuitton have done a sensational one—I can't wait to whip it out. 

What's your current wardrobe MVP?

My Solid & Striped gingham boyfriend shirt. It looks ace tucked into vintage Re-Done jeans. 

Any fashion regrets?

Wearing things I didn't feel comfortable in. It always showed... 

Name 5 people you would invite to your ultimate pool party if you could invite anyone?

Malibu Barbie, Axl Rose, Brigitte Bardot; I think with those three I'd be pretty made up.

Courtesy Solid & Striped
