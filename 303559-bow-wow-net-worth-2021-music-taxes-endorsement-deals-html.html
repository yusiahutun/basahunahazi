Bow Wow’s financial woes

Uncle Sam never hesitates when it comes to going after celebrities and businesspersons who may have failed to be delinquent with tax, and Shad is no exception. The rapper landed in the IRS’ crosshairs with a government debt of a six-figure amount. Precisely, he owed the IRS unpaid taxes amounting to $91,105.61. The amount was dated back to 2006, and this prompted the government to file a tax lien in the state of Florida where the rapper owned property.

However, the rapper disputed the report about his financial struggles on his blog and urged his fans not to believe everything they read.
