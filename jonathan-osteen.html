Early Life and Education

Jonathan Osteen was born on April 20, 1995, in Houston, Texas, United States. His age is currently 28 years old. He was born to his parents Joel Osteen (father) and Victoria Osteen (mother).

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Jonathan is a singer and musician.
