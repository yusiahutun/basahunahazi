Cornhole Christmas Drinking Game

Really, you could play this any time of the year, but this festive Country Living Christmas Stars Cornhole Board Set has us in the mood for a holiday cornhole tournament. It's pretty simple: When one team makes a bag in the hole, the other team drinks.

Christmas Alphabet Drinking Game

mixetto//Getty Images

This one could also work in a virtual format! First, establish an order. As the group goes through the alphabet. each person says a Christmas related name, item, song, and so on, for the letter they have. If you hesitate or draw a blank, you have to drink!

Ugly Christmas Sweater Drinking Game

We're not sure there's a more appropriate ugly Christmas sweater than one that doubles as a drinking game. The instructions are on the back: Hit red, share a drink. Hit white, give a drink. Hit green, I drink. Bullseye, everyone drinks!

Christmas Song Drinking Game

vladans//Getty Images

Chances are, you've already been singing Christmas songs since October, so why not make a game out of it? Each person takes turns singing a line, and if someone messes up, they drink.

Get the tutorial at Home Wet Bar.

Zoreni Inflatable Reindeer Antler Ring Toss

Take turns throwing rings at these inflatable antlers. If you land one, the "reindeer" has to drink.

Christmas Dinner Drinking Game

franckreporter//Getty Images

This game can be played right at the dinner table with no supplies, and you can make up your own rules to keep things interesting. For example, as conversation flows, everyone must drink if someone asks them what they do for a living.

Christmas Card Drinking Game

Comstock Images//Getty Images

Have your friends bring over their holiday cards—they'll need them for this game. After you divvy up the pile of Christmas cards among each guest, everyone reads them out loud and you drink when you hear a certain word or phrase.
