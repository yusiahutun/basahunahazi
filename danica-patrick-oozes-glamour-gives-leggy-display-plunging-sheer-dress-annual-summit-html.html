Danica Patrick oozes glamour as she gives leggy display in plunging sheer dress for annual summit

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, April 13, 2024

She's retired from her NASCAR racing days.

And Danica Patrick was moving on to different work on Tuesday as she gave an impassioned speech at the Fortune Most Powerful Women Summit in Laguna Niguel, California while she was joined by journalist Megyn Kelly.

The 36-year-old brunette sportswoman meant business as she spoke at the event while keeping her look straightforward and businesslike while Megyn, 47, opted for a similar black dress for the chat.
