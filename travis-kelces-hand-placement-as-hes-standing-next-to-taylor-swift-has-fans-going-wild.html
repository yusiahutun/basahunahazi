Travis Kelce's hand placement as he's standing next to Taylor Swift has fans going wild

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, April 22, 2024

Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift attended Patrick Mahomes’ 15 & Mahomies charity gala this weekend, and the PDA was PDAing, to say the least. Of course, Swifties examined every picture and video we were graced with on the night, and they couldn’t help but notice Kelce’s hand placement on Swift’s ‘behind’.

The celebrity couple have been public since September, when Taylor Swift was spotted in the stands of the Arrowhead stadium, cheering on Travis Kelce and the rest of the Kansas City Chiefs. Ever since, they haven’t shied away from the PDA, from kissing in front of the world when the Chiefs won the Super Bowl, to dancing together at Coachella. We’ve seen it all, now…

Travis Kelce’s ‘cheeky hand placement’ has Taylor Swift fans going wild

The two were suited and booted at the event, as they arrived hand in hand. Trav looked dapper in a smart suit, while Tay stunned in an emerald green floor-length dress.

Many pictures from the night have been circulating X, formerly known as Twitter during the last few days, and there’s one in particular that fans can’t get enough of.

In one photo, the adorable couple can be seen standing side by side, as they chat to someone facing opposite. Trav has his arm around Tay, and his hand, on her behind. The music star could be seen smiling next to him, lifting one hand to her face.

The two stayed close to each other throughout the night, showcasing their love. One source revealed that the two couldn’t stop kissing and there was “a lot of overall happiness between them.”

“TRAVIS HAND PLACEMENT YALL I REPEAT TRAVIS HAND PLACEMENTTT,” wrote one fan upon seeing the pics.

Another penned: “Travis’ hand placement is always [sweating emoji]”

It’s not the first time fans have noticed his ‘cheeky’ hand placement. In fact, fans noticed it as far back as one of the first pap pics we received from them, as they made their way over to the car.

Young love!

Kelce referred to Swift as his ‘significant other’

The NFL star’s hand placement wasn’t the only thing Swifties were fangirling over. He referred to her as his “significant other” on stage and fans couldn’t cope.

As a surprise, one of the auction items was four tickets to the Cruel Summer singer’s Eras concert, which ended up going for a whopping $80,000!

“I was just talking to my significant other and uh, we might have one other auction item that wasn’t on the docket, has anyone heard of the Eras Tour?” the NFL star announced on stage, to the crowd going wild.

Social media were also going wild, as one penned: “Not only has Tay never had a bf use this term for her publicly, I don’t think Trav has previously used this term to describe a gf. They are serious serious!!!”
