Liam Hemsworth gives the thumbs up while sporting an injured arm

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Liam Hemsworth looked like he'd had a recent mishap judging by the injured arm covered in bandages.

But all was just fine as The Hunger Games star flashed a smile and gave the thumbs up to onlookers while out in Santa Monica, California on Friday.

Liam's arm was wrapped in a brace and supportive bandages straight up to the elbow, which he held gingerly while running errands with his male assistant.

Doing just fine: Liam Hemsworth gave the thumbs up to feeling better while stepping out in Santa Monica, California on Friday following an injury to his right arm 

The 25-year-old actor kept a low-profile in scruffy beard, black knit cap and dark sunglasses.

A blue T-shirt and low-hanging, belted jeans and grey trainers made up the rest of Liam's casual ensemble.

Later in the day, Liam was spotted strolling across a street with a fresh bandage wrapped around his wounded arm.

Spring to his step: The actor recently completed filming The Dressmaker with Kate Winslet in his native Australia and has some time to relax now

That looks painful: The 25-year-old actor sported a brace and bandages to his elbow as he ran errands earlier in the day with his assistant

Follow me: Liam was trailed by his helpful staffer who even held his iced beverage for him

Fast track: Liam's T-shirt was perhaps a nod to Deus Ex Machina USA motorcycles in Venice, California

Liam smiled broadly through his beard and gave spectators another positive thumbs up.

The 6 ft 3 Australian wasn't sporting any sign of injury on January 17 as he visited a Starbucks in Malibu, California.

Liam emerged from the coffeehouse wearing a grey sweat shirt, blue shorts, baseball cap and beige loafers - and a large iced green tea to assuage his thirst.

Unshaven look: The hunky actor showed off a scruffy beard

Under cover: The Hunger Games star wore a concealing knit cap and cool shades 

The actor will soon be seen in the comedy-drama The Dressmaker opposite Winslet and Sarah Snook.

The story revolves around a fancy woman who returns to her small, rural town in Australia to start a dressmaking shop - and get revenge on those who did her wrong.

Also on Liam's plate is The Hunger Games: Mockingjay - Part 2, which has a US release date of November 15, opposite Jennifer Lawrence, Julianne Moore and Josh Hutcherson.
