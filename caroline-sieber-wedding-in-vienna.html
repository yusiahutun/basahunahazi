Stylist to the stars Caroline Sieber weds in fairytale Vienna wedding

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

    Fashion fans were given a glimpse inside the fabulous, star-studded wedding of well-known stylist Caroline Sieber thanks to journalist Derek Blasberg, who shared intimate photos of the nuptials on his Instagram page.Caroline, an Austrian-born, London-based fashion stylist who is well-known for working with actress Emma Watson, wed her beau Fritz von Westenholz in a stunning ceremony in Vienna.

    Caroline posted this snap of her receiving her custom-made Chanel wedding dress

    A host of fashion heavyweights including Erdem, Christopher Kane, Lauren Santo Domingo, Hamish Bowles, Emma Watson and Tallulah Harlech turned out to witness the fashionista say her vows.

    Derek shared a picture of Erdem, Christopher Kane and himself posing in lederhosen, asking, "Who wore it best? Erdem, Christopher Kane, or me?"

    The beautiful bride kisses her groom, Fritz von Westenholz surrounded by their page boys flower girls

    He also shared a touching picture of the blushing bride in her wedding dress. "Caroline von Westenholz: The best thing out of Austria since Mozart!" he captioned the snap.Another image showed a trio of beautiful blondes – stylist Sophia Hesketh, model Jacquetta Wheeler and contributing Vogue editor Lauren Santo Domingo – posing in matching scarlet dresses. "Sophia, Jacquetta and Lauren: My favorite presents come in red wrapping," he wrote.Pippa Middleton was another high-profile name to attend the wedding. While no snaps were published of Kate Middleton's younger sister, she is said to have worn a dress and jacket by Matthew Williamson teamed with a fascinator and nude heels.Caroline herself also took to Instagram to update her fans.

    Derek posted this picture of himself and actress friend Emma Watson enjoying the lavish wedding in Vienna

    The flower girls' dresses were also custom-made, created by Claire Waight Keller of French brand Chloé.Journalist Gianluca Longo also gave an insight into the wedding, sharing pictures of Emma Watson, who donned traditional Austrian clothing for the post-nuptial festivities.

    Three days before the wedding, she shared a picture of a giant Chanel box containing her wedding dress. As a Chanel ambassador, it's no surprise that she wore a one-off piece designed by creative director Karl Lagerfeld.
