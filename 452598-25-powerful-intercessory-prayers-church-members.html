25 powerful intercessory prayers for the church members

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 29, 2024

Intercessory prayer is praying to God or a saint in heaven on behalf of oneself or others. When having the prayer, you put others before yourself. You remember the needs of others before you talk to God about your problems. As you kneel to pray every day, remember to have intercessory prayers for the church members.

How do you lead intercessory prayer in church? There is no formula when it comes to prayer. God hears all prayers and responds in his own time. Just think about what you need to pray for and pray in your heart because no one will judge you. Then, you can have a prayer for the church with Bible verses as a guide.

Intercessory prayers for leaders of all kinds

Leaders are God's servants on earth. They guide you in everything you do. They need God's grace in their leading capacities. Presidents, kings, prime ministers, bishops, union leaders, university presidents, and even children leaders need prayers as they go about their duties. The following are examples of intercession prayers.

Intercessory prayers for political leaders

I am asking you, God, that you may show our president your favour. Guide him as he leads the nation. I am praying for presidents worldwide, ministers, members of parliament, governors and senators. These are the leaders that you have placed on earth to lead us. Please give them strength, wisdom and knowledge as they work.
