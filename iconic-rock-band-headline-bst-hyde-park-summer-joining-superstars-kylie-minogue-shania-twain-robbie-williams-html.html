Iconic rock band will headline BST Hyde Park this summer joining superstars Kylie Minogue, Shania Tw

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

BST Hyde Park have announced that huge rock back Kings Of Leon are set to headline the concert this summer in London. 

The award-winning band, who have announced a new album and tour, are set to take to the stage on June 30.

They will also bring out special guests Paolo Nutini and The Vaccines during their iconic summer concert sponsored by American Express.

The band gushed they are excited to return to London to perform, they said: 'We can’t wait to get back to London and play at BST this summer. It is always a blast to play in the UK and we are ready to have some fun'. 

The band will also join the likes of Kylie Minogue, who is set to headline on July 13, and Shania Twain, on July 7.

Back in December, Robbie Williams sent fans wild after he announced that he will be headlining the BST Hyde Park Festival on July 6.

The Angel singer was the second headline act to be unveiled after Italian vocal sensation Andrea Bocelli, who will take to the stage the day before, on July 5.

The news comes after Kings Of Leon declared their triumphant return on Thursday as they revealed they have signed a deal with Capitol Records.

It was quite the rollout as they also revealed that their ninth full-length studio album will be titled Can We Please Have fun and will be released on May 10. 

If that already wasn't enough excitement for the Sex On Fire hitmakers also announced a North American tour. 

The band -which formed in 1999 - is composed of brothers Caleb, Nathan and Jared Followill and their cousin Matthew Followill. 

To amp up the excitement for the forthcoming album, the foursome released a new single - and accompanying music video - titled Mustang on Thursday.

Can We Please Have Fun was produced by Kid Harpoon who has previously collaborated with Harry Styles and Florence The Machine.

The album will feature 12 tracks with song titles including Ballerina Radio, Rainbow Ball, Don't Stop The Bleeding, and Hesitation Generation.

Meanwhile Kings Of Leon's tour will hit 26 cities across US and Canada beginning on August 14.

Before that they will headline a concert at BST Hyde Park in London on June 30.

The band will also be announcing additional dates internationally within the next few weeks. 

Frontman Caleb says the new album 'was the most enjoyable record I’ve ever been a part of.'

His brother and drummer Nathan also commented: 'It’s like we allowed ourselves to be musically vulnerable. I love it when a rock band is not embarrassed to admit that every song doesn’t have to be on 11.' 

Kings Of Leon's entire discography beginning with 2003 debut EP Holy Roller Novocaine was released on RCA Records.

They had released eight studio albums, one live album,  two compilation albums, two video album, five extended plays, 26 singles, one promotional single and 26 music videos with the record label.

Their final release with RCA was 2021 album When You See Yourself.
