Keith Marler's biography

The Emmy Award-winning meteorologist was born and raised in the United States. He likes to keep his personal life private. As a result, he has not shared details about his family background, parents' names, and date and place of birth.

Educational background

The American weatherman attended the University of Maryland. He studied and graduated with a bachelor's degree in physics and math. Currently, he is pursuing a Master of Science in Atmospheric Sciences and Meteorology. According to his Linkedin account, he is yet to graduate with a master degree.

Profession

He began his career in April 1998 as a morning meteorologist at KBJR-News6 in Duluth. He held the position until December 2000. Then in January 2001, he joined WUSA TV as the weekend and noon meteorologist, where he worked for two years.

He joined the KMSP-TV in October 2003 and has worked there since then. He anchors weather news every weekday, starting 4:30 am to 9 am on the FOX 9 Morning News. He also hosts the FOX 9 Buzz from 9 a.m. to 10 a.m. and manages other weather duties at the TV station.

Keith Marler's wife and children

Marler is a married man and a proud father of two children. His lovely wife, Susan, is a physics and mathematics teacher. He revealed in a Facebook post that they went out for their first date on February 19th, 1993. They were both students at the University of Maryland.

Susan and her husband have two lovely children. Grace, their daughter and firstborn, was born in 2004, while their son, Jack, was born in 2007. They had another son who was born prematurely and died of Trisomy - a genetic disorder.
