Is Cheri Oteri Married With Husband? Personal Life Along With Tragic Loss Of Father

If you are a ‘Saturday Night Live’ fan, you might know American comic actress, Cheri Oteri who plays a role of Arianna. Cheri, 55, has successfully veiled her relationship status form the eye of media.

Following her popularity, Cheri's fans are intrigued to know about her relationship status. So, let 's dig out her love life along with her personal details.

Is Cheri Oteri Married With Husband?

Hollywood actress Cheri Oteri has made her fan wonder about her dating affair. However, back on valentine day of 2018, she shared the tweet in which she said, "Extra Love Today" which hints towards her faith in love.

As Cheri remains mum about her relationship status, it has been difficult to know about her love life. Moreover, she has been so cautious that neither news of her romantic association has surfaced the media nor has been spotted publically.

It's possible that she is single and enjoying her singlehood status. But it's also possible that she is enjoying her dating life with a boyfriend while hesitating to flaunt her partner publicly. Whichever the case be her fans are hopeful that she would come forward to shed light on her love life in days to come.

Moreover, she is yet get married to experience a conjugal life with a husband. Since there is no hint of marriage in the media, her wedding appears to be a far-fetched dream. But who knows she might surprise her fans by getting married too! 

More About Cheri Oteri

Cheri Oteri was born on 19th September 1962, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.  She grew up in Upper Darby, Pennsylvania and went to Archbishop Prendergast High School. Post graduation she moved to Los Angeles at the age of 25 and worked in Promotions for A&M records for four years.

Cheri had to go through a tough time in her life as her father, Gaetano Thomas Oteri was stabbed to death in April 2008 in Nashville, Tenn. On the charge of murder of 69 years old Gaetano, authorities arrested country songwriter Richard Fagan.

Gaetano was Richard's longtime music associate and publisher too. As per affidavits, a fight had occurred between Gaetano and Richard following which Richard used a Colt pocket knife to cut Oteri across his body before leaving the scene.

Moreover, Cheri seems to share a close bond with her family and even flaunts her family via social media. She took pride on her nephew Nick Oteri's achievement on being selected to work with Jimmy Kimmel via Twitter.

Talking about Cheri's professional life, she worked for a record company for almost five years; she began performing for the show The Groundlings. she entangled in the show The Groundlings from 1995 to 2000.

Besides that, she appeared in such movies as Scary Movie, Inspector Gadget, Liar Liar, and Shrek the Third. She has also made guest appearances on television shows such as, Curb Your Enthusiasm, Just Shoot Me, and Strangers with Candy. With her hard work, dedication, and passion towards her craft, she has successfully accumulated a net worth of $2 million.
