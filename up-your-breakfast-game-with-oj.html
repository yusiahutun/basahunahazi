Up Your Breakfast Game with OJ

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 10, 2024

It’s a fact: Eating breakfast on the regular can provide a significant health boost. Compared with skippers, breakfast eaters give themselves the chance to ingest more nutrients and boost their cognitive performance, among other benefits. And what quintessential breakfast beverage just happens to be a convenient source of nutrients? Orange juice, of course.

To get the most out of your breakfast, here are our top five nutrition-forward (and tasty!) tips featuring Trop50 with Calcium + Vitamin D.

Reinforce Your Skeletal System

CentralITAlliance//Getty Images

No bones about it, you may not be getting enough calcium and vitamin D. According to National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES) data, the majority of adult Americans are not meeting the Estimated Average Requirement (EAR) for either calcium or vitamin D. And that’s a problem, because calcium is vital for strong, healthy bones and teeth, and vitamin D helps your body absorb calcium. Top sources of the former include dairy products, leafy greens, and calcium-fortified foods like cereals and orange juice. One key way to get vitamin D is by heading outdoors (after applying sunscreen, of course); your body produces its own “sunshine vitamin” when your skin is exposed to the sun. You can also get both bone-friendly nutrients at once by drinking Trop50 with Calcium + Vitamin D.

Try this: Turn Trop50 into ice cubes to whirl into your morning smoothie for an extra boost of calcium and vitamin D.

Refuel Right

Sure, you can eat just about anything to get your metabolism revved up first thing in the morning, but it’s vital to ensure you’re not short-changing yourself of the benefits associated with real-food nutrition over a highly processed diet. A vitamin-packed glass of Trop50 with Calcium + Vitamin D as one of your go-to breakfast drinks is a perfect choice, since it’s made from real oranges.

Power Up Your Immune System

Westend61//Getty Images

Adequate sleep, a healthful lifestyle, and a nutrient-rich eating plan all play significant roles in maintaining a healthy immune system. Two especially noteworthy nutrients in this regard are vitamins C and E, both powerful antioxidants that promote healthy cells. Citrus fruits, bell peppers, berries, and tomatoes are full of vitamin C, and almonds, sunflower seeds, and peanut butter are notable sources of vitamin E. And one serving of Trop50 provides 15 percent of the Daily Value (DV) for vitamin E and nearly a day’s supply of vitamin C (80 percent of the DV).

Try this: Alongside your breakfast main, serve a refreshing, produce-packed sunrise salad sprinkled with almonds and an orange-juice vinaigrette. Whisk together 2 tablespoons each Trop50 and extra-virgin olive oil, 1 tablespoon cider vinegar, a pinch of salt and pepper, and an optional drizzling of honey or coconut nectar.

Choose Sweetness with Goodness

Deanna Kelly//Getty Images

Sugar isn’t inherently evil, and you don’t have to totally eliminate it from your diet (after all, birthday cake wouldn’t be birthday cake without it), but ultra-sugar cereals and daily cinnamon rolls aren’t the recommended way to satisfy your sweet tooth. If you regularly indulge in artificial sugars in place of nutrient-rich foods with natural ones, you’re doing your health a disservice. Anytime you have the opportunity to swap out refined sugars for a nourishing fruit or fruit juice—like Trop 50, which has no artificial sugars—go for it.

Keep Your Blood Pressure in Check

Ridofranz//Getty Images

Consider incorporating relaxation techniques, yoga, or meditation into your lifestyle to help maintain healthy blood pressure. Eat good sources of potassium, which supports healthy blood pressure and is found in foods such as dried fruits, lentils, kidney beans, bananas, winter squash, and potatoes. Build your meals mainly around wholesome food sources rather than salty, ultra-processed packaged foods full of excess sodium that can raise your blood pressure.

Try this: Make mashed butternut squash a comforting standby, using Trop50 as the mashing liquid. If you prepare it in the evening, reheat it in the morning for a quick, nutritious breakfast side.
