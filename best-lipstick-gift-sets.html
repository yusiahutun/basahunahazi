Dior Addict Shine Lipstick Case and Refill

Best Refillable

Dior Addict Shine Lipstick Case and Refill

If they don't already own a Lady Dior Bag or Book Tote, we're betting they'll be over the moon about receiving this two-piece fashion case and refillable lipstick tube. Once your friend or family member has emptied their gifted tube, they can choose a new shade to add to their evergreen case.

Pat McGrath Labs Mini Divine Rose Lip Trio

Best with Gloss

Finish: Matte

Most Viral

Merit The Box Set

Most Viral

Merit The Box Set

Best Long-Lasting

Chanel Rogue Velvet Allure Luminous Matte Lipstick Duo

Best Long-Lasting

Chanel Rogue Velvet Allure Luminous Matte Lipstick Duo

Credit: Harrods

This luxe lipstick duo has a lightweight formula that stays put through eating and drinking. And while each of these tubes contains a matte formula, your giftee won't have to worry about dry lips, since the lipsticks contain a blend of shea butter and jojoba oil for ultimate hydration.

Number of included lipsticks: Two

Finish: Matte

Best for Hermès Fans

Hermès Rouge Hermès Gift Set

Best for Hermès Fans

Hermès Rouge Hermès Gift Set

Credit: Selfridges

Known for its rich texture and vibrant color payoff, a single tube of Rouge Hermès satin lipstick alone makes a luxe gift for your favorite beauty enthusiast. This three-piece set from the brand takes their lipstick look one step further with the inclusion of the nourishing Lip Care Balm and beloved Rosy Lip Enhancer. And, when it comes to gift wrapping, few things beat unboxing something from their iconic orange box.

Number of included lipsticks: Three

Finish: Satin; Matte

Lipstick shades from Tom Ford hold a permanent spot in our lipstick collections, and this bite-size set gives your giftee an easy rotation to wear on the regular. From a deep violet hue to a poppy cherry red option, consider this the gift that keeps on giving.

Best for Hydration

Beauty Lip Trio Rouge Louboutin Coffret

Best for Red Lipstick Devotees
