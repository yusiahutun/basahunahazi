Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

There are few things fashion editors love more than a sitewide sale at one of our favorite brands. So it almost goes without saying that this week, BAZAAR staffers are paying close attention to the 30% off sitewide Alo Yoga sale happening now until May 5.

It's not just fashion insiders who love Alo Yoga. Everyone from Rita Ora to Hailey Bieber has been spotted leaving the gym in the brand's buttery soft leggings, bike shorts, and workout sets. Their cozy sweatpants, hoodies and sweatsuits are a frequent travel uniform for Lady Gaga, Jennifer Lopez, and Katie Holmes alike. In addition to athleisure, Alo's corset sports bra and workout onesie have gone viral after sightings on Camila Cabello and Addison Rae, respectively.

The five-day Alo Yoga sale will be over before you know it, so we did some street-style stalking for you and compiled all the celebrity-loved items currently in stock. Keep scrolling to find your next favorite pair of workout leggings—and perhaps a coordinating workout top, gym bag, or a timely gift for mom, too.
