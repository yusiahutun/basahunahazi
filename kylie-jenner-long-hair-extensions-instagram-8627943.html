See Kylie Jenner's Long Hair Extensions

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 10, 2024

It's never a dull moment when it comes to Kylie Jenner's hair. One minute she's sporting a short brunette pixie cut à la her mom Kris Jenner, and, the next, she'll be photographed with butt-skimming blonde tresses. And after months of rocking nondescript shoulder-length locks, the beauty mogul decided it was officially time to switch things up again. 

On Friday, Jenner appeared to have brought back her signature long and layered hair with the help of a few lengthy extensions. In a short video posted to her Instagram Story, she showed off her transformation while seated on what appeared to be her private plane, Kylie Air, wearing a black tank-top and no makeup. Tilting her head from side to side and blowing a kiss at the camera, Jenner ran her fingers through her much-longer locks, which were styled in loose curls with a middle part. 

“Missed this long hairrr,” she captioned the clip.

Jenner's latest hair transformation comes days after she dropped a step-by-step tutorial for achieving her soft glam look (using all Kylie Cosmetics products, of course) on TikTok. Getting ready for the day in a video posted to the social platform, she began her makeup routine by applying concealer under her eyes before blending a warm pink Glow Balm to the apples of her cheeks. She then contours, adds a bit of powder to her nose, and dusts her entire face with a setting solution.

For her eyes, she swiped each of her lids with a subtly-shaded eyeshadow, while her lips were lined and then smothered in a tinted balm. Et voila, she's ready.
