10 Best Eyeshadows for Brown Eyes of 2024

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Your brown eyes are gorgeous on their own, but you can truly make them sing when you play with certain eyeshadow shades. “Specific hues can enhance the allure of your eyes by working in harmony with your natural eye color,” says Saffron Hughes, a makeup artist based in Wales.

“I find that bronzy, warm copper tones (especially if they're a bit metallic) really bring out the lighter specs in the eyes,” says celebrity makeup artist Neil Scibelli. “Plums and deep olive eyeshadow tones are also my go-to's for brown eyes because they make brown eyes look richer and bolder.”

To get the shadows that will bring out the best in your brown eyes, peruse the 10 pro-picks below.

What to Keep in Mind

Formula + Finish

The shadows in this story are all powder, which come in a range of finishes, including matte, cream-to-matte, and shimmer. Deciding which to use comes down to personal preference. If you want drama and light reflection, reach for shimmer options. If you want something a bit softer or moodier, go matte. And for maximum interest, you can mix the two.

Finding Your Perfect Fit

We know the above recommendations look gorgeous on brown eyes, but that might not mean they’re all the best for you. Keep in mind what you like and what you don’t, what shades you gravitate toward, and what finishes you love. From there, you can shop the above with a discerning eye.

Your Questions, Answered

What color eyeshadow makes brown eyes pop?

“Among the most flattering options are shades like blue, green, and purple. What makes these shades particularly appealing is their remarkable ability to create a mesmerizing contrast with the warm undertones commonly found in brown eyes,” says Hughes. “The secret behind this captivating contrast lies in the color wheel. These shades stand in stark opposition to the warm undertones, resulting in a striking effect that causes your brown eyes to truly stand out. It's like a visual magic makeup trick that instantly draws attention to the allure of your eyes.”

Why should you still wear any colors you like?

“Because makeup, and eye makeup specifically, is a reflection of one's personal style and mood — which can naturally change day to day,” says Scibelli. “There's always room to have fun with your eye makeup, whether it's your statement feature for the day, or just a soft backdrop to your style.”
