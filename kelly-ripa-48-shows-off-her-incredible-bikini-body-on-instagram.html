Kelly Ripa, 48, Shows Off Her Bikini Body on Instagram Have a Look!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, March 31, 2024

It’s very clear that Kelly Ripa will not let her age deter her from showing off her bikini body, and that’s exactly what she did recently.

On Friday, March 29, the 48-year-old took to Instagram to share a collection of swimsuit photos, with quite the funny caption. “Warning: this ad contains graphic images of a woman over forty in swimwear,”  the TV host said. Take a look at the post below!

The Live with Kelly and Ryan host also sported some Bose headphones, as she is a brand ambassador for the company. Her fans of course were loving the post, as they rushed to the comments to respond. “Love your sweet and sassy attitude! You go girl!!” one user stated, with another adding, “You keep doing you Lady! You’re an inspiration to show what knowledge, dedication and choice can mean to living your life your own way!!”

“You look lovely Kelly! It’s so pathetic that people having something to say about women who wear swimwear after 40 … what are you supposed to wear a ski suit?!” one fan said. Kelly’s own husband, Mark Consuelos, also commented, sharing three heart emojis.

It is no surprise that Mark, also 48, is commenting on Kelly’s post, especially since this a couple that has no problem showing how close they are. Most recently, the All my Children alum shared a video montage of her guy on his birthday. “Happy birthday to the finest man! You are our heart and soul and rock and roll,” Kelly captioned the video.

The pair have been married for 22 years and have three kids —  Michael, 21, Lola, 17, and Joaquin, 16. “I’m crazy about my wife,” the Riverdale actor once told Fox News. “It’s nice to be married to your best friend.” He continued, “She’s also really patient and really tolerant. I think that’s a big key to the success of our long [marriage]. We’ve been married forever!”

Mark also commented on how he and his wife deal with the negativity they sometimes encounter. “We don’t take anything seriously or to heart,” he said. “Luckily, most of the people are great and we really don’t focus on the negative stuff. There’s too much positive stuff to focus on. If we do respond, it’s always with humor. My wife’s one of the funniest people I know.”
