DStv Yanga channels prices and packages

How much is DStv Yanga now? The DSTv Yanga package is just N2,950 per month and N32,450 per year. Upon subscription, viewing is availed for one month (30 days) for monthly subscribers and 12 months for annual subscribers. However, the viewer is expected to renew their package after one and twelve months, respectively.

DStv Yanga channels packages are paid monthly or annually. If you opt to pay for either the monthly or annual packages, you will enjoy the following:

Monthly

For N2,950, monthly subscribers enjoy the following:

  • 30 days of viewership
  • Watch on three decoders and two streaming devices
  • Online on-demand and Live television content
  • Downloadable content to tablet or smartphone.

Annually

For N32,450, annual subscribers enjoy the following;

  • They pay for 11 months and get one month free of charge
  • One year of convenient entertainment
  • Online on-demand and Live television content
  • Watch on three decoders and two streaming devices
  • Downloadable content to tablet or smartphone.

DStv Yanga subscription

How do you subscribe to DSTv Yanga? There are many different methods you can use to subscribe to DSTv Yanga or any other package, including:

  • Using the DSTv USSD code, *288#
  • Directly depositing to the bank or using internet banking
  • Using DSTv internet self-service on their website
  • Using MyDStv mobile application, which can be downloaded on Google Play Store

Other DSTv packages

  • Premium package – N21,000 per month with over 160 local and international channels and 38 HD channels
  • Compact Plus – N14,250 per month with over 145 local and international channels and 30 HD channels
  • Compact – N9,000 per month with more than 130 local and international channels and 20 HD Channels
  • Confam – N,5300 per month with over 105 local and international channels and 10 HD Channels
  • Yanga – N2,050 per month with more than 85 local and international channels and 7HD channels
  • Padi – N2,150 per month with more than 45 local and international channels and 1 HD channel
