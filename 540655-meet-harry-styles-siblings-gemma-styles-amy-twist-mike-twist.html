Who is Harry Styles?

Harry Edward Styles was born on 1 February 1994 in Redditch, Worcestershire, England, to Desmond Styles (father) and Anne Twist (mother). His father is a finance executive, entrepreneur, and director. Desmond is a former sales and marketing director at Quickdox Limited and has served as the operations manager at HFC Bank.

His mother is a Philanthropist, landlady, and social media star. Harry's parents divorced when he was seven years old. The English singerwent to Hermitage Primary School and Holmes Chapel Comprehensive School. He later dropped out of high school to pursue his music career.

The British celebrity commenced his musical career in 2010 when he unsuccessfully auditioned for the seventh season of the British music competition series The X Factor. He then joined One Direction, an English-Irish pop boy band.

Harry Style launched his solo career in 2016 and signed a recording contract with Columbia Records. The musician released his debut single, Sign of the Times, in April 2017. He has since released several hit songs as a solo artist, including Adore You, Watermelon Sugar, and As It Was.
