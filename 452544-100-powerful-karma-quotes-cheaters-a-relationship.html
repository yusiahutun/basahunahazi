100+ powerful karma quotes for cheaters in a relationship

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Karma is real and plays a considerable role in romantic relationships. Good karma will let your relationship flourish and make your life harmonious and peaceful. The idea of karma is thousands of years old. The term refers to the spiritual principle of cause and effect. It is based on the notion that what you do and put out into the world comes back to you. If you do something that is out of character, like cheating, you will be paid someday. This means that you create your own future by your present actions.
