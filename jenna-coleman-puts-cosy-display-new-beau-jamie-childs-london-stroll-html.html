Jenna Coleman puts on a cosy display with her new beau Jamie Childs during London stroll

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 20, 2024

Prince Harry was once pictured with his hand on the knee of Victoria star Jenna Coleman as they chatted intimately in a marquee at a polo tournament.

But now the Blackpool-born actress, 36, has only one prince in her life. He is film director Jamie Childs, 35, and the pair stopped for a kiss during a stroll in North London. They met when he directed her in Netflix series The Sandman.

During their latest outing, the pair were seen looking very cosy as they packed on the PDA while strolling through north London.

Cosy: Jenna Coleman appeared to be as smitten as ever with her new boyfriend Jamie Childs as they stepped out in north London 

Jamie was seen planting a kiss on his girlfriend and walking with his arm wrapped around her as they seemed to be as smitten as ever.

Jenna looked effortlessly chic in wide leg trousers and a blue striped shirt, which she tied in a knot to give a flash of her taut abs.
