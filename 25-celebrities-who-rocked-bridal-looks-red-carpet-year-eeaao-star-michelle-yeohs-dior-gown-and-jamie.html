25 celebrities who rocked bridal looks on the red carpet this year: from EEAAO star Michelle Yeoh

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 29, 2024

Inside Michelle Yeoh’s eternally youthful beauty regime – at 60

1. Michelle Yeoh

In March, before taking home the Academy Award for best actress for her performance in Everything Everywhere All at Once, Michelle Yeoh took to the red carpet wearing a bright-white tiered Christian Dior gown covered in feathery fringe. The dress appears strapless at a glance but has a high neckline made of mesh.

2. Jamie Lee Curtis

Jamie Lee Curtis showed up to the Oscars’ champagne-coloured carpet in a sheer, long-sleeved Dolce & Gabbana dress.

Curtis rocked the sparkly, long-sleeved dress before winning the award for best supporting actress for her performance in Everything Everywhere All At Once.

3. Michelle Williams

Michelle Williams wore a floor-length, white Chanel dress to the red carpet before the 2023 Oscars. The gown, which had a shiny, silver bodice, was paired with a sheer cape draped over the dress.

4. Halle Berry

Halle Berry wore a white dress accented with sparkly, rose-gold roses to the Oscars. The dress, designed by Tamara Ralph, featured a small opening above the waist and a slit that exposed the actress’ left leg and impressively high gold heels.

5. Andrea Riseborough

Riseborough, who was nominated for best actress for To Leslie, was covered head-to-toe with lace at the 2023 Oscars. Her white, long-sleeved custom Alexander McQueen gown featured subtle details around the neckline.

6. Tems

Singer and songwriter Tems showed up to the Oscars red carpet wearing an asymmetrical Lever Couture dress made of tulle. The artist’s outfit went viral on social media for blocking the view of other audience members, per People.

“Oops,” she tweeted in response.

7. Rooney Mara

Mara wore a tulle wrap dress by Alexander McQueen to the 95th annual Academy Awards. The dress tied at the back, creating a train that trailed behind the actress as she walked the red carpet.

8. Mindy Kaling

Kaling showed up to the Oscars in a strappy, fitted, floor-length white gown complete with detached sleeves. The Vera Wang gown appeared to feature white boning that connects the bra top to her waist, as well as a subtle peplum ruffle.

9. Emily Blunt

Blunt kept it simple in a fitted, long-sleeved, off-the-shoulder Valentino gown. The actress paired the bright-white dress with heels and a clutch of the same colour, creating a monochrome look.

10. Ariana DeBose

At the 2023 Oscars, DeBose wore a white Atelier Versace gown with geometric, silver details and sheer, long sleeves. A slit in the left side of the dress exposed one of her silver heels.

11. Ana de Armas

Wearing a custom-made Louis Vuitton silk gown, the Cuban actress was nominated for best actress for playing Marilyn Monroe in Blonde. De Armas’ dress also featured a tiered, scalloped bottom that created a train behind her.

12. Zoe Saldaña

Saldaña’s off-white Oscars dress was covered in lace and mesh detailing. The Avatar: The Way of Water star’s gown is from Fendi Couture’s autumn 2022 collection, and contrasts its strappy, corset top with a flowy skirt.

13. Sofia Carson

In a look perfect for walking down the aisle, Carson wore a silk-chiffon Giambattista Valli ball gown that featured a modern twist with its unique off-the-shoulder top and small cut-out in the chest. Below her hips, a full skirt gave the look a dramatic, elegant feel.

14. Claire Danes

At the 2023 Golden Globes in January, the Fleishman Is in Trouble star wore a unique floor-length Giambattista Valli gown. In addition to its textured floral pattern, Danes’ dress was topped with a blush-coloured bow at the chest, and its top was edged with diamonds.

15. Chloe Flower

In a show-stopping gown that would make for a memorable bridal look, pianist Chloe Flower wore an asymmetrical dress with dramatic ruffles that extended into a long train.

16. Zuri Hall

Hall’s simple, haltered Nicole + Felicia dress featured a small slit that exposed her white pointed heels. The bedazzled straps criss-crossed at the back, providing a subtle detail that contrasted with the dress’ simplicity.

17. Michelle Williams

At the 2023 Golden Globes, Williams wore yet another elegant gown that could be perfect for a wedding day. The asymmetrical Gucci dress was off-white, with dramatic silver-edged ruffles that extended into a train.

18. Elle Fanning

Fanning wore a busy, cream-coloured Alexander McQueen gown to the Critics Choice Awards in January 2023. Full of patterned pleats and ruffles, the asymmetric, half-strapless, half off-the-shoulder dress was paired with gold heels and dangling earrings.

19. Niecy Nash

In a strapless, cream-coloured Jason Wu gown covered in sequins, Nash walked the red carpet at the 2023 Critics Choice Awards before receiving the award for best supporting actress in a limited series or movie made for television for her performance in Dahmer – Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story.

20. Phoebe Dynevor

At the 2023 Critics Choice Awards, Phoebe Dynevor of Bridgerton wore a cream-coloured Louis Vuitton gown with a low V-neck and pops of pastel colours through its pleated skirt.

21. Zoe Kazan

For the 2023 Critics Choice Awards, the She Said star wore a black-and-white gown that could inspire a bride looking to add some gothic edge to their wedding day. The Rodarte dress’ black lace trim and sleeves contrasted with the white gown, which Kazan paired with Cartier jewellery and Christian Louboutin shoes.

22. Britt Lower

The actress’ white Harithand pantsuit featured a low-cut blazer that extended into a cape. She kept the look simple, accessorising only with pearl earrings.

23. Meghann Fahy

Fahy’s fitted, off-the-shoulder Ralph Lauren dress featured a cut-out on its left side, adding flair to an otherwise simple look.

24. Jessica Frances Dukes

At the 2023 SAG Awards in February, Dukes wore a sparkly Marc Bouwer gown with a large chest cut-out and a dramatic feathery collar.

25. Hillary Scott

Hillary Scott of the band Lady A wore a white dress, which resembled a floor-length blazer, to the 2023 Grammys red carpet in February.
