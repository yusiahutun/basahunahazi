Blake Lively Posts Happy Birthday IG Photo for Ryan Reynolds

  • Blake Lively can't stop trolling her husband, Ryan Reynolds, especially on his birthday.
  • Celebrating the actor's 43rd birthday, Lively posted a photo in which she's picking Reynolds's nose.
  • Lively and Reynolds have a long history of joking about one another on social media.
