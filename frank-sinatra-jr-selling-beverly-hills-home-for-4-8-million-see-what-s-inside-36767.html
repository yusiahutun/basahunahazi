Frank Sinatra Jr. Selling Beverly Hills Home For $4.8 Million! See Whats Inside!

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, September 7, 2024

The son of late legend Frank Sinatra is selling his Beverly Hills mansion for $4.8 million, and it has quite an impressive layout!

The home was built in 2006, though Frank Sinatra Jr. didn't buy it until Dec. 2007. It features 6 bedrooms, 7.5 baths and spans 7,213 square feet.

The property boasts 2-story ceilings in the living room, a formal dining room, library and separate office. The large chef's kitchen has granite countertops and Viking appliances.

An impressive master suite features a fireplace, sitting area, two walk-in closets, and bathroom with tub, shower and double sinks.

In the backyard, a lovely pool and patio serve as the perfect setting for summertime soirees! Extra amenities include an elevator and 3-car-garage.
