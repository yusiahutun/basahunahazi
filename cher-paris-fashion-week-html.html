Cher, 77, looks youthful in a stylish Western leather jacket as she steps out in Paris after Fashion

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, June 28, 2024

Cher showed off her youthful complexion as she stepped out in Paris on Tuesday as Fashion Week came to a close. 

The singer, 77, looked stylish in an oversized Western leather jacket with tan, black and white panels. 

She teamed the statement piece with a white vest top and some distressed jeans with a raw hem.  

Cher accessorised her look with a pair of turquoise earrings and carried a black leather handbag. 

Finally, the television personality boosted her height with heeled boots and styled her dark, glossy locks into waves. 

Out and about: Cher, 77, showed off her youthful complexion as she stepped out in Paris on Tuesday after Fashion Week came to a close

The actress has been out in the French capital enjoying Fashion Week events with her on-again boyfriend Alexander Edwards. 

Cher and Alexander, 37,  appeared to have reconciled — after reportedly splitting back in May.

Contrary to rumours swirling at the time, the couple were not engaged prior to their split.

In a full-circle moment, their arrival at Paris Fashion Week this year is a sweet echo of how they first met — also at Paris Fashion Week, last year.

Following their first meeting, the Little Man singer was spotted holding hands with her much younger beau at Craigs in early November 2022.

During the first time their relationship was on, Cher poked fun at the age gap between her and Alexander when asked by a fan what she liked about him on social media.

'He's kind, smart, hilarious... & we kiss like teenagers,' the I Got You Babe hitmaker candidly replied.

Prior to dating the iconic songstress, Alexander was in a tumultuous relationship with Kanye West's ex, Amber Rose.

Back on: The actress has been out in the French capital enjoying Fashion Week events with her on-again boyfriend Alexander Edwards

The couple – who share a 3-year-old son, Slash Electric Alexander Edwards, split after the Def Jam executive admitted to cheating on her.

While some fans showed concern for the 'Goddess of Pop' when she was dating Amber's ex the first time around, Cher dismissed their fears.

'I'm in love, not blinded by it,' she blithely replied.

The couple, who have a 40-year-age gap between them, were spotted holding hands on a double dinner date with J Balvin and Valentina Ferrer a week before they went to Paris Fashion Week together as a couple.

A source told the DailyMail.com that the pair never actually lost touch, even during the duration of their reported split.
