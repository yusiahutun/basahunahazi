With eight seasons of her reality show behind her, there's little the world hasn't seen of Kim Kardashian.

And on Sunday night's episode, then pregnant Kim let it all hang out as she kept on her quest to avoid getting any stretch marks.

Keeping up with her figure: Kim Kardashian lathered her growing assets with a stretch-mark preventing cream as she travelled with her family to Greece

Getting the royal treatment: Sister Khloe helped Kim relieve her swollen feet

The 32-year-old was on holiday with her family in Santorini, and faced with the prospect of sunning herself in a bikini, made sure to give her growing figure a royal rubdown.

In order to avoid getting any stretch marks, a common side effect of growing skin during pregnancy, the girlfriend of Kanye West lathered on a product to help soothe her skin, exposing her ample chest and lifting up her vest top to display her stomach as well as lowering her cotton trousers to get all angles.

She offered it to her sister Khloe as well, who then treated her sister to a foot massage with the product.

'Don't get horny,' Khloe joked.

No spot untouched: Kim made sure to get in and around her bountiful chest

Pointing North: Kim spread some extra cream around her stomach

Full view: Kim gave herself the reacharound, lowering her trousers to get her hips

Despite being on vacation, the topic of Kim's baby was never far from anyone's mind, as Kim brought up the question of naming her child while the family lounged on their yacht.

Kim said she was not willing to name her child following her family's 'K' tradition for fear the family would be labelled the 'KKK'.

'If we name our kid with a K name, which is kinda what we want because Kanye and I are both K names, then they will call us the 'KKK', complained Kim as she feared comparisons to the race-hate group.

'That is a little crazy though,' sympathized Brody.

Itching to give birth: In another attempt to avoid getting stretch marks, Kim slapped herself repeatedly instead of scratching

Rest and relaxation: Kim's need to keep her skin flawless was apparent as she sunned herself in a very tiny bikini

Family time: Kim was joined by her sisters, brothers and parents on the trip

No expenses spared: The extended family spent their week aboard a luxurious boat

'I am feeling North, North West,' added Brody as the family sailed on a mega yacht during their Greek family vacation.

'Me too. It is pretty and cool and not just like Tomboyish, it is just good,' agreed Kourtney.

Then Kim turned her attention to how good a dad Kanye would be.

'I think Kanye will be really good. He says 'There is nothing I love more in this world than you and me, myself, so imagine a combination of the two',' revealed Kim.

Keeping it simple: Kim discussed that she didn't want a 'K' baby name, unlike her family's choices

Taking a new direction: Brody suggested 'North' -- and Kim made that her choice a few months later

'Not tomboyish': 'It's pretty and cool,' Kourtney suggested

The family matriarch, Kris Jenner, spent the episode butting heads with her step-son Brody Jenner.

The two have always had an uneasy relationship and Kris - who oddly wore a wig during much of the episode - was determined to do something about it.

'I feel like he judges me and it is always after a remark because a lot of times he takes me the wrong way, he just does not get my humor,' complained Kris.

'I just want him to be part of the family.'

'Having a blended family is never easy and I feel like I have been painted as not always positive in Brody's eyes.

'If one of us is going to make a break here I have to be the bigger person and reach out to Brody. I want him to be one of us. Family comes first.'

Just another holiday: The family enjoyed a light meal as their yacht docked in Santorini

All alone: Kim travelled without boyfriend Kanye West at her side

Brody then had to spend more time with the family onboard a luxury yacht, with Kris trying extra hard for them to get along.

This included Kris dancing with Brody and posing in pictures together.

'It is difficult figuring out the relationship between a step parent and a step child, you really have to work at it,' said Kris.

'You have to work at it and have continual communication, which I have not always had with Brody unfortunately.'

Feeling wild: Kris debuted a long wig at her family's first dinner

Needing a change: Kris decided she would surprise her family with her makeover

Divided: While Kris liked her new do, her family was less receptive

But Kris made the family feel increasingly awkward as she rubbed suncream on Brody's back and compared his physique to her husband's before playfully jumping on top of him.

'I want to think that Kris is being overly nice, I don't want to think anything else besides that,' said a bemused Brody.

And Kris kept up the awkwardness by offering to cuddle with Brody as the night got colder.

Brody refused to take the bait saying: 'I just want this trip to be fun.'

 

Adventure: Khloe and Kourtney slipped into wet suits and swam in the warm Mediterranean ocean

YOLO: Living by her fun-loving motto, Kourtney dared Khloe to jump off the side of their yacht

Apprehensive: After a bit of hesitation, Khloe finally mustered the courage to take the leap

Exhausted: While much of the family was partying upstairs, Kim was often found resting in the decks below

Later Brody confided in brother Brandon about the situation with Kris.

'I think she doesn't think I like her. Over the years I thought Kris was the reason I didn't see my father and I painted her as the villain,' confided Brody.

'I just don't think she knows how to act around me, I think she is being nice but it was just an awkward way to be nice. It was a little weird.'

Helping hand: Kris offered to place sun screen on Brody's back as he laid under the sun

Well that's awkward: After commenting on his figure, Kris then acted playful with her stepson

Trying to break the ice: Kris claimed her behaviour was aimed at easing the tension between the pair

Kris could not let it go confiding in Kourtney that she does not know how to act around Brody as she feels he is 'judging' her.

The situation between the pair came to a head as the family enjoyed a day yachting, with Kris confronting Brody about his attitude to her.

'I worry about how you feel about me now as a mom,' Kris told him.

Not Kris' biggest fan: Brody chose to make his sour relationship with Kris the topic of conversation

Placing blame: Brody told his brother Brandon he 'painted her as a villain'

Blended family: Big brother Brandon as well as brother Burt are children of Bruce's first marriage to actress Linda Thompson

And Brody told her that he does have a 'serious issue' with her.

'Can I tell you what my serious problem is, I'm gonna be totally and brutally honest with you?

'I know that you love the lavish lifestyle. You need to look at yourself and be like "Are these things the important things in life?"

'Like this, I don't give a s**t about this,' said Brody gesturing at the yacht.

Tough questions: Brody confronted Kris about her taste for nice things

'Brutally honest': The conversation came after years of animosity between the two

'Definitely a bit of a diva': Kris claimed that she still puts her children first

And Kris was not taking it lying down.

'I'm definitely a bit of a diva, I'm not going to deny that,' said Kris.

'It is not about this stuff, it is about all of you. For real.

'When you were really young I always thought, "I wish he liked me."

'I felt that if you knew me you would love me and I felt you did not know me and all these different layers and sides to me.'

Kris told Brody that she just wanted her family to be happy.

'I don't feel you have ever given two f**ks about the way Brandon and I grew up,' Brody fired back.

To the camera Kris said she felt Brody's words were some of the 'most hurtful' ever thrown at her and that he left her feeling 'so defeated.'

Where's Scott? Family members grew concerned as Scott was continuously a no show

Doing his duties: Scott missed out on the trip to do several appearances in London


Not missing much: Scott still managed to slip in a few seconds in the water

Missing from the family vacation was Scott Disick, who skipped out to do personal appearances in London.

'He gets anxious and like he just thinks it is setting himself up for disaster,' explained Kourtney.

'He probably would get anxious and end up cussing someone out, who knows what would happen.

'Part of me wishes he would just come but I am fine, I like hanging out with everyone.'

Worried: A make-up free Kourtney confided in the cameras that she 'wasn't worried' about Scott's behaviour

Going rogue: But Kourtney was clearly displeased by a strange set of paparazzi photos of Scott's trip in London

Boys time: Between appearances, Scott slipped in a few sips of his favourite drinks

And missing the family vacay did not seem to overly bother him as he partied in England with pals.

'Of course I feel a little guilty for neglecting Kourtney and everybody but the truth is I do not want to ruin her trip, which usually happens on these long family trips where everybody gets on each others nerves,' said Scott.

Kourtney was even more annoyed when she saw pictures of Scott leaving a club with some girls in the early hours of the morning.

'I try to be very understanding of Scott being an only child and not being able to be with my family for a long period of time but it is just disappointing that he let these photographs happen, even though I know he didn't do anything.

'Scott is putting himself in a bad position when he should know better.
