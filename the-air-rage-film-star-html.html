The 'air rage' film star

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, March 29, 2024

Hollywood actress Rebecca Broussard has been arrested on suspicion of air rage after exploding in anger when she was refused more champagne on a transatlantic flight.

Miss Broussard, the former lover of Jack Nicholson, was so ' abusive, aggressive and disruptive' that she had to be restrained as she lashed out.

According to a friend, the Die Hard star had been drinking before boarding the plane and carried on as the flight began.

She was said to have been behaving in a 'drunk and agitated' manner and when cabin staff refused her requests for more to drink the 34-year-old 'lost the plot' and was overheard saying, 'I want some champagne, I've paid £ 4 , 000 for this ticket'.

Her furious reaction forced the pilot of the Virgin Atlantic flight from Los Angeles to Heathrow to make an emergency landing in Canada because of fears for the safety of passengers and crew.

Miss Broussard, who has two children by Oscar-winner Nicholson and last year won a £10million palimony settlement from him, was met at Winnipeg airport on Wednesday by police.

After hospital treatment for her condition, she was taken into custody.

The former model, who was travelling under her married name Rebecca Kelly, has told friends that her behaviour was triggered by a sleeping tablet causing an 'unexplained' reaction.

According to staff on the flight, the actress 'hurled foul-mouthed abuse' and refused to stay in her seat.

A spokesman for the airline said: 'The pilot felt the woman's behaviour was putting the other passenger's and his crew in danger and he was forced to make a decision.

'The woman was unwell as well as behaving in an aggressive, disruptive and abusive manner.

'He decided it would be best for all concerned if he made an emergency landing at Winnipeg.

' The woman was taken to a local hospital, and once she had recovered she was taken into police custody.'

Miss Broussard's outburst caused the the plane, an Airbus A340 300, carrying 252 passengers, to be delayed more than three hours on its 11-hour flight.

Her husband, actor Alex Kelly, was yesterday said to be travelling to see the actress, who was believed to be still in custody.

Miss Broussard, who comes from a wealthy family, met Nicholson, now 65, on the set of The Two Jakes. She had a relationship with him for seven years and was one of the few women he allowed to be his live-in lover.

They have two children Lorraine, 11, and Raymond, nine, and it was the birth of Lorraine that brought Nicholson's 17-year relationship with actress Anjelica Huston to an abrupt end.

Nicholson, who lives alone in a bachelor mansion in Beverly Hills, dotes on Lorraine and Raymond.

Miss Broussard lives nearby and has custody of the children.

Nicholson has said of his children: ' I like to take time out with the children. I have a daughter and son I idolise and want to see as much of them as I can.'
