Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, July 1, 2024

The ‘Win a Guitar Autographed by George Strait’ Contest (the “Contest”)

2.         PRIZE: The prize of the Contest consists of the following:  one (1) guitar, autographed by George Strait.

The winner will be notified by e-mail.  In the event that Sponsor cannot reach the winner within three (3) days after it attempts to notify the winner, Sponsor will select a new winner according to these rules.  Additionally, if the winner is ineligible, it will be deemed as if the winner has rejected the prize, and Sponsor will select a new winner.

To claim the Prize, the winner must:

(a)        confirm receipt of the notice by return email; and

(b)       complete, sign and return a Winner Affidavit & Release form (to be provided by Sponsor), a 1099-MISC IRS tax form (to be provided by Sponsor) and such other documentation (if any) requested by Sponsor.
