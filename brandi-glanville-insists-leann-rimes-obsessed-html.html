Brandi Glanville insists LeAnn Rimes is 'obsessed'

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, June 14, 2024

Their fraught affairs have played out in painful detail in the glare of the public eye.

Yet Brandi Glanville is showing no signs of quashing her beef with her ex-husband Eddie Cibrian and his new wife Leann Rimes, with her latest allegation stating that the songstress is 'obsessed' with her.

The 44-year-old Real Housewives of Beverly Hills lashed out at the couple once again as their long-standing feud rumbles on - this time claiming her love rival is trying to steal her life.

LeAnn, 34, is currently married to Brandi's ex-husband Eddie - with whom she has two sons, Mason, 14, and Jake, 10 - and star has now claimed LeAnn has been "copying her life" and using her employees to "harass" her online.

She said: 'Over the past few recent years I have really made an effort to take the higher road with Eddie and LeAnn for the sake of my boys in an attempt to have a more positive co-parenting situation. I honestly do prefer peace.

'However, just recently I was invited to attend a party for a magazine and just three hours prior to the event was called and told that Leann & Eddie asked that I be uninvited or they would not attend.

'Honestly, who does that? We are all adults here. I've had to deal with harassment online by her employees for years and these are people she pays- her music producer, hairstylist, and former road manager to be specific. 

'I did not react to these recent things I tried to turn a blind eye.'

The My Kitchen Rules star claims she reached a "breaking point" last month when LeAnn allegedly "hurt and tormented" her by using her two sons "as pawns".

She continued in a lengthy statement first obtained by TheDirty.com: 'My last straw was a couple of weeks ago when her and her assistant watched my boyfriend's Snapchats several times over the course of a few hours and just happened to show up where we said we would be to celebrate step-Mother's Day (a day early).

Hurt: 'Honestly, who does that? We are all adults here. I've had to deal with harassment online by her employees for years and these are people she pays- her music producer, hairstylist, and former road manager to be specific'

'They sat across the room from us just to hurt and torment me by using my own children as pawns. I've done my best to keep quiet over all of her antics but bringing my boys into this was my breaking point. I just want the truth out there and for all of her nonsense to stop.

'As with any mother, all I wish is for my boys to have the peaceful family life they deserve. I am in a great place with both love and work and I'd like to be left to live it out without this nonsense.'

MailOnline has approached representatives for LeAnn, Eddie and Brandi for comment. 

Happy days: Rimes and Cibrian married in April 2011 - making Rimes stepmother to Glanville's boys - which has seen both women at each other's social media throats ever since

Brandi and Eddie married in 2001. They split shortly after it emerged Cibrian had had an affair with Rimes - who herself was in a seven year marriage to backup dancer Dean Sheremet - while working together on the 2009 film Lifetime TV movie Northern Lights.

Rimes and Cibrian married in April 2011 - making Rimes stepmother to Glanville's boys - which has seen both women at each other's social media throats ever since.

However last year the pair appeared to have put their differences behind them. 

In April of 2016, Leann shared a lengthy post beneath an inspirational poster that decreed:'You don't need to tear one person down to support another'.
