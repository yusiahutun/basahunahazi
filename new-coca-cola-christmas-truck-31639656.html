New Coca-Cola Christmas truck tour date revealed - see if it's your town

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, February 28, 2024

The Coca-Cola Christmas truck is on a festive tour around the UK, and it's just announced its next stop and a list of upcoming destinations.

After visiting cities like Glasgow, Leeds, Manchester, Liverpool, and Birmingham, the iconic red lorry will be heading to Milton Keynes today (December 10).

Families can step into a Winter Wonderland with the new Snow Globe for a perfect holiday snap. There's also the Ho-Ho-Hoop hut where you can win prizes and enjoy the Coca-Cola Christmas choir. The event is free, except in Dublin and Belfast where tickets need to be booked in advance.

Florence Wheatley, Brand Manager at Coca-Cola Company, Great Britain, said: "Coca-Cola has been spreading Christmas cheer for decades and, this year, we've added even more exciting elements to make it as magical and merry as possible.

"Alongside the beloved 'Holidays Are Coming' advert, we have introduced new elements like the 'World Needs More Santas' TVC and an interactive Santa Quiz."

Steve Butterworth, CEO at Neighbourly, added: "We're excited to join forces with Coca-Cola over the festive period. The Christmas Truck Tour is a fantastic way to increase awareness of Neighbourly's mission to help businesses build stronger communities. The partnership with Coca-Cola is about more than just festivities; it's about making a real difference."

The Coca-Cola Christmas truck tour so far:

Silverburn Shopping Centre, Glasgow: Friday, November 24, 12:00 - 20:00

Fort Kinnaird Shopping Centre, Edinburgh: Saturday, November 25, 12:00 - 20:00

The Metrocentre in Gateshead hosted the truck on Sunday, November 26, from 11:00 - 17:00. The White Rose Shopping Centre in Leeds was next up on Thursday November 30, from 12:00 - 20:00.

Liverpool ONE was the third stop on Friday December 1, from 12:00 - 20:00. The Trafford Centre in Manchester followed on Saturday December 2, from 12:00 - 20:00.

Sheffield's Meadowhall Centre hosted it on Sunday December 3, from 11:00 - 17:00. Wolverhampton's Market Square was next on Thursday December 7, from 12:00 - 20:00.

Leicester's Fosse Park Avenue was scheduled for Friday December 8, from 12:00 - 20:00. Birmingham's Bullring was up next on Saturday December 9, from 12:00 - 20:00.

Centre: MK in Milton Keynes wraps up the announced dates on Sunday, December 10, from 11:00 - 17:00.

This year's tour will make 17 stops in total, with seven more yet to be revealed. This includes eight new locations compared to last year.

The remaining dates and locations are still under wraps, but last year saw the festive attraction visit London, Watford, Cardiff and Bristol.
