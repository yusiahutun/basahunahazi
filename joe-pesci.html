Joe Pesci Acting Career

 The actor started working at the young age of 18 and has been in the film industry for more than six decades now.

 His first role was that of a dancer in Hey, Let’s Twist in 1961. Unfortunately, this role of his still remains uncredited. 

Because of his impressive performance in The Death Collector, Martin Scorsese, and Robert De Niro asked him to act in their next movie Raging Bull. His role in Raging Bull as Joey LaMotta skyrocketed his acting career to new heights.

He also starred in Michael Jackson‘s music video Smooth Criminal where he played the character of a crime boss. During this period, Joe also starred in a tv-series named Half Nelson.

After acting in several big-budget successful movies, Joe Pesci announced his retirement from the acting world in 1999. The reason for the announcement was the fact that he wanted to pursue his passion for music.
