5 of the most expensive marine aquarium fish money can buy in Hong Kong

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

 3. Tiger angelfish (HK$40,000)
Sporting tiger-like stripes, it comes from South Africa and prefers to live in colder, deeper waters.
4. Yellowbar angelfish-semicircle angelfish hybrid (HK$30,000)
This mixed breed is endemic to the waters of India, Australia and Hawaii.
5. Clarion angelfish (HK$10,000)
This vibrant orange fish with a brown head and small mouth is native to Mexico.
Source: various Hong Kong aquariums
