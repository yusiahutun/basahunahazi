Career And Salary 

As a media personality, Paula Ebben co-anchors the WBZ-TV News at 5:30 PM and WBZ-TV 8 PM News on myTV38 with co-anchor Liam Martin.

Note: She is estimated to accumulate an average salary of about $69K per year for her service in the WBZ-TV. 

Paula started her career as a reporter at WGMC-TV Charter Communications in November 2001. After serving for eight months, she joined New England Cable News as a reporter and an anchor. During her progression in New England Cable News, she got nominated for multiple New England Emmy Awards for Anchor, Consumer Reporter, Education Series, and Writing. 

Later, on December 2002, she joined WBZ-TV as an anchor. Also, she started working in the WBZ-TV CBS Corporation from the same year. 

As a part of WBZ-TV’s team coverage of the Boston Marathon Bombings, she, along with her team, bagged the 2014 Columbia DuPont Award and a 2013 Peabody Award. Also, she had won the regional RTNDA Edward R. Murrow Award for Best Feature Reporting in 2005.
