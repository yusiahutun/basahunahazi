The WiZink Center Joins The European Arenas Association

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 11, 2024
Luis García, CC BY-SA 3.0 ES

PARIS (VIP NEWS) — The WiZink Center in Madrid has joined the European Arenas Association (EAA).

EAA’s President, Olivier Toth, announced the news welcoming new members to the fold at the organization’s annual Spring General Meeting held at the Accor Arena in Paris.

With the addition of the WiZink Center, the EAA now boasts 38 venues spread across 19 countries.

“We are glad to be part of the EAA and to be able to share experiences and knowledge with our fellow members from now and on,” said Manuel Saucedo, WiZink Center chief executive.

“We will be enhancing the venue’s role in the Worldwide events industry. Belonging to this Association is a new step for Madrid’s WiZink Center in our international strategy, something that we are very proud of and that reinforces us as one of the most important venues in the world.”
