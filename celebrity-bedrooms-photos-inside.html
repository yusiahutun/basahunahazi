32 private celebrity bedrooms revealed: Holly Willoughby, Amanda Holden, Stacey Dooley and more

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 16, 2024

These look like the perfect setting for a good night's sleep

    Your bedroom should be a sanctuary for rest and relaxation, and these stars have definitely created spaces that fit the bill. With king-size beds, luxurious bedding and opulent interiors, some of these celebrity bedrooms could be mistaken for a five-star hotel room, and they're giving us lots of bedroom design inspiration.

    From Michelle Keegan and Mark Wright's glamorous bedroom décor to Geri Horner's dark aesthetic, take a look through some of the most beautiful celebrity bedrooms below…

    LOOK: Unreal celebrity kitchens revealed

    Millie Mackintosh's bedroom

    Millie Mackintosh shared a look inside her bedroom shortly after welcoming baby Sienna Grace into the world. The luxurious space has cream carpets and white walls with huge sash windows. The bed is dressed with pink and purple linen, and there is a pink velvet chair at the other side next to Sienna's moses basket. 

    Holly Willoughby's bedroom

    Holly revealed a peek inside her bedroom via a photo of her pet cat on her bed. There is a grey and white colour scheme, with white bedding and a grey knitted throw, and a grey button-back armchair as seen in the background.

    RELATED: Inside Dancing on Ice star Holly Willoughby's bedroom

    Amanda Holden's bedroom

    Amanda Holden has the most breathtaking home in the Cotswolds, and her bedroom is made for Instagram! It features exposed brick walls with a vaulted ceiling and wooden awning, while the bed has a metallic brass frame. The bed is covered by a quilted throw and lilac and silver cushions. How dreamy!

    Carol Vorderman's bedroom

    Former star Carol Vorderman revealed her bedroom at one of her gorgeous homes – and it's so beautiful. The star posed in skintight blue sports leggings and a matching long-sleeved crop top to take a mirror selfie, and she inadvertently revealed her gorgeous boudoir. The room has been designed with calming neutral colours including a cream carpet, beige walls and a grey bed.

    Michelle Keegan's bedroom

    Michelle Keegan gave viewers a look inside the bedroom of her former home which she shared with husband Mark Wright when she appeared on . The room has a glamorous aesthetic with mirrored bedside tables and a double bed with an upholstered headboard that almost reaches the ceiling. The couple are now currently living with Mark's parents as they build their dream home from scratch.

    Stacey Dooley's bedroom

    Stacey Dooley showed off her beautiful Scandi-esque bedroom at the home she shares with Kevin Clifton. It follows a minimalist theme with wooden furniture and a muted colour scheme.

    Roman Kemp's bedroom

    star Roman Kemp has a very fancy looking bedroom where he has opted for neutral tones with an ivory upholstered bed frame, white bedding and a cream quilted bedspread.

    Stacey Solomon's bedroom

    Stacey and Joe Swash's bedroom at their £1.2million house features vaulted ceilings with wooden beams that have been painted cream to match the cream walls. As it stands, there are floral cream and pink curtains framing the windows, and dark oak wooden window ledges.

    Fearne Cotton's bedroom

    Fearne Cotton unveiled her quirky bedroom on Instagram. The walls are painted pink with Dulux's Pressed Petal shade, and she has white wooden floors. Her furniture includes a retro fringed floor lamp and a white dressing table. 

    Lorraine Kelly's bedroom

    Lorraine gave her bedroom a cosy update for winter by creating a relaxing seating area with a grey chaise longue and pale blue armchair. Explaining that she favours cooler colours in the bedroom, Lorraine said: "I find them very soothing and a great neutral base for adding bolder furniture and accessories."

    Lorraine's daughter Rosie has a beautifully decorated room thanks to Wayfair. Lorraine explained: "It had to be stylish with lots of storage space and somewhere she would feel happy to spend her time. The furniture in this room was looking quite tired before and there were so many soft toys! I think it had been a little neglected but now it is completely transformed and I love how bright, modern and elegant it looks."

    Amy Dowden's bedroom

    Strictly Come Dancing pro Amy Dowden gave fans a rare peek inside her home with this snap, which showed the matching pineapple lamps she has on display in her bedroom. While the room is decorated in calming neutral tones, with a soft grey quilted bedspread and matching bedside tables topped with candles and a carafe of water, Amy used the two pineapple-shaped table lamps to make a cool design statement.

    Rochelle and Marvin Humes' bedroom

    Rochelle Humes shared a birthday message to her husband Marvin on Instagram with a post that offered a unique look at the couple’s bedroom, which has pale grey walls, a wooden bedside table with a retro-style house phone and white lamp on it, and the highlight – a huge king-size bed with an ornate carved wooden headboard.

    Lucy Fallon's bedroom

    How gorgeous isstar Lucy Fallon’s bedroom?! The quirky room is filled with personality, with a mix of pattern, texture and colour to create the covetable finished result. Lucy has opted for a white and grey zig zag print duvet cover from Next, which is offset by a colourful throw adorned with pom poms, which she found in HomeSense.

    Zoella's bedroom

    How gorgeous is YouTube star (and Joe Sugg’s big sister) Zoella’s bedroom? Although the room is painted white with light carpets, Zoe has added pops of colour with teal bedding, green curtains and framed wall art. The bedroom has a large double bed at the centre, topped with a teal quilted bedspread and cute cushions.

    Not only is her own bedroom beautifully decorated, but Zoe Sugg has worked her magic on her guest bedroom too, which has a striking teal and mustard colour scheme, and gallery wall featuring a selection of statement art work and slogan prints.

    Geri Horner's bedroom

    Geri Horner gave fans a rare peek inside her bedroom, showing her unusual dark green wallpaper featuring splashes of pink and green paint. Geri appears to have a large king-size bed with an upholstered headboard, topped with plain white bedding and a cosy brown throw and matching cushion. There are bedside tables on either side, accessorised with spherical lamps and books.

    Jamie Oliver's bedroom

    Celebrity chef Jamie Oliver has a luxurious bedroom with a king-size grey and gold ornate bed frame, a traditional fireplace and stylish touches including framed prints that read "Peace" and "Kiss". It’s no wonder then, that Jamie and Jools’ bed has been commandeered by their son Buddy.

    Shania Twain's bedroom

    Shania Twain owns some of the most incredible homes in Switzerland, Canada and Las Vegas, but it's her Bahamas property that we can't get over. Shania filmed a video of herself making her bed which is a white wooden style with cream baroque bedding which perfectly matches the curtains.

    Ruth Langsford and Eamonn Holmes' bedroom

    presenters Ruth and Eamonn share a spacious and stylish bedroom, with a relaxing neutral colour scheme. A large king size bed topped with a cosy taupe bedspread and velvet cushions takes pride of place at the centre of the room, with white bedside tables with matching lamps on either side.

    Lady Amelia Windsor's bedroom

    She may be royalty, but Lady Amelia Windsor’s bedroom is refreshingly down to earth. The room has plain cream walls, but Amelia has added character with lots of personal photos. She has placed an array of Polaroid snaps of herself, her friends and family around the frame of her dressing table mirror, while more photos have been stuck up on the walls. The young royal has hung a coloured feather decorative ornament on the walls, while a pot on her dressing table has been filled with various skincare products.

    Kelly Ripa's bedroom

    Kelly Ripa lives in Manhattan in an impressive $27million townhouse with her husband Mark Consuelos and their children, and their stunning home has a hotel-worthy bedroom. The large bed features a cream headboard, and it has been dressed with plain white sheets, and behind that is a metallic wallpaper, adding a luxury feel to the room.

    Matthew Perry's bedroom

    Matthew Perry's former £15million LA penthouse had a pretty impressive room - with an epic view! It had a massive bed, grey accents from the rug and cushions, and wooden flooring throughout.

    Novak Djokovic's bedroom

    Novak Djokovic has an impressive property portfolio and one of the residences is this incredible beachfront in Miami. The bedroom has a neutral colour scheme designed by RDAI - globally recognised for their work with Hermès. Sliding glass doors lead out onto the balcony which surrounds the apartment, and offers the tennis champion beautiful sea views from his bed.

    Eva Longoria's bedroom

    The luxurious master suite in Eva's home has its own private den and bar area, along with his and hers bathrooms and walk-in wardrobes – what more could Eva possibly want? The room has a king size bed, sofa and armchairs, along with a fitted fireplace and views of Beverly Hills from the large windows.

    Jennifer Lawrence's bedroom

    Jennifer Lawrence owns and rents out an apartment in a star-studded building in Tribeca, New York, where Justin Timberlake, Jessica Biel, Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively are among the fellow residents. The master suite is modern and features a neutral décor, ideal for Jennifer's tenants to add their own personal touches with their furnishings and homeware.

    Cindy Crawford's bedroom

    Cindy and Rande's home has five bedrooms including this master suite, which has its own bathroom and powder room. The spacious room is furnished with a king-size bed and wooden dressing tables, along with two armchairs that look out onto the back garden. Two pieces of artwork hanging on the walls add character to the neutral décor.

    Jennifer Lopez's bedroom

    There are four bedrooms in J.Lo's New York duplex penthouse, including this stunning suite that leads out onto a private outdoor garden. The room is furnished with a four-poster bed, chaise longue sofa, and a chest of drawers, with the same white and neutral colour scheme that runs throughout the rest of the property.

    John Krasinski and Emily Blunt's bedroom

    The master suite and bathroom take up an entire floor of the house that John and Emily own in Brooklyn. The bedroom is spacious enough for a king-size bed and two wooden console tables, with a plush pink sofa in the bay window. It is decorated in a distinctive pastel-stripe wallpaper, with a large grey rug placed across the wooden flooring.

    Vince Vaugh's bedroom

    Vince's home (which was previously owned by Kate Bosworth) has three bedrooms including this large master suite, which has plenty of space for a king-sized bed, separate chaise longue and dressing table. The room has dual aspect views across the garden, with a private balcony, en-suite bathroom and two walk-in wardrobes – perfect to store any star's clothing collection.

    Lindsey Buckingham's bedroom

    There are six bedrooms in the luxurious property built by Fleetwood Mac star Lindsey, including this master suite that has a king size bed, plus room for two armchairs, a chaise longue and traditional fireplace. The spacious room overlooks the garden and swimming pool, and also has its own private balcony with a separate seating area.

    Johnny Depp's bedroom

    Arguably one of the highlights of Johnny Depp's LA penthouse is the master bedroom, which features huge floor-to-ceiling windows and has great views across the Los Angeles skyline. With exposed brickwork and pipes the room has a cool, loft-style vibe, and is currently decorated with a portrait from Johnny's personal art collection, which isn't available for sale.
