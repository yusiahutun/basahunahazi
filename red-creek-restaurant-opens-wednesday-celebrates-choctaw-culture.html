Red Creek Restaurant opens Wednesday, celebrates Choctaw culture | Free News

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Bok Homa Casino will celebrate the grand opening of its new Red Creek Restaurant at 11 a.m. Wednesday. The restaurant will offer a variety of dishes, including appetizers, soups and salads, burgers and po’boys, as well as daily plate specials.

Sonny Johnson, president and CEO, invited the public to come to the grand opening and “discover the delicious options” on the menu.

“At Bok Homa Casino, we are always looking for ways to enhance our guests’ experience and satisfaction,” he said. “We are proud to introduce Red Creek Restaurant, a new dining destination that offers a variety of dishes to suit every taste and preference.”

Red Creek Restaurant celebrates the Choctaw (Chahta) heritage, which inspired the name of the restaurant. Bok means “creek” and Homa means “red” in the Choctaw language. Red Creek Restaurant is also part of the recent expansion of Bok Homa Casino, which added 7,000 square feet of gaming and entertainment space, including a state-of-the-art Sportsbook and an entertainment stage.

For more information, visit www.BokHomaCasino.com.
