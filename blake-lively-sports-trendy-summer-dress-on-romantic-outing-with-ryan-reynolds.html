Blake Lively sports 'trendy' summer dress on romantic outing with Ryan Reynolds

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, March 7, 2024

Blake Lively sported a trendy summer dress during a romantic outing with her husband Ryan Reynolds in Paris where they enjoyed some couple time without their kids.

The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants star has been admired for her fashion style and bold statements in public for years. But, just like us, Blake knows that summer is all about comfort and style and she chose the perfect summer dress for a lunch date with her husband.

Blake Lively sports summer dress in Paris

The Hollywood couple turned heads on social media with their recent outing in Paris.

Blake and Ryan were spotted hands in hands as they strolled through the streets of the French capital and enjoyed some time alone without their children.

The Age of Adaline actress sported a trendy summer dress in a beautiful floral print with a long dark blazer, while Ryan sported jeans and a casual printed shirt.

The Gossip Girl actress opted for loose locks and minimal makeup with white sneakers for their romantic tour around Paris.

Blake receives compliments for her dress

Blake received hundreds of compliments for her gorgeous summer outfit from various media outlets and fans on social media.

One Twitter account called Blake’s summer dress “trendy”.

“They look so good,” another fan gushed over the couple’s loved-up pictures in the capital of love.

One outlet labeled the celebrity‘s dress “the prettiest”, while another one said that the actress “put on an elegant display in a printed summer dress”.

The actress launched Betty Booze

The actress recently debuted a new line of sparkling cocktails called Betty Booze in addition to her non-alcoholic drinks brand Betty Buzz.

Blake unveiled the line of sparkling lemonade cocktails next to a lemonade stand with a cute summer outfit for a promo snap on Instagram.

The Gossip Girl star revealed she was inspired to launch the new line after requests from her family and she developed the drink “from the lemonade recipe I made up 13 years ago for summer lemonade stands”.
