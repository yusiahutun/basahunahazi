Beaming Lily James steps out for a stroll with longtime actor pal Bart Edwards in London

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, April 1, 2024

Lily James appeared in great spirits as she stepped out for a stroll with her friend  Bart Edwards over the weekend in sunny London.

The actress, 33, and her pal, 34, had been indulging in some retail therapy, as she carried a white paper bag in her hand while heading down the street.

Lily looked to be having a lovely time with Bart as she threw her head back and laughed as they walked alongside each other.

The Whats Love Got To Do With It? star was sporting a long black shearling jacket, teamed with a polo neck jumper and a pair of blue jeans.

She completed the look with a leather baker boy style cap and a pair of chunky black boots, while styling her locks in a loose natural style.

Aside from her hugely successful acting career, it was recently revealed that Lily is being lined up to launch a solo music career, with producer Naughty Boy admitting he was 'amazed by her performance skills'.

The songwriter, real name Shahid Khan, 42, recently worked with the actress in the studio for her latest film, What's Love Got To Do With It?. 

Speaking to The Daily Mail's Richard Eden, he spoke of his experience working with Lily, on the track she recorded for the new romantic comedy.  

Naughty Boy said: 'Lily James can really sing. I was amazed by how at ease she was in the studio performing.'

Lily, who stars as leading lady Zoe in the film, also features as a guest vocalist on a new track in the new movie.  

The actress recently gushed that it was 'an absolute pleasure' as she took to the recording studio with record producer Naughty Boy.

The song Mahi Sona (The Wedding Song) written by the producer and actress Preeya Kalidas especially for the movie will be available in three versions which are out now. 

The first features Lily alongside legendary Pakistani singer Rahat Fateh Ali Khan, superstar Indian singer Kanika Kapoor, rapper Billy Khan. Another with Brit Award nominee Joy Crookes and the third without features.

Lily, who plays Zoe in the movie, said: 'It was an absolute pleasure working in the studio with both  [composer] Nitin [Sawhney] and Naughty Boy. Music plays a huge part in the film, and I'm thrilled to contribute to this amazing single alongside some incredible artists' 

Lily has showcased her impressive vocals in the past in Mama Mia 2: Here We Go Again as well as Cinderella.

The film follows her character, a documentary filmmaker named Zoe, who navigates the modern dating scene with little success, much to the dismay of her mother Cath (Oscar-winner Emma Thompson).

As Zoe documents Kazim's journey from London to Lahore to marry a stranger chosen by his parents, she begins to question whether the traditional approach to finding love might have something to offer.
