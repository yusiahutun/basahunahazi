Monkey GO Happy

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Last updated on June 5th, 2023 at 09:20 pm

Monkey GO Happy is one of the best $4.99 to play game in the App Store.
Developed by Robin Vencel, Monkey GO Happy is a Family game with a content rating of 4+.
It was released on 25th November 2015 with the latest update 8th January 2018

Whether you are a fan of Family, Education, or Puzzle games, you will find this game interesting and will absolutely like it.

51 people have rated 1.5

You can download the game Monkey GO Happy from APP STORE.

Over 75 stages of puzzling fun to complete! 5 first original Monkey GO Happy games have been compiled into one great app.

Monkey GO Happy from Pencilkids is an internet sensation that has reached millions of players online.

Monkey GO Happy regularly reaches the #1 spot on Primary Games and other popular flash gaming websites. Now it’s available for your iPad or iPhone.

Features:

  • over 75 beautifully designed stages
  • 30 Hats to dress up your monkey
  • 7 different monkeys to choose from
  • Ad FREE
  • NO In-app purchases
  • Automatic save progress

Instructions: Tap on objects and locations on the screen. Drag items to specific spots to solve puzzles and more!

This app has been updated by Apple to display the Apple Watch app icon.

Works with new version of iOS.
