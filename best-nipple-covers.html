How do I find the right nipple cover?

The best nipple cover for your outfit will depend on a few factors, including the cut of your top or dress, the amount of coverage you're comfortable with, and the level of support you're hoping to find. Options range from small, smoothing adhesives to padded, backless bras.

Pay close attention to the shade range for a more natural look, expert Kate Nixon recommends. "If you’re looking to wear something a little sheer and reduce the visibility of the nipple, I’d recommend looking for covers in a shade similar to your skin tone."
