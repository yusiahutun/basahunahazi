Oriini Kaipara- What Is “Moko Kauae” Tattoo Meaning?

A notable broadcaster, Oriini, came to light in 2019 when she tattooed sacred Maori on her face. Since then, people have always been curious about the meaning of her “moko kauae” tattoo.

The tattoo has a traditional meaning which represents a woman’s whānau. The tattoo also represents Maori’s woman leadership and services for her community.

The sacred tattoo is known for one’s status, abilities, status, and commitment to the community. It is also a traditional taonga that passes down from generation to generation.

A woman from the Maori community holds the right to have a “moko kauae” tattoo. The practice of putting the sacred tattoo began in the year 1990.
