Dwayne Johnson and Aaron Paul shoot scenes for Central Intelligence in Boston

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 16, 2024

Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson and Aaron Paul faced a tricky situation as they shot scenes for their new movie in Massachusetts.

The two actors were spotted during a night shoot on a bridge over Boston's Charles River with shocked expressions in their faces.

Johnson, 43, is seen standing behind Paul, 35, who has a revolver in his hand, as they play the parts of CIA agents in the movie Central Intelligence.

Scroll down for video 

Facing the enemy: Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson and Aaron Paul have been shooting scenes for their film Central Intelligence at night on location in Massachusetts this past week

As the cameras rolled, the San Andreas star is seen ducking down behind the Breaking Bad actor before darting out from behind him shooting his gun.

The night-time scene is obviously one where the two agents come face-to-face with an enemy in the espionage action comedy.

Going rogue: Johnson's character - the CIA's top contract killer - is a rogue spy and Paul's character might 'be sacrificed' as he tries to complete the mission he's set himself

Acting out: The Breaking Bad actor was seen venting his anger in  he scene shot on a bridge over Boston's Charles River

In between the intense scenes, however, Paul and Johnson were seen breaking the tension laughing and joking together on the location set.

The film is designed to be a buddy movie showcasing the talents of Johnson and Hart and tapping into The Rock's comedy chops as well as his action star status.

Hart plays a former high school stand out athlete who now has a boring job as an accountant.

On the eve of his class reunion, he's contacted by Johnson, a loser in high school who was bullied by the other kids but who is now a top contract killer with the CIA.

The rogue spy wants his former classmate to help him bring down a plot to sell classified military secrets but it doesn't take long for the accountant to realize he may have bitten off more than he can chew in his quest for some excitement in his life.

Mean: The Rock, a former pro wrestler, channeled his inner warrior for the intense action sequence

Levity: But once the director called 'cut, the Fast & Furious star was seen breaking the tension with a few jokes

The night shoots have clearly been challenging for the cast and crew.

On Saturday, the wrestler turned movie star tweeted from the set: 'therock4am - night shoots. No sleep all week, grinding it out and pushing thru.'

'Just when I thought the night was over - I gotta fight martial arts master @action_jason who's perfected the "540 Cork Round Kick"... to my head.' 

Taking his punishment: Paul shared this black and white photo of himself waiting on the bridge for the director call 'action'

Sore: The 35-year-old actor took some beating during filming and shared this selfie on Instagram on Wednesday as he sought to alleviate his neck pain with a hot water bottle

And Paul has also been taking a beating.

He shared a selfie on Wednesday showing him lying down with a hot water bottle behind his head .

He wrote on his Instagram page: 'Getting beat up in Boston has its perks. Hot water bottle on the neck feels real nice when you have a long night ahead of you. @kevinhart4real and @therock are legends and I'm proud to be working with them. Hope this neck thing doesn't hold us back tonight boys.' 

Meet Bob: Johnson described his Central Intelligence character as like Tom Hanks in BIG but with a twist. He shared this photo with fans and said Bob 'is one of the most lethal men on the planet and 'hates bullies'

Johnson, though, has said he's having a blast creating his Central Intelligence character 'Bob', the inspiration for which is Tom Hanks in the film Big.

'He's a grown man who's extremely childlike and views the world thru exuberant and enthusiastic teenage eyes... Loves to wear his favorite Public Enemy shirt, cargo pants and sneakers to work..' Johnson write on his Instagram page alongside of him in character on the set.

'The "added twist" is Bob has an extremely high IQ and is one of the most lethal men on the planet as a C.I.A. contract killer. Oh the most important character trait.. He hates bullies,' the star went on. 

'Can't wait for y'all to meet "Bob". #FightThePower #BeWaterMyFriend #FannyPackYetDeadly CENTRAL INTELLIGENCE. SUMMER 2016.'
