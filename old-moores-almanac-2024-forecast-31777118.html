Old Moore's Almanac 2024 forecast includes horror earthquake and 'Trump assassination warning'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 20, 2024

An astrological almanac has made some predictions for 2024, including “an assassination warning for Trump, the end of cash and the end of roads”.

“A massive earthquake that extends across multiple borders,” is also on the cards for the new year, according to the publishers of Old Moore’s Almanac.

The book has successfully made predictions since 1764, reportedly forecasting several major world events from the coronavirus pandemic, a global recession and Donald Trump losing the White House.

According to Sunday World, the almanac has also predicted for 2024 the election of Ireland’s first female Taoiseach alongside an “embattled” year coming down the tracks for Sinn Féin’s Mary Lou McDonald.

The almanac lists sets of current information, like weather forecasts and tide tables and eerie predictions for the future, with many prophecies seeming to come true. Old Moore’s Almanac is 260 years old in 2024, with some of its earliest copies residing in Trinity College Dublin.

The book is famous for its future-seeing, with the Mirror reporting that the publishers claimed: “These predictions have been getting great domestic and international news coverage for their accuracy. For example, before it happened, Old Moore said that Kate Middleton already had a baby on board (before it was announced) and that the baby’s name would be Charlotte.

2023's guesses also saw Old Moore mention the words "submarine drama", which could have been referring to the tragic Titan submersible implosion over the summer.

“In previous years, Old Moore predicted that Kanye would have mental health problems and that Trump would run for president again. He also predicted the rise of Bitcoin over 10 years ago. And of course, he predicted the pandemic, a year before it happened."

The almanac also says Old Moore predicted there would be “aliens for real” in 2023, which did see a furore in the American government, when David Grusch, a member of a Pentagon office testified there was a covert government programme to recover crashed alien spacecraft.

But what can we look forward to in 2024? Well Old Moore is playing it close to the chest until the new almanac is released but the publishers have let a few key predictions be known. “Old Moore’s Almanac predictions for 2024 include an assassination warning for Trump, the end of cash, the end of roads, and a massive earthquake that extends across multiple borders,” they said.
