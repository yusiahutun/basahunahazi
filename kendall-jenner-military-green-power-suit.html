Kendall Jenner Updates the Power Suit in a Sculptural Blazer

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, February 2, 2024

Kendall Jenner definitely knows a thing or two about suiting up.

While hosting an event to premiere Mercedes-Benz's new electric G-Class on Tuesday, the supermodel showed out in an updated take on the power suit that emulated the best of her signature minimalistic style.

The military-green set consisted of a sculptural double-breasted blazer, complete with rounded structured shoulders and a shapely waist that cinched in at her center. For her bottoms, she wore matching olive-green trousers in a draping straight-leg silhouette, with fabric nearly pooling at the bottom near her feet. Ever the trendsetter, Jenner finished off the look with shoes that have been dominating the street-style scene over the past few seasons: a pair of ’90s-esque square-toe boots, these awash in a dark chocolate hue.

Gilbert Flores for Variety//Getty Images

Jenner is no stranger to the power of a good suit.

In March, she was spotted wearing another monochromatic set while walking around Beverly Hills. That one was a navy blue set from Bottega Veneta, featuring a blazer with a wrap-cinched waist and unique pocket flaps protruding from the center and pleated straight-leg pants. She added shiny black pumps, narrow black rectangular sunglasses, gold earrings, and an orange iteration of Bottega Veneta’s Kalimero Città bag.

Don’t let her affinity for suits fool you—Jenner is just as capable of sartorially embracing her feminine side, too. Take her Easter ensemble, for example. The model rung in the holiday by wearing a dreamy white Rodarte dress with puffed sleeves and a big black bow adorning each shoulder.
