Arnold Schwarzenegger 'taking things slow' with new girlfriend 35 years younger

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Arnold Schwarzenegger is reportedly ‘taking things slow’ with his new 41-year-old girlfriend, Hungarian actress Timea Palacsik. The two share a 35-year age difference.

The 76-year-old Hollywood star was first linked to Timea earlier this month as the pair were spotted sharing a kiss during a bike ride in Venice Beach a few days ago.

Arnold Schwarzenegger ‘really likes’ Timea Palacsik

The pair, who are 35 years apart are said to have met through mutual friends, and although they’re not official yet, sources tell US Weekly that the Terminator actor ‘really likes’ the Wonder Woman actress.

“Arnold is enjoying his life and dating, but he really likes Timea. They aren’t official yet, but his friends see how he is with her and think they will [be] very soon,” claims the insider.

As they come from similar backgrounds, the source tells the publication they’ve “been in the same orbit for a while.”

It looks like the age difference isn’t a problem for the couple, as they also say the Hungarian actress keeps Schwarzenegger “feeling very young.” It’s also said they got along since the moment they met.

 “They’re taking things slowly but are a very adorable couple, and they are always laughing and giggling,” the insider concludes.

The new alleged romance comes two years after Arnold’s divorce was finalized with former wife Maria Shiver, nearly one decade after the paperwork was first filed.

The couple posed together on Instagram months ago

Although they’ve only just been linked it looks like the pair have known each other for a while, as the Nobu restaurant owner took to her Instagram months ago to congratulate Schwarzenegger on his Netflix docuseries.

Taking to her 2.4 million followers, the 41-year-old shared a range of photos of the two, with some animals, which she captioned:

“2017 and 2023. Congratulations @schwarzenegger to your @netflix docuseries [white heart emoji]. Thanks for the special dinner and I was so happy to meet Whiskey & Lulu & Schnelly ( he is a boy not a girl sorry:)) as well.”

The actor’s docuseries was released on the streaming giant in June 2023, and explored Arnold’s life, and highly impressive career, as an athlete, actor, and politician.
