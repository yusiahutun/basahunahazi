Jaime Pressly's Babies Born See the First Pic of the Precious Twins

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, April 1, 2024

Major congratulations are in order! Jaime Pressly welcomed her adorable twin boys Leo and Lenon into the world on Oct. 16, and the first photo of the little cuties will absolutely melt your heart. Jaime and longtime boyfriend Hamzi Hijazi announced the happy news on her Instagram on Oct. 23, joking about how the twins will be "double trouble."

The first photo of the babies shows the wide-eyed infants snuggled up in matching blue striped onesies on a big elephant-print pillow. The bundles of joy look happy and healthy. We're thrilled for the parents! This is their first child together, though Jaime has a son named Dezi Calvo from her former relationship with Eric Calvo.

It was no surprise that Jaime was having twins. Her stomach was absolutely massive leading up to the birth! Three weeks before the babies came, she showed off the bump in a sweet photo of her nursery, which features two matching gray cribs from Pottery Barn, as well as a huge stuffed giraffe and adorable safari animal wall art.

Sadly, it's unlikely that Jaime will be able to breastfeed her twins. She underwent a double mastectomy after finding lumps and dealing with pain beginning in 2007. "When I had my son I got mastitis, but I didn't know because I thought it was just regular breastfeeding pain," she explained on The Talk. "I still have some breast tissue left, just from the mastitis because it mutated into something else because it sat dormant for a while. But it was the craziest thing. Thank God it wasn't [cancer]."
