Therefore, Aiden is rumoured to be the son of Adrian Cypron Kerr, Cleopatra Bernard’s alleged boyfriend.

If it is indeed true that Aiden is not Dwayne Onfroy's son, it would mean that he is XXXTentacion's half-brother.
