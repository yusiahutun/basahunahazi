Who is Pastor TD Jakes?

Bishop Thomas Dexter Jakes, popularly referred to as TD Jakes, is a senior pastor of the Potter's House of Dallas in the United States of America (USA).

According to his profile on his website, he is also an accomplished entrepreneur who has produced multiple blockbuster films and written several books.

The Potter's House of Dallas sits on a 34-acre hilltop campus and boasts a capacity of 30,000 worshippers.

When did TD Jakes establish Potter's House?

The preached founded Potter's House in 1996 with minimal resources.

The non-denominational, multicultural church and global humanitarian organisation has over 50 diverse ministries.

Potter's House has campuses worldwide, including South Africa and Sidney, Australia.

Bishop Jakes primarily derives his income from the annual MegaFest festival, drawing in more than 300,000 worshippers globally. The MegaFest is televised on The Word Network and Lightsource.com, along with various other service initiatives originating from the Potter's House.

TD Jakes Enterprises

TD Jakes Enterprises is a social is a socially impactful holding company with an inventive portfolio deeply rooted in content creation, fostering cultural change, promoting community development, and making strategic capital investments.

Among the entities under TD Jakes Enterprises are Good Soil, Dexterity Media, Dexterity Sounds, Dexterity Collective, Dexterity X and Dexterity Ventures.

What's the value of TD Jakes's mansion?

According to Real Estate Insider, Jakes resides in a lavish $5.1 million (KSh 805.5 million) mansion in Dallas.

He lives near other American celebrities worth millions of dollars.

The home, which measures 10,681 square feet, features five bedrooms and offers a sight to behold showing the pastor's opulence.
