Halle Berry, 56, showcases her incredible physique in string bikini

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, October 9, 2024

Halle Berry left her fans in awe when she posted a new photo of herself showcasing her incredible physique in a tiny string bikini. 

The 56-year-old actress shared the picture on Instagram, donning a brown bikini that highlighted her enviable figure. 

She accessorized the look with a gold kaftan and held a glass of bubbly. 

"Keeping my spirits up," she captioned the post.

This recent post comes just days after Halle shared another snapshot that offered a glimpse into her carefree days, enjoying sun and sand at the beach. 

In the photo, the actress flaunted her toned legs while frolicking by the water, wearing a blue and white patterned cover-up, a straw hat, and what appeared to be a swimsuit underneath.

The ageless star recently opened up about her beauty secrets in an interview with Marie Claire magazine. 

She revealed that her morning routine starts with taking vitamin A-D-K, fish oils, and collagen in her coffee, followed by various supplements to help address gut issues.

Interestingly, the actress confessed that she stopped eating breakfast over 20 years ago. Instead, she breaks her fast at around 2pm every day, listening to her body's cues. 

“I start off my morning with one cup of coffee, and that's all I’ll have until 2pm. And I use hazelnut, like milked hazelnuts instead of any kind of milk, and I do a sugar substitute, and that gets me through,” she told the publication. 

Her fitness routine also plays a significant role in maintaining her stunning figure. 

Halle shared that her workouts vary, with some days focusing on cardiovascular activities, others on light weights, calisthenics, yoga, or Pilates. 

She recently added a Pilates machine to her home gym and loves the variety it brings to her exercise regimen. 

“Sometimes it's that. I just try to mix it up. I think our bodies get used to the same exercises. So for me, it's always been important to do different things all the time,” she added.
