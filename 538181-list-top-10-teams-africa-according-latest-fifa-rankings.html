List of Top 10 Teams in Africa According to Latest FIFA Rankings as Kenya Drops

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 16, 2024
  • The rankings come days after the conclusion of the African Cup of Nations (AFCON)
  • AFCON finalists Cote d'Ivoire and Nigeria moved into the top five, while Morocco remained at the top
  • No African team is in the top 10 of the global ranking, with Argentina remaining at the helm

According to FIFA's latest rankings, the AFCON winner, the Elephant of Ivory Coast, and runners-up Super Eagles of Nigeria moved into the top five, while bronze-winning Bafana Bafana of South Africa moved into the top ten.

Senegal climbed 3 places up to emerge as the 17th-best team in the world and second-best in Africa

3. Nigeria

The highest mover is the Super Eagles, who moved from 42nd to 28th position to become the third-best team in Africa

4. Egypt

Egypt dropped three places in the latest FIFA ranking to number 36 in the world and 4th in Africa

5. Ivory Coast

The current AFCON Champions, Ivory Coast, are ranked as the 39th-best team after moving 10 places

6. Tunisia

Tunisia dropped to 41st from 54th and is ranked as the 6th-best African team

7. Algeria

Algeria is ranked 43rd after dropping 13 places in the latest FIFA rankings

Argentina remains at the top of the world

Globally, World Cup reigning champion Argentina remains at the top of the ranking with the top ten unchanged.

France, England, Belgium and Brazil complete the list of the top 5 men's football teams worldwide respectively.

In that order, others in the top 10 list are the Netherlands, Portugal, Spain, Italy and Croatia.

Kenya drops in latest FIFA rankings

In the East Africa region, Uganda remains the top-ranked at number 92, with Kenya dropping one place from 110 to 111.
