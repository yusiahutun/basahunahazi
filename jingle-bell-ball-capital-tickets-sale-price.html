Capital FM Jingle Bell Ball announces line up with Liam Payne, Rita Ora and tickets are on sale now

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, March 18, 2024

LOOKING for a Christmas party to beat all others this year, then the Capital FM Jingle Bell Ball is the place to be.

Taking place on the 7 and 8 December, Liam Payne, Sam Smith, Rita Ora and more have been booked for the 'UK's Biggest Christmas Party'.

Pretty much anyone who's anyone in pop will be playing the legendary event, with Liam Payne, Rita Ora, Jonas Blue, Sam Smith, Tom Walker, Sigala, Mabel, The Script and more all announced across the weekend.

The huge party will take place at London's O2, so there's more then enough room, but you'll need to be quick if you want to get into this party.

Tickets went on sale at 8am on November 7 from AXS, and you can get them here. So make sure to set your alarms, as this is one you won't want to miss.

Who's playing at Capital's Jungle Bell Ball 2019?

  • 7 December: Liam Payne,  Rita Ora, Jax Jones, Aitch, Lauv, Jonas Blue, Ava Max, Young T & Bugsey - buy here 
  • 8 December: Sam Smith, Tom Walker, Sigala, Mabel, The Script, AJ Tracey, Anne-Marie - buy here

More acts will be announced on Capital FM this week, so keep your eyes peeled and your radio's tuned in if you want to hear more.

Last year was huge, with Little Mix, David Guetta, Liam Payne, and Halsey all taking to the stage to perform for 20,000 fans. This year is sure to be another huge party.

However, fans will need to make sure they buy their tickets from AXS to be able to enter.

Capital FM confirmed on their site that 'Capital’s Jingle Bell Ball tickets are only available from our official ticket agent AXS and mobile ticketing is in operation for this event. Please do not buy tickets from any other ticketing sites as any unauthorised tickets bought from resale sites such as Viagogo or StubHub will not be valid and you will not be permitted entry to the event.
'Tickets can only be purchased from the official ticket agent AXS or via AXS Official Resale, which provides fans who are no longer able to attend the event with the option of reselling their tickets at face value to other fans (with a cap of no more than 10% above the price originally paid)'.
