Murugi Munyi Shares Fun Moments with Family During Vacation in Mombasa: &quot;Enjoying This One&quot;

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 25, 2024
  • Podcaster Murugi Munyi showed off the fancy lifestyle and experience she is enjoying in Mombasa with her family
  • The delighted mother shared fun and memorable moments on their second day in the coastal region of Kenya
  • She flaunted the kind of food they ate, the accommodation services they enjoyed, swimming, the beach and so much more

As the year 2022 comes to a close, some people are still enjoying the festivities and holidays with their loved ones.

Content creator Murugi Munyi is among the people who are enjoying soft life before they usher in a new year.

The YouTuber is currently in Mombasa where she is spending quality time with her loved ones.

Family fun in Mombasa

She recently shared some memorable moments in a clip while enjoying soft life in the coastal area of Kenya.

Vincent Mboya gives mum KSh 100,000

Earlier this month, Vincent Mboya's mum broke down after he gave her his first salary worth KSh 100,000 and a paid vacation.

Mboya rose to fame after Lang'ata MP offered to help him with KSh 18,000 rent, but he later offered to return it and gave him 48 hours to collect his money after publicly sharing that he helped him.

YouTube subscribers

His consistency in content creation, catapulted by hard work, saw him gain over 110,000 subscribers in less than a year.

In his recent YouTube video, Mboya treated his mother to a lifetime surprise after he showed up in Mombasa and, in front of family and church leaders, gave her KSh 100,000.

"I have decided to bring my first salary, nipatie mama yangu all of it. Si mingi sana, it's just around KSh 100,000. Afanye kitu anataka kufanya nayo," he said.
