Lottie Moss strips topless for thermometer-bursting snap as she parades killer curves

Lottie Moss left fans picking their jaws up off the floor as she stripped topless for a sultry outdoor snap.

Risking a major wardrobe malfunction with the string ties hoisted high up on her waist, Lottie’s multitude of delicate tattoos were visible to the lens, including a Cupid sitting on a cloud, a pair of cherries and the words ‘Not Yours’ written in cursive.
