Who Is Stormi Bree, Joe Jonas's Rumored New Flame?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, October 3, 2024

Amid his divorce from Sophie Turner, Joe Jonas sparked romance rumors when he was spotted leaving Los Cabos, Mexico, with model Stormi Bree Henley (who goes by Stormi Bree) by his side. Per multiple reports, the two appeared relaxed while walking to their private jet, and that same day, Jonas posted a video of the beach to Instagram featuring a romantic song playing in the background.

Here’s what we know about Jonas’s rumored new girlfriend.

Ella Hovsepian/Robert Smith/Patrick McMullan via Getty Images//Getty Images

They’ve confirmed their romance.

Over the weekend of March 2, the two were spotted sharing a kiss at the Bondi Bowling Club in Sydney, Australia (where the Jonas Brothers were on the latest leg of their world tour), during an outing with Jonas’s younger brother, Nick, and other friends.

Joe wore a colorful graphic shirt, khaki shorts, and an abstract camo baseball hat, while the model wore a breezy little black dress, black knee-high boots, and dangly gold earrings.

Splash News

While Jonas and Henley have been spotted together on several other occasions, one of the strongest indicators of their relationship so far was when they both shared posts in late February from a boat ride in the Sydney Harbour.

On his Instagram Story, Jonas posted a video of a seaplane gliding across the water right next to their boat. The same day, Henley posted a slideshow that saw her lounging on a boat in a black bikini and a netlike cover-up. She also shared a video from the boat ride the day prior, and the clip looked almost identical to one Jonas’s younger brother shared as well.

The lovebirds were careful, though, not to tag each other in their respective posts.

Joe Jonas via Instagram//Instagram

She was crowned Miss Teen USA in 2009.

As a teen, Henley grew up competing in American beauty pageants. She was crowned Miss Tennessee Teen USA in 2008, and then became Miss Teen USA in 2009. When she won, it was her first time ever in the national competition.

At the time, she told Seventeen, “It’s surreal. I’m still waiting to wake up…I felt like I’ve come a long way and I’ve tried so hard for this. I just wanna tell everybody out there to try their hardest, ’cause they can do it.”

She auditioned for American Idol.

After winning Miss Teen USA, Henley had dreams of becoming a country music star. She auditioned for season 10 of American Idol, singing “Father Can You Hear Me” from Diary of a Mad Black Woman for judges Steven Tyler, Jennifer Lopez, and Randy Jackson. But while she did move on to the next round, Henley was eliminated during the first phase of Hollywood Week.

Her YouTube page features three original songs.

Although she has never released an official studio album, Henley has shared three different music videos on her official YouTube page, which in total have received over 200,000 views.

In 2018, Henley shared the first of the three, for a song titled “Intangible.” By this time, the pageant winner seems to have ditched her country music aspirations to focus on a more experimental sound.

Henley then released visuals for tracks titled “Northern Home” and “Melt,” in 2021 and 2022, respectively.

Oh, she also has a couple of directing credits.

Not only does Henley sing, she also has a few directing credits to her name.

The “Intangible” credits listed on YouTube note Henley’s contributions: “Visual by Stormi, written by Stormi and Saro, produced by Stormi and Rex Kudo.”

And the credits for “Melt” say Henley produced the song and directed the visuals as well.

She has appeared on Brooklyn Nine-Nine and in short films.

As if all that weren’t enough, Henley also has a few acting credits to her name.

Most notably, she appeared in a season-three episode of Brooklyn Nine-Nine, “Boyle’s Hunch.” Henley plays Dvora, a love-stricken assistant to an art gallerist. Dvora steals a piece of artwork to prove her devotion to her boss.

Besides this role, Henley also appeared in the short films Midland (2015) and Shiny (2016), according to her IMDb page. She has also done cameos in the videos for two tracks by musician Saro: “Sardonic” and “Boy Afraid.” She played Sara in 2016 sci-fi film 2307: Winter’s Dream, and Summer in 2021 comedy Hot Water.

She has a daughter named Gravity.

Before linking up with Jonas, Henley was in a relationship with fellow model Lucky Blue Smith. They have a daughter together, Gravity Blue Smith, born in July 2017.

At the time, father Lucky shared a gushing post on Instagram, which read, “She’s here😍😍😍 meet Gravity Blue Smith, I’ve never felt so happy... I love you more than anything in this whole world little princess❤️.”

Henley also shared a message: “Welcome to the world little angel, the most perfect beautiful gift I could have ever dreamed of.”
