Who is Joe Rogan?

Joe Rogan is an American podcaster with over $150 million net worth as of 2023. He has worked as a comedian, UFC colour commentator, and more. Joe’s television career flourished over the years, as he worked in various capacities, starting in 1994 when he hosted the show Half Time Comedy Hour.

In his The Joe Regan Experience podcast, a massive hit today, Joe has interesting conversations with guests on fascinating topics and genres like politics, science, humour, comedy, and philosophy.

Joe co-founded the fitness company Onnit which was sold to Unilever in 2021. He frequently advertises the company’s products on his podcast.

Rogan is married to Jessica Ditzel, a former cocktail waitress. They have two daughters, born in 2008 and 2010. Joe’s family lives in a $14 million home in the United States of America on Lake Austin, Austin, Texas.

The story behind Joe Rogan's Tattoos

Does Joe Rogan have tattoos? It is already established that he has; the big question is, “How many tattoos does he have, and where are they located?” The podcast host has two significant tattoos on his arms, which look magnificent. What do they mean?

Tattoo on Joe Rogan's right arm

Joe Rogan has a Miyamoto Musashi tattoo on his right arm. Joe got the tattoo of the renowned Japanese samurai after reading The Book of Five Rings at the age of 16.

During one of his podcast sessions on his show, Joe spoke about the inspiration behind the forearm tattoo that extends towards his elbow.

My right arm, I have a tattoo of Miyamoto Musashi because I read a quote when I was 16 years old and doing martial arts... I read The Book of Five Rings, and he wrote this book, which is a book on strategy. Miyamoto Musashi was a samurai who killed 62 men in one-on-one combat and wrote this book about it.
