Who is Lawrence O’Donnell?

Lawrence O’Donnell is a famous American television anchor with a strong presence in the multi-media industry. On top of being an anchor, he is an actor, liberal political commentator, writer (is a writer and producer for the NBC series The West Wing), and executive producer.

O’Donnell started his career in 1977 as an author, composing several books. He became a legislative aide to Senator Daniel Patrick Moynihan from 1989 to 1995. O'Donnell started hosting a 10:00 om show on MSNBC called The Last Word with Lawrence O'Donnell on September 27, 2010.
