List of logistics companies in Kenya and their contacts

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, March 19, 2024

Logistics companies in Kenya move goods around the country and globally. Therefore, they play a crucial role in Kenya's economy. It is impossible to conduct functions like manufacturing, farming, and selling items to people without moving goods.

Kenya has different types of logistics companies. They range from giant multinational corporations like Maersk Kenya Limited to startups like Twiga Foods and Glovo. Also, the country has freight forwarders that work well with big and small logistics companies to ensure goods reach the customers on time.

Best logistics companies in Kenya
