Bella Hadid Wears a Sunset-Printed Bikini and Belly Chain in Ibiza

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Bella Hadid has taken her love of '90s and '00s fashion to a new level.

The model shared a series of pics on Instagram of herself rocking a very Y2K outfit while on vacation in Ibiza yesterday. In the photos, Hadid poses in a PRISCAVera "Sunset" triangle bikini top and ruched skirt, while sitting on stone steps. Hadid also wears black platform thong sandals and Veneda Carter x Flatlist Eyewear tortoise sunglasses, with her hair in pigtail braids and a patterned bandana atop her head.

Hadid also showed off her accessories in a series of close ups. The throwback jewelry includes a gold belly chain with discs, a thick gold and emerald bangle, a gold and ruby necklace, and large hoops with her name in script.

The 24-year-old also added some edge to the look with dainty, '90s inspired temporary tattoos by INKED by Dani. The temporary tatts include a classic butterfly on her chest and a series of three dots under her belly button.

Hadid also posted throwback pics from an earlier beach vacation last week, which featured a new hint at the model's love life. The slideshow, which included snaps of Hadid eating pizza on the beach while wearing a bikini, ended with a pic of Hadid kissing her rumored boyfriend, Marc Kalman, on the cheek with a white towel wrapped around her head.

Hadid first went public with the art director last month, posting a similar photo dump which included a photo of the pair looking close. Page Six reported at the time that Hadid and Kalman had been secretly seeing each other for a year while avoiding paparazzi. "They basically came out on their anniversary — or somewhere around it," a source told the outlet.
