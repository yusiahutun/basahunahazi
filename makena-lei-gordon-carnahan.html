Makena Lei Gordon Carnahan

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

You might have heard about a lot of personalities who gain fame after getting born into a celebrity family. Today’s topic is also related to the same category. Makena Lei Gordon Carnahan grabbed attention as the daughter of the American actress, Helen Hunt, and the American producer, Matthew Carnahan.

The 15 years old, Makena undoubtedly enjoys a lavish life as her mother has accumulated a huge net worth of $55 Million. Many of her well-wishers are waiting to find out more detail about Makena. If you are one of them, then stay tuned with us and keep reading until the end.

Born With a Silver Spoon In Mouth

Makena Lei Gordon Carnahan was born with a silver spoon in her mouth. Her mother, Helen Hunt is an American actress, director, and screenwriter who is currently 56 years old. She is also recognized as the winner of three Golden Globe Awards. Some of her films include Twister (1996), Cast Away (2000), Soul Surfer (2011), The Miracle Season (2018), and so on.

Whereas, Makena’s father, Matthew Carnahan established his career as an American producer, writer, and director who is currently 58 years old. He is best known for his work in an American docudrama TV miniseries, Valley of the Boom.

Split of Parents

As we prior mentioned, Makena Lei was born to her parents, Helen Hunt and Matthew Carnahan. However, the couple never married each other. The former couple started their relationship in 2001. After three years of being in a relationship, the couple welcomed Makena in May 2004.

Before that, the ex-couple had already become the parents of their son, Emmett Carnahan in 1998. After sharing over one and a half decades of relationship, the duo finally separated their ways in August 2017. Though the couple is not together at present, they both are taking care of their children.

Past Marriage of Mother

Prior to being in a relationship with Matthew, Makena’s mother, Helen Hunt was married to her long-term boyfriend turned husband, Hank Azaria in 1994. Her ex-husband, Azaria is widely famous as an American actor, voice actor, singer, and comedian.

After dating for a long, the former couple finally exchanged their wedding vows in a traditional Jewish ceremony. Their marriage ceremony was held at nearby their home in Southern California on 17 July 1999. Also, the ex-pair has appeared together in Mad About You and an episode of The Simpsons. After a year of their marriage, the duo separated their ways due to some irreconcilable differences on 18 December 2000.

Currently, Hank is married to Katie Wright, a former American actress. The duo also shares a son named Hal, born in 2009. Currently, Hank resides in New York with his family.

Half-Brother, Emmett Carnahan

Makena Lei grew up along with her half-brother, Emmett Carnahan. Before her birth, Emmett was already six years old. Nevertheless, he is currently 21 years old. Emmett is popular as an American actor, famous for his roles in The Miracle Season and House of Lies.

Enjoys $95 Million of Parents

The famous star, Makena Lei Gordon lives a lavish life. She is still too small to get involved in any sort of profession. Though she is currently at an early age, she enjoys the huge net worth of her parents.

Her mother, Helen has an estimated capital of $75 Million as per the Celebrity Net Worth. On the other hand, her father, Matthew Carnahan accumulated assets of $20 Million as per the same website. Therefore, she is spending a rich life with no doubt.
