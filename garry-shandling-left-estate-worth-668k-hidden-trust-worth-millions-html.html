Garry Shandling left behind estate worth only $668K

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, April 11, 2024

He died back in 2016 at the age of 66.

And finally details about Garry Shandling's assets are being revealed via documents obtained by TMZ.

According to the outlet, the massively successful comedian and talk show host left behind an estate worth only $668,636.

Of course that seems like a relatively small amount given Shandling's long and decidedly very successful career.

Aside from touring as a stand up comedian, and staring in a multitude of movies, Shandling also enjoyed heading up two successful television programs: The Larry Sanders Show and It's Garry Shandling's Show.

The unexpectedly paltry estate was left with his lawyer and long-time friend Bill Isaacson.

While the amount seems low, it seems that Isaacson is also a trustee of a mysterious trust set up by Shandling that's hidden from public view.

It is likely that the 'hidden' trust in fact houses the bulk of the entertainer's assets.  

Shandling died as a result of pulmonary thrombosis according to his autopsy report.

The legendary comedian was killed when a blood clot traveled from his leg to his lung, and the cause of death has been listed as both 'natural' and an 'accident.'

He also had an enlarged heart and had Xanax, oxycodone and hydrocodone in his system as well at the time of his death according to the report.

TMZ was the first to obtain the actor's autopsy report.
