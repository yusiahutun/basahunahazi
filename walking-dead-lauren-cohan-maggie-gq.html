&quot;Walking Dead&quot; Star Lauren Cohan Gets Sexy Makeover for GQ

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, January 1, 0001

Maggie got a makeover!

"The Walking Dead" star Lauren Cohan shows us what's underneath all the dirt, grime, sweat-covered clothes and messy hair she usually sports for the hit AMC series in a hot new photo shoot for GQ.

The British actress -- who plays Southern on the zombie-packed show -- looks drop dead gorgeous in an animal print Dolce & Gabbana bodysuit.

In another photo, she smolders in a black Cushnie et Ochs dress with a cleavage cutout and slit up the waist.

While she's clearly stunning either way, we love seeing the stars of "The Walking Dead" out of their zombie apocalypse outerwear!

"I'd always fantasized about playing a southern role," Cohan tells the mag. "I definitely had this idea she'd be soooo southern. I had to dial that in slightly."

The new season of "TWD" premieres October 12 on AMC.
