Miguel Gomez's biography

The rapper was born in Santiago de Cali, Colombia, on August 20, 1985. He later moved to Houston, Texas, the USA, where he grew up with his family. Miguel has not shared any information about his parents and siblings. He went to Clements High School (Sugar Land, TX), and graduated in 2003.

Rapping career

The 36-year old star started rapping under Aztek Escobar. He was the first artist signed by Jay-Z's Roc La Familia Label. However, Miguel never released any album on the label.

Aztek Escobar had a debut album, Colombian Necktie, but the label was dissolved before the album was released. The rapper has produced the following mixtapes:

  • King Of Kings (2005)
  • Rise To Power (2006)
  • Blood In Blood Out (2007)
  • I Swear To God (2009)

Miguel owns two remixes. The first remix was Draped up, which was released in 2007. In the same year, he released a Bun B remix featuring:

  • Lil' Keke
  • Slim Thug
  • Chamillionaire
  • Paul Wall
  • Mike Jones
  • Aztek
  • Lil' Flip
  • Z-Ro

He also played a minor role in the music video for Soul Survivor by rapper Young Jeezy in 2005.

Acting career

Gomez started his acting career in the Miami episode of the FX comedy series Louie in 2012. The same year saw the actor take the role of Jake in The Domino Effect TV series. In 2013, he played Eugene in Bless Me Ultima.
