List of internet service providers in Kenya and their rates 2023

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 2, 2024

Can you imagine a world without Internet connectivity? A significant percentage of the current global population relies on the Internet to connect with other people and conduct businesses. There are various Internet service providers in Kenya today. Kenyans normally choose a provider based on coverage, stability, and reliability.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

Internet connectivity is needed to work, study, and connect with the world. Internet service providers in Kenya ensure that customers do these things by making sure they can access the Internet. They also route online traffic, resolve domain names, and maintain the network infrastructure needed for connectivity.

List of Internet service providers in Kenya in 2023

In contemporary Kenya, there are many Internet service providers. Some have national coverage, while others are limited to specific locations.

The prices they charge for connectivity depend on the speed, location, and number of users. Read on to see the ISP options available in the country.
