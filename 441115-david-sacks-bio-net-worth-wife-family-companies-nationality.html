David Sacks biography

David was born on 25th May 1972 in Cape Town, South Africa. He is 49 years old as of 2022. When he was only five years old, his parents relocated from South Africa to Tennessee, United States. He has not offered any information about his parents and siblings' names and whereabouts.

In the United States, he joined Memphis University School in Memphis, Tennessee, before joining Stanford University. He earned his Bachelor of Arts in economics at the university in 1994. He received a J.D. from the University of Chicago Law School four years later.

Geni.com

David founded Geni.com in 2006, a genealogy website that enabled family members to build an online family tree collaboratively. At the company, he aimed at gaining more visibility on everything taking place there. As a result, he created a tool for the employees to share information among themselves. In 2012, Geni.com was acquired by MyHeritage.

Yammer

In 2008, David changed the internal communication tool at Geni.com into a standalone company under the name, Yammer. The first Enterprise Social Network was launched in 2008 to offer a secure solution for all internal corporate communication and collaborations.

Yammer app became one of the fastest-growing Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) companies in history. It exceeded eight million enterprise users in just four years. In July 2012, Yammer was acquired by Microsoft for $1.2 billion.

Zenefits

David made a significant investment in Zenefits. In January 2016, the board appointed him as an interim CEO during a regulatory crisis. He negotiated deals with insurance regulators, ending the company's licensing compliance crisis. Former Ooyala CEO Jay Fulcher succeeded him.
