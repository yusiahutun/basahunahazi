Gilda’s Club will host a free family event for any family impacted by cancer, according to a news release. An Easter Egg hunt will be 10 a.m. until noon Saturday, March 23, at the Gilda’s Club Quad Cities Davenport Clubhouse, 1351 W. Central Park Ave. Suite 200, Davenport.

The event also will feature a craft for all ages, games, and a group of therapy dogs to help sniff out egg. Kids, teens, grandchildren, friends, and egg hunt enthusiasts are all welcome. Participants do not need to be a registered Gilda’s Club participant. Kids and teens can bring friends.

Registration is required. Spots are limited. For details and to reserve your family’s spot, call Gilda’s Club at 563-326-7504, email program@gildasclubqc.org or sign up online here.
