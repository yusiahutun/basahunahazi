The 10 Wackiest Country Music Album Covers, Ranked

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 6, 2024

The country music genre has created some truly iconic album artwork over the years. Look to either modern or classic country music, and you'll find some of the most recognizable album covers of any genre.

But hey, they can't all be winners.

When it comes to album cover art, country music has also produced some truly spectacular duds, and this list is dedicated to those projects. Whether the cover art was unintentionally terrifying, distractingly wacky or just plain cheesy, you'll find some of the greatest misses in country music album cover art history here.

In this countdown, you'll find Garth Brooks celebrating Christmas with an off-puttingly somber face. You'll also find a nude man wearing only a pile of banjos, a headless guitarist and what just might be the world's longest mullet.

As for Tim McGraw's submission? Even his own daughter couldn't help but poke fun at it, almost three decades later.

Keep scrolling for the all-time wackiest and weirdest album covers in country music history. If we forgot your favorite chuckle-worthy cover, be sure to chime in and let us know.
