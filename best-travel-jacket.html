13 Travel Jackets for Your Next Getaway

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, February 29, 2024

1

Best Lightweight Quilted Jacket

Burberry Grosgrain-Trimmed Quilted Shell Jacket

1

Best Lightweight Quilted Jacket

Burberry Grosgrain-Trimmed Quilted Shell Jacket

Credit: Net-a-Porter

For a long flight or bus ride, you’ll want a soft, squishy jacket that can double as a pillow if the need arises. This quilted jacket can be easily packed, layered, or worn solo depending on the temperature.

Sizes: XS–XL

Materials: 100% polyamide

Best Denim Jacket for Travel

Blazé Milano Sahariana Nariida Maya

2

Best Denim Jacket for Travel

Blazé Milano Sahariana Nariida Maya

Credit: Blaze Milano

For travel, Pinardi suggests a denim blazer with a matching pair of jeans for a comfortable and put-together look. Of this blazer, she says, “It fits for every single occasion, from a dinner out with friends with a more elegant look to running errands with leggings and a pair of sneakers.”

Sizes: 00–IV

Materials: 100% cotton

Best Oversize Travel Jacket

To avoid paying fees for overweight luggage, you may occasionally need to pile on all the layers you can. This oversize style from the Row is not only soft and comfortable, but also big enough to fit a few items underneath.

Sizes: XS–L

Materials: 95% wool, 5% cashmere

Best Convertible Travel Jacket

Best Leather Travel Jacket

8

Best Leather Travel Jacket

Reformation Veda Allen Jacket

A leather jacket is a wardrobe essential, especially when you’re traveling. It’s tough enough to handle whatever wear and tear your day might have in store. Once at your destination, it’s a style you can break out for daytime strolls or nights out.

Materials: Leather

9

Best Classic Travel Jacket

Barbour Beadnell Waxed Cotton Jacket

9

Best Classic Travel Jacket

Barbour Beadnell Waxed Cotton Jacket

Credit: Nordstrom

British label Barbour is known for making workhorse jackets for outdoor activities. This wax-coated cotton jacket is a mid-weight style that you can layer with a down jacket or sweater, depending on the weather.

Sizes: 0–18

Materials: 100% cotton with waxed coating

Reviewers say: “I was waiting to write a review on this jacket until after I got back from my yearly autumnal visit to New York. Now that I’ve returned, I can say this jacket was perhaps one of my best investments yet. The temperature ranged from 45-65 and we experienced rain and sun alike. I wore this jacket during nature hikes in the rain and windy walks on the beach. It performed outstandingly well.”

Best Lightweight Travel Jacket

13

Best Lightweight Travel Jacket

Alex Mill Mel Jacket

Credit: Alex Mill

New York–based label Alex Mill is known for practical pieces with a slightly preppy twist. This cotton jacket is relatively lightweight, making it easy to throw in your bag or over your shoulders if you’re not immediately in need of putting it on.
