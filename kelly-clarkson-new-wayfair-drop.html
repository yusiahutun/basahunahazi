The Kelly Clarkson Home Wayfair Line Just Dropped Stunning New Pieces

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

Well, folks, Kelly Clarkson has done it again. Since launching her home furniture line with Wayfair almost 2 years ago, she's stunned us with every release, and this newest drop is no exception.

The line, dubbed Kelly Clarkson Home, carries a variety of home furnishings and décor, from rugs and outdoor sets to sectionals and bed frames in a style that's a perfect blend of French-inspired patina and pattern with relaxed country motifs—AKA, perfect for us. Kelly recently revealed that new pieces have arrived, and fans are thrilled. "I’m so excited to show y'all the pieces I just added to my @wayfair collection," she wrote on Instagram.

In another post, Kelly showed off the pieces in her line that are all about getting organized. "Okay, y’all! Now that we’re a few weeks into 2022... I gotta ask. Who else is still organizing? 🙋‍♀️ I love @wayfair because they have super functional pieces that help me stay on top of things, but still feel like me. Their products make it all easier, whether I’m hosting or picking up after the kids."

In addition to the gorgeous, the collaboration with Wayfair also leaves us with no doubts about the quality of everything in the line. Much of the furniture is solid wood and most items have 4-5 star ratings from customers.
