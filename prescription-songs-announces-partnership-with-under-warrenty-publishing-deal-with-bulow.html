Prescription Songs Announces Partnership With Under Warrenty &amp; Publishing Deal With blow

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 22, 2024

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) – Prescription Songs, a leading independent publishing company, in conjunction with hit songwriter Emily Warren’s publishing arm, Under Warrenty, is thrilled to announce the recent signing partnership with artist-songwriter bülow. Identified for inclusion in the Rx roster by Prescription Songs’ Head of A&R, West Coast, Rhea Pasricha, bülow has achieved over 1 billion streams and garnered global critical acclaim. Notably, bülow contributed as a co-writer on Beyoncé’s “TEXAS HOLD ‘EM.”

Named the Juno Breakthrough Artist of the Year in 2019, bülow has honed her intuition in songwriting. Besides releasing her own music and embarking on tours, she has crafted several hits for others, including Beyoncé’s “TEXAS HOLD ‘EM” from her latest album, Cowboy Carter, currently topping Billboard’s Top 200 albums chart for the second consecutive week. Additionally, bülow has songwriting credits on Charli XCX’s “Yuck,” Leah Kate’s “Monster,” and Nessa Barrett’s “la di die” and “madhouse.”

In her personal projects, bülow blends alternative pop with astute observations, intimate revelations, and a pioneering spirit underscored by live instrumentation. Having spent her formative years in Germany before relocating to England with her family, she commenced busking at the age of 11. Her journey has taken her from Holland to Texas to Toronto, infusing her songwriting with a musical wanderlust. bülow has released five EPs, including 2022’s Booty Call, and collaborated with a diverse range of artists, from Ty Dolla $ign to Mura Masa.

“I’m immensely excited to embark on this journey with Emily (Under Warrenty) and Prescription,” bülow expresses. “Countless serendipitous moments have brought us together naturally. I respect this team and their capability to represent such an incredible roster while maintaining attentiveness and kindness. I eagerly anticipate joining forces as I continue to develop my music and collaborate with fellow artists.”

Warren, fellow hit songwriter at Prescription Songs, shares bülow’s enthusiasm for the partnership. “Collaborating with Megan, aka bülow, encapsulates everything I find exhilarating about music. I’ve been a fan of hers for a long time — her songs are among my all-time favorites. Once we began writing together, everything clicked. Among her many admirable qualities is her unapologetic authenticity; she has been in the industry for some time and remains deeply connected to her creativity and artistic expression. That level of authenticity is exceedingly rare and incredibly inspiring. I’m thrilled to be part of the support system around her and excited to witness her continued evolution.”

Prescription Songs’ Pasricha adds, “I’m incredibly honored to collaborate with Emily Warren/Under Warrenty in signing bülow to Prescription Songs. We pride ourselves on signing talent that we genuinely admire first and foremost — and I’ve been a fan of bülow and her music for years, ever since Emily first introduced me back in 2017. It’s a gratifying full-circle moment to work together officially, and what better way to commence this partnership than by releasing a boundary-pushing single like Beyoncé’s ‘TEXAS HOLD ‘EM.’ I’m thrilled to be part of bülow’s next career chapter, and undoubtedly, this is just the beginning of an exciting journey ahead.”
