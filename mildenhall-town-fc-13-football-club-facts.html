The History of Mildenhall Town FC

Mildenhall Town FC, also known as “The Hall,” is a well-established football club based in Mildenhall, Suffolk, England. The club was founded in 1876 and has a rich history that spans over a century. Over the years, Mildenhall Town FC has become a beloved institution in the local community, attracting loyal fans and producing talented players.

The Club’s Home Ground

Mildenhall Town FC plays their home matches at Recreation Way, a stadium located in Mildenhall. The ground has a capacity of 1,500 spectators and provides a fantastic atmosphere for both players and fans. The club takes great pride in their home ground and maintains it to ensure an excellent playing surface.

Success in Non-League Football

Mildenhall Town FC has had notable success in non-league football competitions. The club has competed in various leagues, including the Eastern Counties League and the Thurlow Nunn League.
