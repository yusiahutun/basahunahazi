43 Best dressed celebrities in April 2024: Kylie Minogue, Zendaya, Jennifer Aniston &amp; more

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, April 29, 2024

Coachella is sure to bring a flurry of enviable looks from the style set this month

    Enter April, a new month of opportunities for stylish looks and fashionable fits from the best-dressed stars. 

    Kicking off the month, the iHeartRadio Music Awards brought together some of the biggest names in music and delivered a red carpet of sartorial splendour on 1 April. 

    Also on the agenda this month, Coachella is sure to deliver some desert-friendly fashion from the style set, with everything from crochet to crystal-adorned jumpsuits making their way to the California desert in the past (Harry Styles).

    Da'Vine Joy Randolph

    Oscar winner Da'Vine Joy Randolph oozed glamour at the Time100 Gala in her custom Elisabetta Franchi gown.

    The champagne-hued dress featured sparkling embellishments, an asymmetrical neckline, and a floor-sweeping train.

    Kylie Minogue

    Kylie Minogue pulled out all the stops for the Time100 Gala, opting for a black and silver gown by Elie Saab.

    The beautiful dress featured cut-outs across the chest and shoulders, highlighting Kylie's toned arms. The black skirt skimmed over her petite figure and flashed a hint of leg with a thigh-high slit - a style we know Kylie adores!

    Zendaya

    Zendaya's Challengers promotional wardrobe continues to wow, with this tennis whites inspired outfits showcasing her slender frame.

    Zendaya

    Zendaya's tennis-ball green suit spotted in Midtown, NYC, will go down as yet another ace sartorial win for her stylist, Law Roach. 

    Victoria Beckham

    Victoria Beckham was an ethereal goddess in a sheer, cloud blue chiffon dress to mark her 50th birthday. 

    The fashion designer's star-studded birthday bash brought together her family and closest friends, and even gave fans an incredibly rare Spice Girls reunion.

    Alicia Keys

    Alicia Keys turned heads in double denim at the premire of 'Hell's Kitchen' on Broadway on Saturday 20 April. 

    Salma Hayek

    Salma Hayek exuded mermaid vibes in a scintillating strapless gown covered in blue sequins. The actress put on a stylish display when she arrived at the Fondazione Cini, Isola Di San Giorgio dinner gala during the 60th Biennale Art 2024 fair in Venice.

    The 57-year-old beauty was accompanied by her husband Francois-Henri Pinault and her model stepdaughter Mathilde.

    Zoe Saldana

    Striking a pose for the cameras, Zoe Saldana looked amazing in a black strapless gown. The glittering design featured a structured bodice and was perfectly paired with satin pumps with statement strap detailing and a coordinating clutch bag.

    Zoe kept her beauty blend fresh and natural and added a pop of colour to her look thanks to scarlet nail varnish. 

    Zendaya

    Serving up tennis fashion like a total pro as she continues her epic sports-themed press tour, Zendaya was radiant in a luminous lime number. 

    Theactress looked sensational when she arrived at the Los Angeles premiere after-party at Funke wearing a tennis-ball inspired halterneck dress with a plunging neckline that came down to her navel, courtesy of Celia Kritharioti Couture.

    Ellie Goulding

    Ellie Goulding looked like she had stepped out of modern adaptation of 'Men In Black' at the 2024 Playing for Change Impact Awards at the Rubell Museum in Miami Florida. 

    The British singer, who is passionate about environmental sustainablity, layered a satin dinner jacket over a boxy white shirt and structured black briefs. 

    Jennifer Aniston

    Jennifer Aniston, 55, looked pristine in this waist-cinching all-black ensemble she wore to the PaleyFest LA screening of at the Dolby Theatre.

    The star teamed black cigarette pants with a sleeveless turtleneck and peep-toe heels.

    Nicola Coughlan

    Nicola Coughlan, 37, made an appearance at the star-studded  Bridgerton season three photocall in London looking sensational in this black satin midi dress which was enhanced with an incredible gold chest-plate that went down to her thighs.

    She styled the showstopping look with platformed black heels and a bold chunky gold chain necklace.

    Rihanna

    Rihanna proved that the mob wife trend that has taken TikTok by storm shows no sign of slowing down. 

    The 'Umbrella' singer, 36, was spotted leaving The Nice Guy, a restaurant in Los Angeles wearing a leopard print mini skirt and fur jacket to match her furry animal print bag, all of which was teamed with a corset top and huge sunnies.

    Reese Witherspoon

    Reese Witherspoon, 48, looked so chic when she stepped out for the PaleyFest LA screening of at the Dolby Theatre. 

    The actress wore a figure-skimming simple black midi dress with a pair of strappy stilettos and chunky gold hoop earrings.

    Mariah Carey

    The 'Hero' singer, 55, looked sensational as she performed at the grand opening of her new show, Mariah Carey: The Celebration of Mimi Live, at Dolby Live in Vegas. 

    Mariah wowed in an array of outfits including this dipped-hem gold gown in a high-shine material. The garment had a corseted top and was paired with sheer rhinestone-adorned fishnets and Hollywood curls.

    Lupita Nyong'o

    Looking sensational in sparkles, Lupita Nyong'o lit up the red carpet at the CinemaCon Big Screen Achievement Awards at Omnia Nightclub at Caesars Palace.

    Lupita nailed the Hollywood glamour brief in a gorgeous metallic gown with delicate spaghetti straps and a low, scooped back. She completed her stellar look with shimmering drop earrings and a slick of plum-hued gloss.

    Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

    Rosie Huntinton-Whiteley graced the red carpet at Tiffany & Co.'s opening celebration for the House's 'Tiffany Wonder' exhibition at the TOKYO NODE gallery in Japan. 

    The statuesque beauty looked ethereal in a white high-neck dress with a strapless design and a sheer, tiered skirt. The bridal-inspired look was complemented thanks to sparkling Tiffany earrings and a glittering brooch pinned to her breast.

    Hunter Schafer

    Hunter Schafer was a vision in a Monet-inspired slip dress at the 2nd Annual GQ Global Creativity Awards in NYC.

    The actress looked like a painting in her abstract print dress and matching heels, wearing her long copper tresses in a sleek style with a dramatic side-party. Keeping her jewellery subtle and her beauty blend natural, Hunter gets top marks from us.

    Chrissy Teigen

    Chrissy Teigen proved that leopard print is making a comeback as she graced the red carpet at the 2024 City Harvest Gala in a sheer animal print gown adorned with shoulder bows.

    Paris Hilton

    Paris Hilton schooled us in experimental fashion as she rocked a deconstructed cage dress at the Fashion Trust U.S. 2024 Awards.

    Karlie Kloss

    Karlie Kloss was elegance personified in monochromatic olive as she stepped out in NYC on April 10. 

    Rosamund Pike

    Saltburn's Rosamund Pike single-handedly proved that Barbiecore is not dead in New York City this month.

    Ariana Grande

    Ariana Grande leaned into whimsical glamour as she chanelled her counterpart, Glinda, during the Universal Pictures and Focus Features Presentation during CinemaCon 2024.

    Zendaya

    If the dressing for the US Open was a competitive sport, Zendaya would have won gold a long time ago. The actress embraced all things 'Game, Set, Match' in custom tennis ball heels for the press call of her new movie in Rome.

    Demi Moore

    Demi Moore stopped us in our tracks when she stepped out in Milan, Italy, wearing a shimmering slip dress for the Dolce & Gabbana 40th Anniversary Exhibition. 

    Demi showed off her feminine silhouette in the ultra-sheer dress, revealing a set of black lingerie beneath her glittering chainmail gown.

    Lupita Nyong'o

    Lupita Nyong'o proved she's the diamond of the season in a glittering co-ord and mesh detailing at at the Dolce&Gabbana 40th Anniversary party in Milan, Italy.

    Lea Michele

    Lea Michele looked radiant in a red halterneck gown as she cradled her baby bump.

    The star, who recently announced she is expecting her second child with husband Zandy Reich, was the picture of elegance on the red carpet at the 2024 Hope Rising Benefit in New York City.


    Billy Porter

    Billy Porter was serving up some serious fashion at the Williams Institute Legacy Gala Event. 

    The icon rocked a baby blue, double-breasted suit jacket with 80s-inspired epaulettes and a cherub in chains corsage attached to his lapel. The fashion-forward look was completed with a tiered white skirt featuring asymmetrical layers of frothy tulle.



    Dua Lipa

    Levelling up her leather game, 'Houdini' hitmaker Dua Lipa nailed the dadcore trend in an oversized, forest green trench and boxy knee-high boots. 

    Sobhita Dhulipala

    Indian actress Sobhita Dhulipala stole the spotlight on the red carpet for her new movie, , directed by and starring Slumdog Millionaire star, Dev Patel. 

    The raven-haired beauty looked sublime in a crystal-adorned gown as she paired her look with spellbinding diamond drop earrings. 

    Andrew Scott

    If anyone can pull off a lime-green suit with XL lapels, it's Andrew Scott. 

    The Irish actor cut a dapper figure in a suit by Giuliva Heritage at the Los Angeles premiere of Netflix's 'Ripley' at The Egyptian Theatre Hollywood on 3 April.

    Dakota Fanning

    Dakota Fanning was an etheral beauty in an asymmetrical organza gown by Fendi for the premiere of Netflix's 'Ripley'.

    Featuring rippling layers of organza fabric, the American actress cut a whimsical figure on the red carpet.

    SEE: Everything you need to know about 'The Talented Mr Ripley' Reboot starring Dakota Fanning

    Beyoncé

    We all know Beyoncé is in her country era, but she also appears to have entered her blonde era. 

    Joining a fleet of stars at the 2024 iHeartRadio Music Awards, the hitmaker took to the stage in a head-to-toe leather look, sporting a full head of platinum blonde curls.

    Meryl Streep

    Hollywood muse Meryl Streep looked utterly angelic in a billowing white Lanvin gown at the 2024 iHeartRadio Music Awards. With a plunging neckline, ethereal draped skirt and delicate crystal-adorned clasps down the front, the actress looked glorious in the monochromatic number. 

    Embodying an effortlessly sustainable style, Meryl actually recycled the dress for the occasion. She previously wore the pleated frock almost a decade ago at an event honouring Lanvin's creative director, Alber Elbaz, in 2015.

    Cher

    Cher, 77, solidified her icon status at the 2024 iHeartRadio Music Awards in edgy cargo pants and a bejewelled bodysuit adorned with oversized crystal cuffs. 

    The singer's waist-length raven hair was styled in beachy waves as she sported her signature fluttering eyelashes and pink lip beauty look.

    Aptly, Cher also picked up the 'Icon Award' on the night. 

    Katy Perry

    Katy Perry was a sheer delight in an all-over mesh dress and futuristic shades as she joined a host of stars at the iHeart Radio Music Awards.
