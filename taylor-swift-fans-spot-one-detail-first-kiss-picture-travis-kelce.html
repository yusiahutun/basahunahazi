Taylor Swift's fans spot surprising detail about her first kiss picture with Travis Kelce

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Swifties spotted a crucial detail as Taylor Swift was pictured kissing beau Travis Kelce on the cheek after a Kansas City Chiefs game.

Chariah Gordon, girlfriend of Chiefs’ wide receiver Mecole Hardman Jr, shared a photo with the 'Shake It Off' superstar and the two football players on Instagram following the game on Sunday.

In the photos, Taylor looked loved up in a red Chiefs jersey as she put one arm around Travis and the other around Chariah. Meanwhile, Travis looked over the moon to be pictured with his girlfriend, particularly as she gave him a kiss on the cheek.  

But fans spotted one key detail - something unimaginable: Taylor wasn’t wearing her signature red lipstick.

Earlier when photographed at the game, the star was clearly spotted with her usual bold red lip. Yet in these post-game photos, it wasn’t there - potentially smudged off in a cheeky smooch after the Chiefs’ victory over the Los Angeles Chargers.

“Awww and I know she feels comfortable with y'all cause the red lip came off,” one fan exclaimed. 

Another fan said: “Imagine getting kissed on the cheek by Taylor swift. Jealous of him.”

Taylor is rarely spotted without her iconic bold red lip - it’s been a signature throughout her entire career. But more recently while spending time with Travis, fans have been noticing her photographed with a more smudged look, taking it as an indication of how loved up she must be with her new boyfriend.

More fans were amazed by the sweet photo of the duo together. “Thanks for breaking the internet Chariah” one person commented. Plenty of other people seemed to think the photo broke the internet as well.
