Harry Styles steamiest penis confessions from best schlong to leaked nudes

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, February 19, 2024

He first shot to fame back in 2009 when he auditioned on X Factor and joined the boy band One Direction.

After the show, the group won the world over with their hit tracks like What Makes You Beautiful, Little Things and Live While We’re Young.

In 2015, the group decided to split up after five years of superstardom, months after Zayn Malik sensationally left the group. Since then, all of the band members have launched their own music careers, but none have been as successful as the leading man.

It appears the singer is quite proud of his impressive member when he declared he has the “best schlong ever.” Last year, the Watermelon Sugar hitmaker left a very telling snap on his Instagram Stories.

He could be seen posing next to a porta-loo in a shimmering turquoise and pink tracksuit next to a cheeky poster. Grinning for the camera with his hands under his chin, the singer showed off the sign that had been taped to the door of the cubicle.

It read: “Best schlong ever” - a dig at the One Direction hit track, Best Song Ever. It didn’t take long before his loyal legion of fans went wild immediately after he posted it.

One user giggled: “I feel like this joke had to start during One Direction” as another begged: “Prove it!” A third gushed: “He is so unhinged” as a fourth chuckled: “Please submit this for show and tell.”

Leaked nudes

It’s not just Harry who has supported this claim either, after his pal Ed Sheeran backed up the rumours of his rather large package in the past. In 2015, Ed was asked if he wrong the song Little Things for the boyband because of their literal ‘little things’.

But the singer songwriter was quick to shut that speculation down. He explained to New Zealand’s ZM Online: “Harry definitely hasn’t got a little thing, so no, I did not write ‘Little Things’ about little things.”

But the Lego House didn’t stop their with the eye-opening revelations, when he claimed that Harry accidentally leaked his own nudes online. He explained that Styles himself had been the leak of the personal image which was taken when he was 17.

He told the hosts: “Did you know Harry leaked that picture himself? He leaked his own picture! I think it’s amazing. He was just like, ‘Nobody knows’.”

Strict film rules

Since the embarrassing snap was leaked online, the hunk had avoided bearing his intimates to the general public any further. In his most recent film My Policeman, Harry insisted that there be a ‘No Penis’ clause written into his contract before he took on the role of Tom Burgess in the 2022 film.

During an interview on The Howard Stern Show, he recalled what it was like to act in the high-profile film. He told viewers: “It does feel vulnerable.

“I’d never kissed anyone on camera before and it felt like giving a part of myself away in some ways. I wasn’t naked in Don’t Worry Darling. I was naked in My Policeman.

“There’s no peen in the final cut. There’s bum bum... I don’t think the peen was intended to be involved. The peen, it was pre-negotiated that that would remain my own.”

When discussing filming sex scenes on set, Harry confessed that the most important thing for him is “trust”. He added: “If you remember that the most important thing on the set is the two human beings doing it.

“If at any point either one of you is uncomfortable, I think having the conversation where it’s like, ‘It doesn’t matter if they’re getting great stuff, if you don’t feel good, you tell me and we’ll stop’.”

Meanwhile, a source went on to say: “It’s incredible, he seems to have at least three or four girls in his room every night.”
