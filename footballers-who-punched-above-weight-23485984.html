Footballers who punched above their weight with WAGs - Peter Crouch to Luis Suarez

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Over the years, football has been full of teams and players who have punched above their weight on the pitch.

But plenty of top-flight stars have followed that mantra off the pitch too.

Yes, we're talking about when they score with a beautiful woman despite not necessarily being blessed in the looks department themselves.

Many, who it is fair to say are no oil paintings, have had serious relationships with stunning models, actresses or the 'girl next door.'

Here are some of the Premier League players who were as successful off the field as they have been on it.

The former England and Liverpool star is always good for a soundbite, none more so when asked what he would be if he wasn't a footballer.

"A virgin," was his quick-witted response.
