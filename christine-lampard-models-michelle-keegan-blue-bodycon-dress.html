Christine Lampard models Michelle Keegan's 36 bodycon dress and wow

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, February 2, 2024

The Lorraine and Loose Women presenter rocked a lovely little Very number

    Christine Lampard is a champion of high street fashion and she had us rushing to snap up her latest purchase on Bank Holiday Monday.

    The presenter, who is filling in for regular host Lorraine Kelly this week, donned a lovely bodycon number from Michelle Keegan's Very range. Looking gorgeous in electric blue, mother-of-two Christine modelled the chic workwear look backstage at ITV – and can you believe it's only £36?

    The button-down, ribbed number has a bodycon fit, a scoop neck, and a flattering mid-calf length cut. Best of all, it won't break the bank since it's currently on sale at £36, reduced from £45.

    Loose Women star Christine captioned the photo: "Back on @lorraine for the last week of Easter holidays see you tomorrow at 9am dress from @veryuk @michkeegan."

    Michelle herself had the best reaction to Christine wearing her 'Ruched Front Knitted Midi Dress' design, liking the snap and commenting: "Beautiful as always".

    Others showered Christine with compliments, with one writing: "Love you on Lorraine x," and another penning: "Stunning!! Love that dress & blue is definitely your colour". Emma Bunton commented: "Beautiful in blue." We definitely agree!

    £36 AT VERY

    Christine, who raises children Patricia and Freddie with her husband Frank Lampard, has really been killing it in the style stakes this week.

    On Tuesday, the Northern Irish beauty wowed in yet another figure-flattering dress, this time rocking a bubblegum pink number from L.K.Bennett.

    The bright shade looked seriously gorgeous with Christine's bronzed complexion and brunette locks.

    The statement 'Heidi Cotton-Blend Rib Knit Dress' boasts a V-neck with open collar detail and a below-the-knee fit, making it perfect for both work and drinks.

    £259 AT L.K.BENNETT

    This isn't the first time Christine has worn one of Michelle's dresses. In February, the presenter caused a stir when she emerged in Very’s 'Knitted Stripe Midi Dress'.
