Ryan Seacrest Girlfriend, Who's He Dating Now 2024? Aubrey Paige

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, May 12, 2024

Aubrey Page (2021 - 2024)

Ryan Seacrest’s last girlfriend is Aubrey Paige. They met on Memorial Day Weekend in the Hamptons New York in 2021.

“I’m happy. I’m happy in the present moment,” Seacrest said on an episode of Live with Kelly and Ryan. “I don’t think about anything else. Why push it?” He then added, “I think having kids at the right time would be great.”

On Seacrest’s last day on Live! With Kelly and Ryan, his girlfriend made a sweet post for his departure. “Ryan, never have I ever met a more inspiring man,” the model wrote on Instagram. “You make it look so easy, but the truth is there is just nobody quite like you. I am so beyond proud of you. Not just for your work ethic, but for your caring heart and the genuine love that you give to others.”

Paige continued: “I am so excited for this next chapter of your life and our life. May it be filled with total presence, joy and relaxation in every single moment. The best is yet to come. Cheers 🥂 Love ya babe.”

On Christmas 2023, fans noticed that Paige did not post a birthday tribute to her boyfriend, whose birthday is on December 24. She also posted a video featuring her family celebrating Christmas sans Seacrest. “Where’s ry ry?” one commenter posted.

On Valentine’s Day 2024, she finally made her debut on his Instagram account after three years of keeping their relationship private. “Happy #ValentinesDay @aubreypaige_ , can’t wait to eat that cookie dough with you but first, @disneyparks churros,” he captioned the post.

The two were confirmed to have broken up on April 24, 2024. “After three beautiful years together, Ryan and Aubrey have mutually decided to part ways,” a source close to the couple told People. “They plan to stay in each other’s lives as good friends and support one another’s endeavors.”

After news of the breakup was reported, many sources came forward about the reason behind the split. Sources revealed to Radar Online that Seacrest felt “pressure” to propose to Paige and start a family. Aubrey was “clear from day one that she’s not just interested in getting married. She wants a guarantee the deal will include having children.”They further claimed that “She wants kids, and she wants them with Ryan.” However his busy schedule averted him from the plan. “He’s always on the run, and can’t even imagine how he’d handle all his projects if he was kept awake by the cries of a newborn,” the source said.

A day after the news broke, Aubrey went on her Instagram to respond to those who questioned the relationship. “S/O to my haters, this one’s for you 💋,” the model wrote alongside an Instagram video of herself in a pool on Wednesday, April 25. “Happy #winewednesday.”
