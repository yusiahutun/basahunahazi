Brian Hallisay's Married Life With Wife &amp; Net Worth Details

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 17, 2024
EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: March 11, 2020Brian Hallisay is an American actor and former Wall Street professional known for his appearances in TV shows and movies such as Suits, Revenge, and The Client List...is married to Jennifer Love Hewitt...has two kids with her; daughter Autumn James Hallisay and son Atticus James Hallisay...has a net worth of around $2 million...

Brian Hallisay began his acting career in Hollywood in 2005. His debut role was in the TV show, Strong Medicine, in which he portrayed Dr. George Harmon for a single episode. Including his debut job, he now has 30 credits as an actor. 

Brian has worked in many popular TV series and movies like 9-1-1 (2018 to 2019), Suits (2019), Revenge (2014 to 2015), The Client List (2012 to 2013), and American Sniper (2014) to name a few. 

Brian’s Personal Life: Wife & Children

Brian is married to Jennifer Love-Hewitt. The couple first met on the set of NBC series Love Bites and started dating in early 2012. 

Then, after just more than a year of dating each other, the couple got engaged in late May 2013.

Their wedding followed shortly after, as Brian married Jennifer on the 20th of November 2013. Their marriage was a private affair and happened only five days before Jennifer gave birth to the couple’s first child. 

Their daughter, Autumn James Hallisay, was born on the 26th of November 2013. Then, about two years after Autumn’s birth, their second child, son Atticus James Hallisay was born on the 24th of June 2015. 

Brian Hallisay captured with his wife Jennifer Love Hewitt, their daughter Autumn James Hallisay, and son Atticus James Hallisay in 2015 (Photo: Daily Mail/KSJ)

The family now lives in California.

Details About Net Worth

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Brian has an estimated net worth of $2 million.

On a similar topic, his wife Jennifer has a net worth of $18 million, according to the same source.

Bio Of Brian Hallisay

Brian Hallisay was born on the 31st of October 1978 in Washington, District of Columbia, the United States.

He is the brother-in-law of Todd Hewitt.
