Paris Hilton's ex and former NFL quarterback Matt Leinart ties the knot with actress Josie Loren

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, April 21, 2024

He's dated Hollywood it-girls Paris Hilton, Kristin Cavallari, and Aly Michalka. 

But as of Saturday, former NFL quarterback Matt Leinart is officially off the market.

The 35-year-old athlete married actress Josie Loren, 31, in a beautiful black-tie ceremony in Asheville, North Carolina.

I do: On Saturday, former NFL quarterback Matt Leinart married actress Josie Loren, 31, during a beautiful black-tie ceremony in Asheville, North Carolina 

Matt and Josie said their vows in the Basilica of St. Lawrence before partying the night away at an impressive lodge overlooking the Blue Crest Mountains.

Josie's hair was pulled back chignon style and held in place with a bedazzled headband. 

Meanwhile, Matt wore a black tux and bow tie. 

The Make It Or Break It star arrived at the church in an old-fashioned car while the rest of the bridal party rode in on an old fashioned trolley.

There were several hundred guests at the reception including 98 Degrees singer Nick Lachey and wife Vanessa Lachey. 

Nick put on an electric performance of Stevie Wonder's hit song Superstition just after the couple cut their five-tier cake, according to Ashvegas.com. 

While the dance floor was hot, guests also participated in a wedding photo booth that was well supplied with silly props.
