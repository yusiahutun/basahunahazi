Marlon Wayans embraces Fifty Shades nudity: 'I'm naked in like eight of my films'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 23, 2024

But this latest incarnation of the dominant businessman may not have the same effect.

Marlon Wayans has spoofed erotic phenomenon Fifty Shades Of Grey in new comedy Fifty Shades Of Black, in which Christian is now a criminal, a total stalker and rubbish in bed.

In the film, Marlon plays the heartthrob kinkster, as well as briefly impersonating Channing Tatum in Magic Mike – but he doesn't reckon Jamie Dornan and Channing have anything to worry about.

Speaking to the Daily Star Online, the 43-year-old said: "They're way sexier than me. If I was a woman, I'd have sex with both of them before I'd have sex with my character.

"He was a stalker, and he's really bad in bed. I think it was like, 15 seconds of sex [in the film]? That's a record."

Fifty Shades Of Black takes all of the creepiest parts of Dornan's Grey – showing up unannounced at his girlfriend's work, watching her while she's sleeping – and exaggerates them to the point of ridiculousness, but left some of the blockbuster's original script in tact.

Wayans said: "Some things you don't want to bother too much. You use it as a set-up, take it to a weird place and go slightly more creepy – or a lot more creepy – than he was, go more stalker. He was so unpredictable and so weird in the movie that he was a fun guy to play.

"You didn't know what we was going to do next. Like pushing her face into a muffin? It was so crazy and so wrong, but it's funny because it looked like what he [Jamie Dornan] wanted to do in Fifty Shades Of Grey. He was a total stalker."

Naturally, the raunchy subject meant that Marlon was involved in some half-naked scenes – "Nudity, for me, I don't trip. I've been naked in, like, eight movies" – with co-star Kali Hawk.

But perhaps the most hilarious scene comes when Christian is taught how to have sex by his mother's friend – played by Florence Henderson, aka The Brady Bunch's Carol.

So how was it having sex with Carol Brady?

Marlon said of the 82-year-old legend: "I've been wanting to do that for years. I wrote that in there just because. I was gonna do it with Alice, but she passed away. But to do it with America's mom… it's so wrong, but so right.

"She was just down. She was like, 'what else can I say?' She improvised some stuff. The one about all black guys being good in bed and having rhythm? That was her."

Wayans is most famous for his parody work, particularly the Scary Movie franchise, but despite there being Fifty Shades sequels coming, he won't continue to make a mockery of popular films.

The Hollywood hunk told us: "I think I'm done with parody. I want to do other things. I want to flex my creative muscles in other areas.

"I want to do an action comedy, buddy comedies, a magical comedy like Liar Liar. I've done parody for 20 years, [dusting hands] I'm out. It was fun."

Fifty Shades Of Black hits cinemas on March 11.
