Nicki Minaj hits at Tyga in 'Pervert 17' shirt in Feeling Myself video

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, April 6, 2024

Nicki Minaj appears to have taken a dig at fellow rapper Tyga in her new music video Feeling Myself.

The 32-year-old wore a jersey printed with the words 'Pervert 17' - an apparent reference to the 24-year-old rapper's alleged romance with 17-year-old Kylie Jenner.

According to TMZ the dig was costly as the couture Givenchy jersey's price tag was in the neighbourhood of $1700.
