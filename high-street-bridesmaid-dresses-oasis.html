Oasis drops new bridesmaid dress range - and brides on a budget will love the price tag

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, October 7, 2024

Prices start from £85

    Choosing the perfect bridesmaid dress for your gals is undoubtedly one of the hardest tasks on a brides to-do-list. Finding a style to suit every member of your party is no mean feat – especially when your girl gang is made up of different shapes and sizes. Also, if you have a fair few girls in your clan – it can be a costly exercise too. However, Oasis has the answer to all your woes. The brand has just launched its second 'Bridesmaids Collection' and it's been given a fabulous update for AW18. There's plenty of different styles to suit everyone, from multi-way dresses, cold-shoulder cuts to super-sparkly embellished gowns, there is something for all tastes. With five different colourways that include the regular blush, soft grey, and navy, this season sees the launch of oxblood and slate blue – the perfect tones for an autumnal ceremony. Prices start from £85, with the most expensive frock coming in at £150, so a truly reasonably-priced collection.

    Wine is a brand new shade for Autumn 

    Maybe it's because we all had royal wedding fever, but we can't help but notice that there seems to be a lot of choice for brides in 2018, from high street wedding dresses, one of the world's cheapest wedding gowns on QVC and the fact that brides have even been wearing jumpsuits to say their vows.

    £85, Oasis

    Amongst all this was the opening of The Wedding Gallery – the world's first ever wedding department store. Yes, you heard that right. The idea is so clever, we are surprised no one has thought of it before.

    A one-stop shop which occupies the basement area of One Marylebone in London, the gorgeous space showcases everything you could possibly need to plan a wedding, from proposal experts, honeymoon destination advisers to of course, wedding dresses galore. The groom doesn’t need to be left out either – there’s even a menswear section to boot.
