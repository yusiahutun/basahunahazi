Strictly sexy: Mollie King laid bare in plunging bra and tiny knickers

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 29, 2024

The Saturdays singer is currently raising temperatures on Strictly Come Dancing each Saturday night.

** MOLLIE KING STRICTLY: UNDERWEAR PICTURES **

Causing a stir both on and off the dance floor, Mollie King has been romantically linked to her professional dance partner AJ Pritchard, 22, but she was quick to shut down the rumours.

And now the starlet has stopped traffic once again, this time daring to bare in a sizzling photoshoot for Women's Health.

Having landed the cover spot on the December issue, the 30-year-old modelled three scantily-clad looks.

Opting to feel the breeze, the blonde bombshell flaunted her cleavage in a sinfully low-cut silver bralet that left very little to the mind's eye.

No doubt keen to display the results of her hardcore dance rehearsals, the bombshell displayed her washboard stomach.

Rocking a white pair of knickers, the Brit beauty let her toned legs to do the talking as she posed in front of a mirror display.

In another eye-catching shot, Mollie switched up her attire in an all-black underwear set, paired with a number of silver bangles.

With her trim figure taking pride of place, the small screen siren looked in her element as she ruffled up her golden locks.

Saving her most jaw-dropping look for the coveted front cover spot, the pop star stripped down to a plunging sports bra and tiny black knickers.

Serving major breast appeal, the body confident lass unveiled her abs to the masses as she flashed a winning smile at the camera.

Dishing the dirt on how she maintains her body confidence, Mollie told the title: "There are bits that I would like to… okay, pretty much every part of my body I'd like to improve...

"But I do think you get to a point where you have to accept yourself and say, 'This is my figure, and I could work on it, but I'm never going to drastically change it.'"

This isn't the first time the starlet has turned heads with her flesh-flashing antics.

Earlier this week, the Ego singer poured her trim physique into a strapless, cleavage-hugging gown for the Pride Of Britain Awards.

Doing little to dispel the persistent romance rumours, King attended the event with her Strictly partner AJ.

The full interview appears in the December issue of Women’s Health, on sale Tuesday 7th November
