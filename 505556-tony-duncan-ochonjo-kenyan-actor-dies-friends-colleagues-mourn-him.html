Tony Duncan Ochonjo: Kenyan Actor Dies, Friends and Colleagues Mourn Him

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 20, 2024
  • Renowned actor Tony Duncan Ochonjo died on Thursday, May 11, while receiving treatment at Kiambu Level 5 Hospital
  • His close friend told TUKO.co.ke that Ochonjo succumbed to liver-related complications and kidney failure
  • It should be noted that the comedian featured in several local TV programmes, including Ramogi TV's Nyarkochia and KTN's Mhweshimiwa series

The theatre industry has been thrown into mourning following the death of one of them, Tony Duncan Ochonjo.

Ochonjo breathed his last at 2 am on Thursday, May 11.

The actor had featured on several entertainment TV programmes like the Mweheshimiwa series on KTN among others.

The news of his death has left his colleagues and friends shattered.

Cause of Ochonjo's death

TUKO.co.ke reached out to his close friend, Peter Ouma, who confirmed that Ochonjo succumbed to liver-related complications and kidney failure.

He also revealed the fallen actor passed on while receiving treatment at Kiambu Level 5 Hospital.

"We will greatly miss Ochonjo. He succumbed to kidney and liver failures. He is body is still at Kiambu Level 5 Hospital but were planning to take him to Chiromo mortuary," Ouma said.

Sanda Dacha's message

One of his colleagues, Sandra Dacha, penned a heart-melting message on Facebook as she shared the news with her fans.

"A day like this last week is when we took you to the hospital. My colleague Dunco took his last breath at 2 am today. You embrace death because life doesn’t exist without you. You have left some wonderful memories that will never fade from our hearts. Until we meet again rest in peace Ochos," she wrote.
