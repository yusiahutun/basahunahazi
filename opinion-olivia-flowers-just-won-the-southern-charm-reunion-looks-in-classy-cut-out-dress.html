Opinion Olivia Flowers just won the Southern Charm reunion looks in classy cut-out dress Fr

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, February 16, 2024

Southern Charm’s reunion looks are finally here as the much-anticipated season finale looms. Fans have been scrambling to find out the Southern Charm finale date for weeks. But just hold on a second, as the cast’s glitzy and glamorous get-ups are catching our attention for a hot minute.

Olivia Flowers has just won the dress rankings, and that’s before the Southern Charm reunion has even aired. She wore red, suggestively to symbolize the drama that erupted on the past season. However, her show rival, Taylor Ann Green, who hooked up with Olivia’s ex, is also wearing the same color. Awkward!

Southern Charm reunion looks

The Southern Charm reunion looks feature Olivia Flowers and Taylor Ann Green – inspired by The Scarlet Letter, she told Bravo – in red gowns. Green’s red dress has a rosette detailing and a high leg slit.

Madison LeCroy told Bravo she channelled Hollywood icon Marilyn Monroe for her show-stopping look while Venita Aspen’s outfit was inspired by Angela Bassett in Waiting to Exhale.

Leva donned a long black gown with silver straps, giving 90s glam, while Craig Conover wore a grey suit. Austen Kroll went for red velvet blazer, and his male co-stars all donned suits in varying blazer colors.

Olivia Flowers wins with classy dress

Olivia picked red or the reunion, suggestively pointing to the drama caused by Green when she hooked up with Austen Kroll. She said “Sharon Stone at the 2013 Cannes Film Festival” was her style guide.

I believe she’s really gone and won the season, having maintained her classiness throughout the Bravo show season, but shows just the right amount of skin (her fun side) but keeping her legs covered.

Her dress is basically party in the back, business in the front, as it has an open back. She topped off the look with Steve Madden heels, Nicole Rose Jewelry accessories and opted for a loose ponytail hairstyle.

Season finale date is on its way

Southern Charm’s season finale airs on January 4, 2023, at 8pm. The season 9 two-part reunion will air later that month, but we’ll finally see the drama come to a close during the last episode.

The reunion took place on a gorgeous and swanky set with beautiful dark accents and furnishings, and the men even wore James Bond-esque suits to match the scene!
