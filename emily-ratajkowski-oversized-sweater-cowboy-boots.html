Emily Ratajkowski Styles Big Sweater with Cowboy Boots &amp; No Pants

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

These days, cowboy boots are as useful for making a fashion statement as they are for mucking about a ranch.

The proof is in Emily Ratajkowski's pudding. The model has been spotted on various occasions strutting New York City's sidewalks in the Western-themed footwear (her trusted pair is a now out-of-stock iteration from Zara, though a similar pair is linked below).

Cowboy Leather Heeled Ankle Boots

Cowboy Leather Heeled Ankle Boots

For instance, she expertly meshed together the aesthetics of the downtown It girl with the country cowgirl in November, when she styled boots with a boxy blazer and light-wash jeans. And just last month, Ratajkowski wore the footwear with another blazer—that time, a chic two-toned version—and leather pants.

Her latest cowboy boots-centric outfit is taking on spring. While out for a walk with baby Sylvester earlier this week, the My Body author opted for an oversized Blanca knit sweater in a sunny two-toned yellow palette. Eschewing pants, she let the boots do all the talking for the bottom half of her look. She accessorized lightly with cat-eye sunglasses, gold By Alona earrings, and a black shoulder bag.

TheImageDirect.com

EmRata previously opened up about her relationship to fashion in a 2021 interview.

"I feel like fashion can be a wonderful celebration of yourself," she said. "Choosing what you want to wear on a given day is such an empowering, exciting feeling. I love playing dress-up and I think we should all remember that fashion shouldn't be taken too seriously. It should be fun!"
