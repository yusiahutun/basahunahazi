Youth to the People Vitamin C Eye Cream Review: Worth the Hype?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

I’m incredibly picky about my eye creams. The skin around my eyes displays the wear and tear of life more than anywhere else, therefore I need a formula that performs above and beyond—just hydrating won’t cut it. Every morning I wake up with incredibly puffy eyes, and if I skip my much-needed 8+ hours of sleep for a couple days in a row, you can instantly tell. It’s not that dark circles are more prominent, I just look haggard.

TBH, I wasn’t expecting as much as I got with Youth to the People’s Peptides + C Energy Eye Concentrate, but after running through an entire bottle at lightning speed, I can confirm there’s more than meets the eye here (ba dum tss). In fact, I’d go as far as to say it might sit in my top two favorite eye creams of all time. Considering I test beauty products for a living, this is a pretty big statement.

The Peptides + C Energy Eye Concentrate brings an illuminating, almost shimmery appearance to the skin right upon application. This quick-fix sets the perfect canvas for concealer to glide over (I’ve never witnessed any pilling—ever). But the results from consistent, daily applications are really what matters; I’ve never seen such a youthful, healthy appearance across my eye area, and I can absolutely see a difference if skip applying it for several days.

Youth To The People Peptides + C Energy Eye Concentrate

I’m not the only one who swears by the eye cream‘s fast-acting and long-term results. As one reviewer wrote on the brand’s website, “This product definitely depuffs my eyes in the morning. I put it on first thing and by the time I get into my car to go to work, they’re visibly better. Not greasy at all.”

Another described their eyes as “vibrant” after using the product, writing “I’m a person who deals with dark circles no matter how much I sleep I get. I can really tell the difference when I use this product versus when I don’t. My eyes look less sunken in and I look like i have more energy.”

Join me in stocking up on more bottles while it’s down to $36 post-Cyber Monday with a free mystery gift.
