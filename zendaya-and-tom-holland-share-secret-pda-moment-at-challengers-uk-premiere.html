Zendaya and Tom Holland share 'secret' PDA moment at Challengers UK premiere

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, March 11, 2024

Zendaya is taking over Tom Holland’s hometown as she attended the Challengers UK premiere last night, and her supportive boyfriend was by her side. They shared a rare PDA moment ‘behind the scenes’ as they continue to squash split rumors.

Although Tom Holland didn’t walk down the red carpet with his girlfriend, he was still there in full force to support her, and of course, once again the Euphoria star slayed the premiere with another tennis-themed outfit. It seems like it’s Zendaya‘s world, and we’re just living in it.

Tom Holland supports Zendaya at Challengers Premiere – as she gushes about him on red carpet

The celebrity couple likes to keep their relationship more low-key than most, so when X, formerly known as Twitter, sees a PDA moment, you know they’re going to go wild.

The Spider-Man actor let Zendaya do her thing on the carpet, but the couple shared a sweet kiss and hug away from all the paps, in a video captured by one attendee.

“I love them more than anything, your honor,” wrote one fan as the video circulated the internet.

“My beloved parents!!!” joked another.

Although Tom wasn’t by her side while she took part in press interviews, Zendaya couldn’t help but gush over him while in his hometown. She said was “extremely excited” about him starring in the upcoming Romeo & Juliet play.

“I’m extremely excited, I can’t wait to watch,” the 27-year-old explained, before admitting she’d been practicing his lines with him.

Couple goals!

The star slayed premiere in her ‘tennis gear’ once again

It goes without saying, the Euphoria star stole the show once again with her tennis-themed look. She once again opted for a white dress, representing the Wimbledon white.

The Thom Browne dress was detailed with tennis racquets and coquette bows and featured a huge thigh split, showing off the star’s toned legs.

From the back, the piece had a low cut, showing off Zendaya’s dramatic braided ponytail, held up by a white bow. She finished off the look with a red lip, and honestly, we think this is our fave Challengers look yet.
