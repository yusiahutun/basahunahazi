Margot Robbie and Florence Pugh's adorable BAFTA Red Carpet interaction has fans going wild

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 30, 2024

The 2024 BAFTAs are underway as celebrities such as Margot Robbie and Florence Pugh are gracing the Red Carpet in London. The two had the cutest interaction, and fans are doing what they do best, fangirling.

Margot Robbie, who is nominated for Best Actress for her leading role in Barbie, stuck to the theme on the Red Carpet, wearing a figure-hugging silk pink and black velvet gown, as fans call her “evening Barbie.” But it wasn’t just her fashion choices getting attention. In fact, it was her adorable interaction with Lady Macbeth star, Florence Pugh.

Florence Pugh calls Margot Robbie ‘the coolest lady ever’

As the celebrities took to the Red Carpet to sign autographs and take selfies with fans, Florence Pugh surprisingly popped up behind Robbie, and one lucky fan caught the interaction on camera.

It looks like Florence may have been more starstruck than fans upon meeting Margot as she runs up behind her and shouts (what sounds like): “Oh my god, it’s the coolest lady ever!”

The Australian movie star then turned round with a smile exclaiming: “Awww, that’s lovely!”

Of course, fans went wild with the interactions as the video circulated on X, formerly known as Twitter, as one penned: “Margot Robbie and Florence Pugh in the same video, my day is just getting better and better!”

“I NEED A MOVIE WITH MARGOT ROBBIE AND FLORENCE PUGH HURRY UP,” another exclaimed.

A third fan screamed: “MARGOT ROBBIE AND FLORENCE PUGH I CAN NOW DIE IN PEACE!”

Both women stunned with their Red Carpet looks

Both women stunned with their blonde locks on the Red Carpet, as the Australian actress had her hair in a neat updo, to compliment her ‘evening Barbie’ outfit.

The 28-year-old also stunned with her short blonde hair, gelled back to create a ‘side quiff.’ It was her dress that caught all the attention though, showing off her curves in a plunging black velvet gown.

Of course, she added some extra spice, with a futuristic metallic corset. Although the gown was floor length, the actress was showing off some skin with slits in the back.

To finish off the look, she wore a matching ultra-long velvet shawl to give that extra oomph, and it did exactly that. Fashion goals!
