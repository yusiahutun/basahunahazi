Dorinda Medley hides her bare breast behind lettuce

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, September 19, 2024

She's one of New York's favorite Housewives.

And Dorinda Medley, 52, gave fans one more reason to love her on Saturday, after she posted a hilarious selfie that shows the starlet hiding her bare breasts behind large leaves of lettuce.

The RHONY regular added a silly caption to her scantily clad pic, writing 'I told you I never waste food!' along with the tags '#summerfun' and '#lettucesee.'

Au naturale! Dorinda Medley made a cheeky display on Instagram Saturday, posting a picture of her topless torso covered with only lettuce leaves

The Housewife was captured going au naturale while hiding her topless behind large lettuce leaves as she offered a silly wiggle for the camera.

While Dorinda ditched her top, the Bravo personality did don a Gucci bucket hat, dark sunglasses and a coral colored bikini bottom.

Last week's episode picked up in the middle of an explosive showdown between housewives Ramona Singer's and Bethaney Frankel.

During their spat, the Skinny Girl founder said Ramona was 'not a good friend' while the other Housewife accused the reality-entrepreneur of 'sleeping her way to the top.'

After her fight with Frankel fizzled out, Ramona returned to her accommodations where she ended destroying her guest room and causing thousands of dollars of damage. 

 Trashy: After her explosive fight with Bethenny, Singer returned to her guest room to trash the spot, reportedly causing thousands in damage

'When I walked into that room and saw what she did, I wanted to cry,' Medley explained after encountering a guest bedroom ravaged by the out-of-control Singer.

'It was not even about the paint work,' said Dorinda. 'It’s like, this is my home — I love my home. Enough has been taken from me, now you’re going to do this to my home?'

A source close to the production revealed to People that Singer was 'drinking like crazy' during the night, and decided to destroy her guest room after her earlier argument with Frankel.

Battle: The Skinny Girl founder told Ramona she hadn't been a 'good friend' which prompted the Housewife to accuse Bethenny of 'sleeping her way to the top'

A separate source explained that although Dorinda is still shocked by Ramona's behavior, she is trying to put the incident behind her. 

'Dorinda is still not happy with [Ramona] still, but they have to work together. They are still pleasant to each other, but you don’t forget after disrespecting a home like that.'
