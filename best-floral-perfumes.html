The 18 Best Floral Perfumes in 2024

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 4, 2024

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Courtesy of Marc Jacobs/Sephora

Expert Favorite

Lancôme Paris La Nuit Tresor

Expert Favorite

Lancôme Paris La Nuit Tresor

One of Grugeon's top picks is this gem from Lancôme. In addition to its base notes of patchouli, papyrus, and incense, the fragrance also blends together heart notes like orchid, strawberry, and of course, essence of the black rose. It's nicely rounded off with fruit-forward pear, bergamot, and tangerine. "This fragrance provides a new way to look at and experience the addictive smell of rose," she explains.

Best Floral Eau de Toilette

Dior Miss Dior Blooming Bouquet Eau de Toilette

Best Floral Eau de Toilette

Dior Miss Dior Blooming Bouquet Eau de Toilette

Credit: Ulta

Who needs a literal bouquet of flowers when they can apply Miss Dior Blooming Bouquet Eau de Toilette? Its combination of calabrian bergamot, damascus rose, peony, and white musks make it the perfect spring season fragrance.

Best Luxe Floral

Bvlgari Allegra Magnifying Rose Eau de Parfum

Best Luxe Floral

Bvlgari Allegra Magnifying Rose Eau de Parfum

Credit: Bloomingdales

The stylish design of Bvlgari's Allegra Magnifying Rose eau de parfum bottle alone is enough to want to spritz it on. Add in its blend of Damascena rose, plus, more intense Turkish rose, and any true floral lover will be sold.

Best White Flower Floral

Chanel Gardénia Eau de Parfum

Best White Flower Floral

Chanel Gardénia Eau de Parfum

Credit: Chanel

Anyone who loves floral fragrance but is over the scent of rose should try out Chanel's Gardénia eau de parfum. Its notes of jasmine and orange blossom make the fragrance subtly fresh and floral.

Best Fruity-Floral

Giorgio Armani My Way Eau de Parfum

Best Fruity-Floral

Giorgio Armani My Way Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora

Armani's My Way eau de parfum has a classic tuberose blend that also features gourmand and fruity notes of bourbon vanilla and pear.

Best Solar Floral

Viktor&Rolf Flowerbomb Tiger Lily Eau de Parfum

Best Solar Floral

Viktor&Rolf Flowerbomb Tiger Lily Eau de Parfum

Credit: Ulta Beauty

The latest iteration of Viktor&Rolf's beloved Flowerbomb eau de parfum can seamlessly transport you from spring to summer, thanks to top notes of coconut milk accord and bergamot, a middle note of tiger-lily accord, and mango at its base.

Best Spicy Floral

Kilian Paris Rose Oud Eau de Parfum

Best Spicy Floral

Kilian Paris Rose Oud Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora

Just when you think you're spritzing on a classic rose perfume, Rose Oud by Kilian Paris takes things up a notch. The scent gets a spicy kick from its cinnamon and cedarwood notes that's sure to set it apart from the rest of the floral fragrances in your collection.

Best Earthy Floral

Marc Jacobs Daisy Wild Eau de Parfum

Best Earthy Floral

Marc Jacobs Daisy Wild Eau de Parfum

While Grudgeon is especially partial to Marc Jacobs's original Daisy eau de parfum this fresher iteration combines banana blossom, jasmine, and vetiver for more of a wildflower bouquet. And just imagine how fun its stem-filled bottle will look sitting on your countertop.

Best Citrus-Floral

Ellis Brooklyn Florist Eau de Parfum

Best Citrus-Floral

Ellis Brooklyn Florist Eau de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom

Tuberose is the star note in this citrus floral perfume from Ellis Brooklyn. Hints of Italian bergamot, golden gardenia, and honeysuckle add a zesty twist to its classic floral composition.

Best for Date Night

Maison Margiela Replica On A Date Eau de Toilette

Best for Date Night

Maison Margiela Replica On A Date Eau de Toilette

Credit: Sephora

This scent's blend of black currant liquor accord, Isparta rose petal, and patchouli is intoxicating enough to spark a new romance—or rekindle an old flame.

Best Warm Floral

Valentino Donna Born In Roma Eau de Parfum

Best Warm Floral

Valentino Donna Born In Roma Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora

Blackcurrant, jasmine grandiflorum, and bourbon vanilla notes create Valentino's Donna Born In Roma warm floral scent, which isn't overly sweet. The blend is also inspired by a combination of Roman street style and edgy haute couture.

Most Versatile Floral

Molton Brown Flora Luminare Eau de Parfum

Most Versatile Floral

Molton Brown Flora Luminare Eau de Parfum

Credit: Neiman Marcus

Flora Luminare by Molton Brown is a true floral fragrance through-and-through, starting with its signature floral ylang-ylang note. Its composition also features heart notes of tiare absolute, jasmine absolute, and rose absolute.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Most Viral Floral

Gucci Flora Gorgeous Gardenia Eau de Parfum

Most Viral Floral

Gucci Flora Gorgeous Gardenia Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora

Notes of pear blossom accord play nicely with white gardenia in Gucci's best-selling Flora Gorgeous Gardenia eau de parfum. The popular scent is finished with a hint of brown sugar that lingers on the skin for hours.

Best Clean Floral

Henry Rose Flora Carnivora Eau de Parfum

Best Clean Floral

Henry Rose Flora Carnivora Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora

This fresh floral perfume blends earthy vetiver with fragrant jasmine, tuberose, and orange blossom for a versatile scent that isn't relegated to just the spring months. It also happens to be extremely layerable, as an added bonus.

Best Soft Floral

Creed Spring Flower Fragrance

Best Soft Floral

Creed Spring Flower Fragrance

Credit: Nordstrom

It's difficult not to picture yourself running through an English garden after a few spritzes of this soft floral scent. It combines top notes of bergamot and grapefruit with jasmine and white flower bouquet before drying down with base notes of ambergris and musk.

Best for Summer

D.S. & Durga Rose Atlantic

Best for Summer

D.S. & Durga Rose Atlantic

Credit: DS & DURGA

Described as "an eau de parfum built on notes of summer by the ocean in New England," this fresh floral perfume starts with notes of salt water and white moss before drying down to a rose accord with top notes of lemon oil and bergamot.

Best Musky Floral

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Amyris Femme Eau de Parfum

Best Musky Floral

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Amyris Femme Eau de Parfum

Credit: Bergdorf Goodman

Maison Francis Kurkdjian's Amyris Femme eau de parfum combines the scent of Jamaican Amyris tree with rare iris from Florence, Italy. The fruity-floral fragrance has distinctive notes of lemon blossom, pear accord, and sweet pea to round it out.

Best Vegan Floral

Liis In This World

Best Vegan Floral

Liis In This World

For a woodier take on floral fragrances, try Liis In This World. The scent combines lavender, fig, and violet with warm tonka wood and sage for a fresh and balanced aroma.
