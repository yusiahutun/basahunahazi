Napoli-supporting model wears only body paint to game as she fires money into the air

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 9, 2024

Fans of Napoli must have felt like Christmas had come early after winning their first Serie A title in more than 30 years - and Paola Saulino marked the occasion by donning her birthday suit.

Or at least the Italian actress was close enough, considering a lick of paint was all that stood between her and complete exposure as a historic campaign came to a close on Sunday. The actress and OnlyFans model was at the Stadio Diego Armando Maradona as Luciano Spalletti's side concluded their season with a 2-0 home win over relegated Sampdoria.

And as if the football wasn't enough of a spectacle on the field, Saulino ensured fans were kept entertained with another eye-bulging display in the stands.

Spectators could be seen snapping photos of the daring display, with Saulino dipped from head to toe in the club's iconic baby blues, with Napoli's badge and Italy's tricolore (the red, white and green streaks) also front and centre.

The masterpiece also featured several female silhouettes painted on in black, not that the risque factor wasn't high enough already. And just to top off the cheeky performance, Saulino topped it off by adding her name to the back with '69' as her shirt number.

And the frolicking festivities continue outside the stadium - named after club legend Maradona, who spearheaded Napoli's last title charge in 1990. There, Saulino strutted her stuff with confidence and fired fake money bills into the air using two prop guns.

Supporters in Naples will be hoping club owner Aurelio De Laurentiis is quite so liberal with his cash in the summer transfer window. However, that may be a prayer too far considering Napoli's usual spending habits don't allow them to compete with Europe's established elite.

Despite ending a 33-year search for a Serie A title, there's also a sense of uncertainty at the club following rumours Spalletti is set to move on this summer. There's also the matter of increased interest in the team's top stars, with star striker Victor Osimhen believed to be attracting attention from Manchester United.

After promising a naked bus tour to celebrate Napoli's title win earlier this season, Saulino provided the next best thing with an almost-nude demonstration on home soil. Missing out on silverware is never easy, but even fans of Juventus, AC Milan and Inter may have a soft spot for Napoli if Saulino pledges to top Sunday's show in future.
