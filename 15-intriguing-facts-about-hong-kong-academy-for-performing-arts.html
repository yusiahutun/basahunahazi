FAQs

1. What courses are offered at the Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts?

The Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts offers a wide range of courses in music, dance, theater, film, and television. These include undergraduate and postgraduate programs, as well as short-term and professional development courses.

2. How can I apply to the Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts?

To apply to the academy, you will need to complete an application form, attend an audition or interview, and provide supporting documents such as academic transcripts and recommendation letters. The specific requirements may vary depending on the program you are applying to.

3. Are scholarships available for students at the academy?

Yes, the Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts offers scholarships and financial aid options for eligible students. These scholarships are awarded based on merit and financial need, and can help offset the cost of tuition fees and other expenses.

4. Can international students apply to the academy?

Yes, international students are welcome to apply to the Hong Kong Academy for Performing Arts. However, they may need to fulfill additional requirements, such as providing proof of English proficiency and obtaining the necessary visa for studying in Hong Kong.

5. What are the performance opportunities available for students?

The academy provides numerous performance opportunities for students to showcase their talents. These include regular student productions, collaborations with professional artists and organizations, as well as participation in national and international festivals and competitions.
