Everything We Know About Tom Segura's Wife

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Tom Segura’s wife is a famous title, but the woman who bears it currently is the one it was made for. Segura is a hilarious stand-up comedian who has become a force to be reckoned with in comedy. 

He has razor-sharp wit, a no-holds-barred approach, and an exotic sense of humor that keeps his audience captivated and ultimately gasping for breath between bouts of laughter. Tom Segura has no doubt carved out a niche for himself in comedy.

Unsurprisingly, he got a naturally hilarious woman when he chose to marry. Not only is she the definition of funny, but she also possesses an arsenal of other talents. All of which perfectly complement Segura’s. As a result, they have established themselves as a power couple in the comedy world.

Keep reading to discover interesting details about Tom Segura’s wife. 

Who Is Tom Segura’s Wife? 

Tom Segura’s wife is Christina Pazsitzky. She was born in Chilly Windsor, Ontario, Canada, on June 18, 1976. Her birth came a few years after her parents escaped Hungary in the mid-60s. She studied philosophy at the University of San Francisco and spent some time at the University of Oxford. 

Some sources claim that Pazsitzky once had a stint in law. It lasted the whole of two weeks. What became Pazsitzky’s permanent thing was making people crack up in laughter.

Ultimately, she heard the call of the stage, and there was no resistance from her. She abandoned being a scholar and took up the microphone, venturing into the comedy world with passion and determination. 

How Did Christina Pazsitzky Become Tom Segura’s Wife?

Pazsitzky and Segura’s relationship started with them being friends. That was mainly because Christina was still in a committed relationship the year they met. 

However, after that ended, Segura asked her out. It sounds like a cliche experience, but things got interesting because Pazsitzky turned him down! 

Fortunately, she changed her mind and decided to pursue him as he had done for her. She worked up the courage to ask him out, and the rest is what people call history. 

Fast forward to 2023, and the pair now have an adorable family, blessed with two children, who they dote on. The two are protective parents known to keep their kids out of the public eye.

Tom Segura’s Wife’s Career 

Christina Pazsitzky proudly bears the title of Tom Segura’s wife, but she is equally talented and almost as successful as him. She is best known for the unique and humorous pop culture commentary she provides on several Vh1 countdown shows, TV Guide Channel, SyFy Channel’s “Insane or Inspired,” Tru TV’s “World’s Dumbest” and E! Television’s “Wildest TV Moments.”

She has also appeared on TruTV’s “How to Be a Grown Up” and was a Round Table Regular and Writer on E!’s “Chelsea Lately” and TBS’ “Funniest Wins with Marlon Wayans.” 

Aside from comedy, Pazsitzky also has a background in animation. She has written on and lent her voice to several animated projects, including the feature film “TV: The Movie with the Guys” from MTV’s “Jackass” and the television series “Trolls.”

The talented comedienne won Animation Magazine’s “Up and Coming Writer” Award for her efforts. She also narrated the 2015 documentary “Can We Take a Joke?”

Pazsitzky’s stand-up can be experienced on the Showtime extraordinary “Red Light Comedy” filmed in Amsterdam, TV Guide Channel’s “Stand Up in Stilettos,” and Nuvo TV’s “Stand Up and Deliver.” 

The talented woman has also performed at the Just for Laughs Comedy Festival, Sketchfest, the Aspen Comedy Festival, Moontower Comedy Festival, South African Comedy Festival, Lucy Fest, and Gilda’s Fest. 

Her talents have allowed her to perform in comedy clubs all over the U.S. and Canada, and it has also helped her perform before troops in Afghanistan, South Korea, UAE, and Africa.

What Is the Secret to Christina Pazsitzky and Tom Sugura’s Marriage?

Pazsitzky and Segura have always been open about their relationship. They often share intimate details of their sex life with the audience of their podcast, “Your Mom’s House.”

During the “Just For Laughs Festival” in 2011, Segura revealed the key to their successful union. He said, “Intimidation and fear. My wife is Kim Jong-Il, and I am the people of North Korea.” Whatever they’re doing is undoubtedly working because they have been married since November 8, 2008. 

Other celebrity power couples like them in Hollywood include Melissa McCarthy and Ben Falcone, Leslie Mann and Julie Appatow, and Rita Wilson and Tom Hanks.
