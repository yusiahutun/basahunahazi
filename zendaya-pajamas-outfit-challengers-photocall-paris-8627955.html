Zendaya Wore Pajamas At Challengers Photocall In Paris

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, October 7, 2024

Zendaya scored yet another ace in the fashion department while continuing to promote her upcoming film Challengers. The Dune 2 star rarely misses when it comes to serving looks, and she somehow made pajamas look red carpet-ready during a photocall on Saturday, April 6. On the first night of the Paris leg of her press tour, Zendaya headed to a shoot at Hotel de Crillon in a set of yellow satin co-ords that effortlessly played into the pajamas-as-outerwear trend.

With the help of her longtime stylist Law Roach, the Euphoria actress turned heads in the silky matching set, which featured faux pockets and a luxe fabric in a trendy, almost yellow hue that’s perfect for spring. Zendaya styled her boxy top by only buttoning one button, leaving her midriff exposed and making the fit more relaxed. The star showed off the pajama-like cut and the mini pockets on the front of her matching slacks while posing for the cameras. She paired her bold outfit with strappy sky high silver platform heels, a matching frame handbag, and delicate gold hoops.

In keeping with her effortless Parisian outfit, Zendaya kept her accompanying beauty look sultry and classic. She showed off her newly-blonde locks in a voluminous blowout with a deep side part and shiny curls that hugged her face. The Disney alum kept her makeup simple with bold brows, a nude peach-hued lipstick, thick black eyeliner, and glowing, bronzed skin with subtle contour. 

Zendaya’s latest fashion slay promoting Challengers comes just hours after she chose a ‘60s-inspired look that was equal parts playful and mod for her daytime responsibilities in Paris. The actress wore vintage Louis Vuitton – a 2013 green-and-white checkered mini coat that just grazed her fingertips – and played into the era's beauty aesthetic with a Priscilla Presley-esque beehive updo accessorized with a white bow headband and classic white pumps.

Judging from this a pajama-inspired 'fit, it’s a safe bet that Zendaya will be continuing her sartorial winning streak over the coming days.
