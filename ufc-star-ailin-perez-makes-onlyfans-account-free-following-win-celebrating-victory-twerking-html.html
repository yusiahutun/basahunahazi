UFC starAilin Perez makes her OnlyFans account free to celebrate her win over Lucie Pudilova... af

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 6, 2024

UFC star Ailin Perez celebrated her win at UFC Vegas 82 by making her OnlyFans account free to subscribe - having made headlines for twerking after her victory over Lucie Pudilova. 

Perez was dominant over her rival in their bantamweight clash and won via a unanimous decision, and celebrated in some fashion in scenes that appeared to make her son watching in the crowd grow a little uncomfortable. 

After being given the decision, the 29-year-old turned around while holding an Argentina flag and began twerking because gesturing and talking to the camera. 


Perez, who fights out of Miami, Florida, has a record of nine wins and two losses in the UFC - but is also known for having a huge following on social media. 

She has 221,000 subscribers on Instagram and 1,400 on YouTube - in addition to a community of followers on OnlyFans.

And in the wake of her latest victory she decided to make the platform free to show fans her page was 'art, not pornography'.

Internet stars can make huge amount of money on OnlyFans by charging subscribers anywhere between £10 and £20 a month - plus extras for additional content. 

After her win over Pudilva, she said: 'For anyone who wants to check out my OnlyFans, at this moment, it's free.

'My OnlyFans is not pornography, it's art. All my followers could prove that by checking it out.'

Perez now wants to fight fellow South American star Norma Dumont next - in a fight that is being dubbed the 'Battle of the A**es'

'What bothered me a little bit is that she keeps trying to call me out to fight but then she doesn't want to fight and keeps trying to run away and claiming that I'm doing that with her.

'Norma, you don't just owe this fight to me but to the fans of South America because a lot of people want to see this fight, and I want to show the people that this is going to be a battle of a**es.'
