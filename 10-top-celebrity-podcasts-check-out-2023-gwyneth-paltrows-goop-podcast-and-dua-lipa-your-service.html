10 top celebrity podcasts to check out in 2023: from Gwyneth Paltrows The Goop Podcast and Dua Li

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, October 8, 2024

It’s pretty common for big shots like Markle to hop on the podcast train. With their famous names, high profiles, Hollywood connections and already huge fan followings, it’s a no-brainer for celebrities to give this creative venture a shot.

Guess which 3 tech billionaires saw the biggest wealth jump in 2023 …

So, which other famous faces host podcasts that you can listen to in 2023?

1. SmartLess – Will Arnett, Jason Bateman and Sean Hayes

Hosted by three comedians – Will Arnett, Jason Bateman and Sean Hayes – SmartLess invites listeners to learn, laugh and uncover the lesser-known facts about our favourite celebs.

The premise for the show is that, every Monday, one of the hosts reveals his mystery guest to the other two. Some of their recent interviewees have included Matthew McConaughey, Matt Damon, Jessica Chastain, Tom Holland and Idris Elba. According to its Instagram bio, SmartLess is currently the No 1 comedy podcast in the world.

2. I Weigh with Jameela Jamil

I Weigh originated from a social media post, which evolved into a mental health movement, and is now a podcast. Hosted by British actress Jameela Jamil (The Good Place), the show covers mental health topics and aims to confront societal norms by bringing up difficult topics and experiences.

Jamil chats with a variety of interviewees, from cognitive neuroscientists like Dr. Caroline Leaf to climate activist Greta Thunberg.

3. The Big Podcast with Shaq

Shaquille O’Neal may be known for his incredible career during his basketball days, but the NBA analyst has also worked up an impressive resume in the entertainment world.

One of his main ventures now is his podcast. The Big Podcast with Shaq releases weekly episodes, joined by co-hosts Nischelle Turner and Spice Adams, covering topics from pop culture to music.

Meet Christie Brinkley’s lookalike model daughter, Sailor Brinkley Cook

4. The Goop Podcast – Gwyneth Paltrow

Releasing new episodes every Tuesday, Paltrow and author Cleo Wade interview celebs like Katy Perry and Kim Kardashian, but also cover deeper topics in health and well-being, with titles like “What Do Healthy Relationship Dynamics Look Like?” and “What We Get Wrong About Manifestation”.

5. Sibling Revelry with Kate Hudson and Oliver Hudson

Hosted by brother-sister duo Kate and Oliver Hudson, Sibling Revelry invites fans to discover the family dynamics of one of America’s most famous families.

The laid-back, conversational appeal of the podcast makes it a hit among their huge fan bases, while guests like Seth and Josh Meyers, psychologist Edith Eger, and Stella and Mary McCartney, make every episode as intriguing as its previous one.

6. Podcrushed – Penn Badgley

Renowned for his long-standing stints with Gossip Girl and Netflix’s You series, Penn Badgley has added podcasting to his growing list of talents. Podcrushed, hosted alongside Nava Kavelin and Sophie Ansari, invites celebs on air to discuss their most embarrassing and relatable love stories growing up.

Does Shakira age? How the MTV VMAs winner looks so youthful at 46

7. Armchair Expert with Dax Shepard

Dax Shepard started Armchair Expert podcast out of his love of talking to people, and 300 episodes later, the American actor is still going strong. The goal of the production is, he says, to “discover human ‘truths’ without any laboratory work, clinical trials or data collection”. His methodology? Interviews with CEOs, talk show hosts, singers and more.

8. The Michelle Obama Podcast

It’s not too surprising then to hear that she runs her own podcast, where she candidly speaks to friends, family and colleagues about their top tips to get through life’s biggest challenges. Some of her top interviewees include Oprah Winfrey, Gayle King, Tyler Perry and Conan O’Brien.

Succession IRL? Meet Rupert Murdoch’s 6 kids, from Lachlan to Chloe and Grace

9. Dua Lipa: At Your Service

Hosted by pop icon Dua Lipa, At Your Service shines the spotlight on some of the world’s biggest musicians and influencers.

Run in conjunction with the BBC, Lipa is at no shortage of A-listers at her fingertips, chatting with guests like Blackpink’s Jennie, Elton John, Amal Clooney, Megan Thee Stallion, Billie Eilish and more.

10. High Low with EmRata

The model-actress-influencer now runs her own podcast, High Low with Emrata, where she gets up close and personal with stars like Nelly Furtado, Troye Sivan, Elliot Page, Anitta and Kelly Rowland. The Gone Girl cast member also covers pop culture topics like the Gwyneth Paltrow ski trial and “Swiftology”, a course about the business savviness of Taylor Swift.
