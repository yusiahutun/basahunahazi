    How to Apply e.l.f mascara

    Applying e.l.f mascara properly can help you achieve the desired lash look while minimizing clumps and smudges. Here's a detailed guide:

    Before You Begin:

    Start with clean, dry lashes. If you're using an eyelash curler, do so before applying mascara.

    Have a clean spoolie brush on hand to comb through lashes and separate any clumps.

    Application Steps:

    Prep Your Mascara:

    Open your e.l.f mascara tube and gently remove the wand. Make sure not to pump it in and out, as this can introduce air and dry out the product.

    Check the Wand:

    Examine the mascara wand for any excess product or clumps. Wipe off any excess on the edge of the tube to prevent overloading your lashes.

    Start with the Base:

    Hold the mascara wand horizontally and place it at the base of your upper lashes. Wiggle the wand slightly to deposit mascara at the roots. This adds volume and helps lift the lashes.

    Wiggle and Sweep:

    Keeping the wand close to your lashes, gently wiggle it back and forth as you sweep it upwards. This helps distribute the product evenly and prevents clumping.

    Repeat the Process:

    Continue wiggling and sweeping the mascara wand through your lashes, working your way from the base to the tips. Apply additional coats if you desire more volume and length.

    Outer and Inner Corners:

    Pay special attention to the outer and inner corners of your lashes, as they can be harder to reach. Tilt the wand vertically to coat these areas without smudging.

    Bottom Lashes:

    For your bottom lashes, hold the wand vertically and gently apply the mascara. Be careful not to overload them, as they are naturally shorter and can clump easily.

    Comb Through Lashes:

    Immediately after applying mascara, use a clean spoolie brush to comb through your lashes. This helps separate any clumps and gives a more natural finish.

    Fix Mistakes:

    If you accidentally smudge mascara onto your skin, wait for it to dry and then gently remove it with a clean cotton swab or makeup remover.

    Allow to Dry:

    Let your lashes dry completely before blinking or applying any other makeup. This helps prevent smudging and keeps your lashes looking well-defined.

    Remember that practice makes perfect, and finding the right technique for your lashes might take a bit of experimentation. e.l.f mascaras are generally user-friendly and forgiving, so don't hesitate to adjust the steps to your specific preferences and lash needs.
