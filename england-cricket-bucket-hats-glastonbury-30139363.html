England cricket stars look like theyre at Glastonbury as fans love new bucket hats

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

Fans were left in awe as England’s cricket stars emerged wearing snazzy new bucket hats.

England got underway with the second day of their Test match against Ireland on Friday. It was more of the same for Brendon McCullum’s side as England dominated early proceedings, thanks to Ollie Pope and Ben Duckett blitzing their way to 216 runs from 226 balls.

But England fans were as delighted with the players’ new choice of clobber as their scintillating form.

Stuart Broad conducted his pre-play interview with Sky Sports wearing a striking bucket hat in the same colour as England’s turquoise pre-match gear.

Captain Ben Stokes was likewise snapped wearing the bucket hat, while TV footage captured several more England players wearing them on the balcony.

Are you a fan of the England Cricket Team bucket hats? Let us know in the comments section below

Swathes of fans gushed over the more fashionable choice in comparison to the traditional hats worn in test cricket. And they all took to Twitter to share their adoration for their trendy and laidback heroes.

Some were quick to point out the resemblance with the choice of clothing often seen at festivals such as Glastonbury up and down the country.

One Twitter user wrote: “England attempting the 90s slacker/ ‘Alex from Glasto’ look because they're just so bloody laid back.” while another tweeted: “Bucket hats and casual sunglasses give proper festival vibes.”

A third wrote: “I honestly don’t think I could be more enamoured with a sports team.”

A fourth: “The ECB didn't need to invest millions into the hundred to engage with the younger generation - they just needed to sell bucket hats.”

While others chimed in with: “It’s not a want it’s a need,” and “Pure vibes.”
