Giant dog's Wayne's World costume wins Halloween

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, April 19, 2024

It's a Halloween costume that can only be summed up in one word: Excellent! 

On Tuesday night, a picture of former Miss Delaware Kate Banaszak with her dog Kellan dressed as Wayne and Garth from Wayne's World was posted to Reddit. 

Kellan, an enormous Irish wolf hound, wears Garth's classic thick-rimmed black glasses, Aerosmith shirt, denim jacket and, of course, rocks wild blonde hair. 

Miss Delaware Kate Banaszak (left) posed for a picture with her dog Kellan (right) dressed as Wayne and Garth from Wayne's World in an epic 'couples' Halloween costume

The dog is the spitting image of Dana Carvey's character, while Banaszak wears all the essential gear to pull off Mike Myers' Wayne. 

Those who have seen the post, originally shared by user enelprinceofthemoon, have made it known that this is one of the best 'couples costumes' they've ever seen. 

They've also made it known that 'they're not worthy'.

'That dog is more Garth than the actual Garth Dog,' one user wrote. 

'That dog is more Garth than the actual Garth Dog,' users wrote on Reddit (Mike Meyers, left, and Dana Carvey, right)

Another said: 'Still not 100 per cent sure this isn't actually Dana Carvey....'

Many other couldn't get over the size of Kellan, who appears to dwarf Banaszak in the photo.  

'Please tell me that dog is photoshopped... IT CANT BE THAT BIG?!?' one person posted.
