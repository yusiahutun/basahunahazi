She is known for her demure style, but Lauren Phillips spiced things up as she attended the launch of 2014 Australian Grand Prix on Wednesday night.

The television presenter was dressed to impress in a racy red scarlet jacket featuring a plunging neckline that showed off her cleavage in a lacy black bra.

Teamed with skintight black jeans and sky-high black boots, the model really made herself known at the event.

Commanding attention: Lauren Phillips attended the official launch of the 2014 Australian Grand Prix in a daring ensemble on Wednesday evening

Phillips looked sleek with her hair pulled back in a high ponytail and kept her makeup relatively subtle to show off her bright red lips which complimented her attire perfectly.

The model was also sporting a glowing tan, a nude manicure and some fine jewellery to finish off the look.

But the delicate black bra peeking from beneath her statement jacket was sure to have turned the most heads in her direction.

Phillips was seen posing with former Miss Universe Australia Scherri Lee-Biggs and Getaway presenter Catriona Rowntree.

Lee-Biggs showed off her trim figure in a slim fitting white knee length dress, while Rowntree sported a cute spotted frock and metallic sandals.

A former WAG, Phillips split with long term partner Gary Ablett Jr back in 2012 after a nine year relationship.

Her current boyfriend Lachlan Spark doesn't appear to have attended the Grand Prix event, so Phillips took it on solo.

The pair live together in the Myer Ambassador's hometown Melbourne after Spark made the big move mid-last year.

The television producer is formerly a beach-loving boy from Sydney but relocated to a city with a different pace to be with the TV host.
