FAQs

1. When does the Zundert Flower Parade take place?

The Zundert Flower Parade takes place on the first Sunday in September.

2. How long has the Zundert Flower Parade been running?

The Zundert Flower Parade has been running for over 80 years, with its first edition taking place in 1936.

3. How many floats are there in the Zundert Flower Parade?

Each year, the Zundert Flower Parade features around 20 extravagant floats, each designed and created by a different neighborhood in Zundert.

4. How many flowers are used in the Zundert Flower Parade?

It is estimated that around 500,000 dahlias are used to decorate the floats for the Zundert Flower Parade.

5. Can visitors participate in the Zundert Flower Parade?

No, participation in the Zundert Flower Parade is limited to the residents of Zundert and is organized by different neighborhoods within the town.

6. Are there any other activities during the Zundert Flower Parade?

Aside from the parade itself, there are various other events and activities that take place during the Zundert Flower Parade weekend, such as live music performances, food stalls, and art exhibitions.

7. Is there an admission fee for the Zundert Flower Parade?

Yes, there is an admission fee to enter the viewing areas along the parade route. The fees may vary, so it is best to check the official website for more information.

8. Are there any accommodations available near the Zundert Flower Parade?

Yes, there are several hotels, bed and breakfasts, and guesthouses in and around Zundert that offer accommodations for visitors during the Flower Parade weekend. It is advisable to book in advance as the demand can be high.
