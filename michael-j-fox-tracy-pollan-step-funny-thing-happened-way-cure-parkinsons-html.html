Michael J. Fox and Tracy Pollan step out at A Funny Thing Happened On The Way To Cure Parkinson's

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

Michael J. Fox, his wife Tracy Pollan and their kids stepped on the red carpet at A Funny Thing Happened On The Way To Cure Parkinson's in New York on Saturday.

The 61-year-old actor donned a purple and red button-down shirt under a black blazer. He added a pair of dark jeans to the ensemble.

He stood in a pair of black, red and white sneakers as he wrapped an arm around his loving wife, Tracy Pollan. 

Family affair: Michael J. Fox, his wife Tracy Pollan and their kids stepped on the red carpet at A Funny Thing Happened On The Way To Cure Parkinson's in New York on Saturday

The 62-year-old actress wore a tight black dress with a sparkling silver look to the ensemble. Her skirt was rustled, and she stood on matching high heels. 

She parted her blonde hair in the middle, her wavy tresses brushing her shoulders.

A Funny Thing Happened on the Way to Cure Parkinson's is an annual gala dedicated to raising money for the fight against Parkinson's Disease. It is put on by The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research. 

Parkinson's Disease, according to the Mayo Clinic, 'is a progressive disorder that affects the nervous system and the parts of the body controlled by the nerves.' Fox was diagnosed with the degenerative disease in the early 1990s.
