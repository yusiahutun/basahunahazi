    No matter how strong our resolve, sometimes we get frustrated and want to give up—whether that happens at work, training for a big race, or even a relationship. Those are the times when we most need some encouragement—that could be a pep talk or reading just the right inspirational quote or some handpicked quotes about success. If those little pep rallies don't help and you're still thinking about the Q word, you can also look to the Good Book for a little inspo. Check out these bible verses about strength and bible verses about patience. Still not sure if you can chug along on your current path? We got you! It's time to get channel your inner Rocky, get the eye of the tiger, and embrace these quotes about the importance of never giving up!
