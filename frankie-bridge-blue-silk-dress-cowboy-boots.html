Frankie Bridge amazes in the most flattering slinky silk dress

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, May 26, 2024

 The Loose Women star paired her dress with cowboy boots

    Whether she's opted for a casual look in her favourite jeans or is wearing a spring-perfect midi-dress, Frankie Bridge always looks incredible, and her latest shoot with HELLO! proves she can rock just about anything and look amazing.

    The 34-year-old sat down with HELLO! ahead of the launch of her new column, modelling a series of fashion-forward outfits, including a silky blue slip dress which is perfect for any spring occasion in your diary.

    Frankie paired the slinky, figure-skimming dress with on-trend cowboy boots, adding an extra fashionable edge to the outfit.

    Speaking of her always on-point fashion choices, Frankie said: "It sounds cheesy, but fashion is a way of expressing yourself. It's finding that happy medium of knowing what's in fashion but also sticking to what you know and what you like."

    As well as fashion, Frankie shared the other things in her life that make her happy. Find out what she said in the video below...

    Despite always looking out of this world, Frankie hasn't always had such chic style, with the star sharing throwback looks on her Instagram, with her young son Carter and her husband, Wayne Bridge, rating her most iconic looks.

    On a snap of Frankie wearing an ultra-skimpy dress during her Saturdays era, Carter said: "I don't like the skin showing," while Wayne joked about the revealing look: "I thought it was alright mate." 

    During her girl band days, Frankie and her bandmates Rochelle Humes and Mollie King were famed for their coloured tights and peplum dresses, with Frankie reflecting on their questionable outfits during her exclusive chat with HELLO!

    "I always laugh that any fashion mistake of mine is all over Google, whereas most people can hide theirs," she joked.
