We love receiving Photo Submissions from aspiring and accomplished photographers alike. But first, please review our Cliche Photo Submissions Guidelines.

Submissions:

Cliche Magazine accepts submissions from emerging photographers, designers, fashion brands, stylists, artists, musicians, bloggers, writers, filmmakers. If accepted, your work may be featured on our website, magazine or both. By submitting your work to us, you grant us permission to use your work in any of our brands, publications, websites, advertisements, marketing materials. You acknowledge that you own all copyrights or have permission to use the submitted content and Cliche is not responsible for any possible copyright infringement.

If we like your submission, we may feature it on our website or on our social media accounts. If we LOVE your submission AND it’s exclusive, we will feature it in our magazine.

Requirements:

All fashion editorials require 4-10 photos, with minimal repeats. Ideally, 6 different looks.
12 pages = 6 spreads, 2 horizontal shots, 8 vertical shots.
10 pages = 5 spreads, 2 horizontal shots, 6 vertical shots.
Beauty stories require 5 different photos, with 5 different makeup looks.

Please limit the use of H&M, Forever 21, and Urban Outfitters. Vintage pieces should only be used to “enhance” but should not dominate the entire shoot.

If your editorial or fashion video has been published in another publication, we will not accept it.

Please DO:
provide contact information

Please DO NOT:
send high resolution images. 2mb limit per image
no zip files (images attached or google drive)

For clothing credits:
please list complete stock list with image number and description with the designer name
