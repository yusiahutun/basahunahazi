Mark Zuckerberg shares sweet snaphot of baby Max's first swim

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 5, 2024

    Mark Zuckerberg is a very proud father. The Facebook CEO shared another adorable photograph of his two-month-old daughter Max on Sunday, capturing a special milestone in the little girl's life. "Max's first swim," the 31-year-old wrote alongside the image. "She loves it!"Max looks adorable in a blue and white striped swimming costume, with a red rose on the front, as she floats on her back, her daddy supporting her head. The photo was a big hit with Mark's fans, and has already racked up nearly two million likes, with 37,000 comments.

    Max's first swim. She loves it!

    Posted by Mark Zuckerberg on Sunday, 24 January 2016

    Mark Zuckerberg proudly shared a photo of daughter Max's first swim

    Mark has shared a number of sweet moments with Max ever since she was born in November 2015 – including a photo of him changing a nappy, one of him and wife Priscilla Chan reading to their daughter, and an adorable photo of Max dressed up in a Star Wars Jedi cloak, which he captioned: "The force is strong with this one. "Mark and Priscilla are clearly totally besotted with their little girl – so much so that they have pledged to give away 99 per cent of their $46billion Facebook stock to charity in her honour. Mark announced their plans in a touching open letter to their daughter, posted on 1 December.

    Mark and wife Priscilla welcomed their baby girl in November 2015

    "Your mother and I don't yet have the words to describe the hope you give us for the future," it read. "Your new life is full of promise and we hope you will be happy and healthy so you can explore it fully. You've already given us a reason to reflect on the world we hope you live in…"Max, we love you and feel a great responsibility to leave the world a better place for you and all children. We wish you a life filled with the same love, hope and joy you give us. We can't wait to see what you bring to this world."
